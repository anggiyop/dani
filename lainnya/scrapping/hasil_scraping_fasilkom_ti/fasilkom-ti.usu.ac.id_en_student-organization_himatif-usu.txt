About

HIMATIF USU is the student association of the Information Technology study program at the Universitas Sumatera Utara. This organization was founded on April 28, 2008 under the name Himatel, then changed its name to Himatif on September 17, 2011. Himatif is an internal organization of the University of North Sumatra, under the Student Government of the Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology, University of North Sumatra.
