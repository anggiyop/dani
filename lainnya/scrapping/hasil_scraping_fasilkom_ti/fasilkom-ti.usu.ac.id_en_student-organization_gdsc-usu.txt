Google Developer Student Club Chapter USU (GDSC USU)

About

Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) is the official community of Google Developer as a bridge between theory and practice for students who have an interest in developing their skills in the field of technology, especially Google Technologies. With GDSC's core values of Connect, Learn, and Grow, this community invites students to grow together through learning and expanding professional connections.
