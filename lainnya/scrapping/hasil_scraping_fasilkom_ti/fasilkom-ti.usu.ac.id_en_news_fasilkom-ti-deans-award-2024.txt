Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology (FasilkomTI) USU Hosts Dean's List Award 2024 to Recognize Outstanding Students

 

On June 19, 2024, the Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology (FasilkomTI) at Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) organized the Dean's List Award event. The purpose of this event was to honor and appreciate students who have achieved remarkable success at both national and international levels.

 

The event was graced by the presence of the Dean of FasilkomTI, Dr. Maya Silvi Lydia B.Sc., M.Sc., and Vice Dean I, Dr. Mohammad Andri Budiman S.T., M.Comp.Sc., M.E.M., marking a significant moment for students who have excelled in various prestigious competitions.
