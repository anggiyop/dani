Superior Quality, Qualified HR
The Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology of Universitas Sumatera Utara places the improvement of the quality of human resources as a top priority. In an effort to realize this commitment, FASILKOM-TI has successfully achieved accreditation, which is a recognition of the quality standards of education and services. This accreditation not only reflects the faculty's commitment to the provision of quality education but also demonstrates a focus on developing students' competencies and skills.
