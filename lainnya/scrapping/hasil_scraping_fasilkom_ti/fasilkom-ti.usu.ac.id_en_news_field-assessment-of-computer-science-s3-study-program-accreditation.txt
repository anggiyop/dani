Field Assessment of Computer Science S3 Study Program Accreditation
Published At

19 November 2022

Published By

Ahmad Fadhil S.Kom

Thumbnail Field Assessment of Computer Science S3 Study Program Accreditation
Fasilkom-TI USU held an accreditation assessment of S3 Computer Science by LAM INFOKOM on November 18-19, 2022, involving facility visits and interviews.

Medan, Humas Fasilkom-TI - The Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology (Fasilkom-TI) of Universitas Sumatera Utara  (USU) conducted a field assessment of the accreditation of the S3 Computer Science Study Program on November 18-19, 2022 at the Science Data Seminar Building and Artificial Intelligence Fasilkom-TI.
The accreditation field assessment was carried out by the Informatics and Computer Self-Accreditation Institute (LAM INFOKOM). The activity was opened by the Master Ceremony (MC), followed by remarks from Vice Rector IV Prof. Dr. Opim Salim Sitompul, M.Sc, Dean of Fasilkom-TI Dr. Maya Silvi Lydia, B.Sc., M.Sc, and the LAM INFOKOM assessor team, Agus Harjoko, Ph.D and Prof. Dr. Kusrini, M.Kom.
In her speech, Prof. Dr. Kusrini, M.Kom, said that the LAM INFOKOM assessment is based on the standards set by the faculty. "Unlike the BAN-PT assessment, after that we focus on what has been achieved and what will be done afterwards," she said. After that, the activity continued by visiting classrooms, lecturer rooms, laboratories, and infrastructure facilities, then discussing criteria A, B, C1-C9, D, and E.  
On the second day, the activity continued by interviewing lecturers, education staff, students, alumni, and graduate users. Furthermore, the submission of the draft minutes of the field assessment to the head of the Study Program Management Unit (UPPS) for review and ended with the signing of the minutes of the field assessment and the delivery of feedback and coaching suggestions.
