Learning Outcomes

Graduates of the Undergraduate Program in Computer Science are capable of compiling scientific descriptions, having the competence to develop problem-solving models for both individual and organizational objectives by implementing computer science algorithms, designing and implementing computation-based solutions that follow the most recent development approaches, and evaluating the efficiency and effectiveness of the methods in solving problems from various points of view.

Learning Outcomes
1	Capable of identifying, analyzing, and applying his skills to solve challenges in the field of computer science
2	Mastering theoretical concepts in computer science as well as theoretical concepts in special disciplines such as information retrieval systems, computer networks, robotics, and artificial intelligence.
3	Capable of making effective decisions based on information and data analysis in the field of computer science and also providing guidance in choosing  numerous alternative solutions both independently and in groups.
4	Capable of taking responsibility for their own work and being assigned responsibility for the accomplishment of organizational goals
5	Capable of creating new job prospects by developing and applying entrepreneurial models based on computer science and information technology.
