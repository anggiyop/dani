The Doctoral Program in Computer Science of Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) is the highest level of computer science education at USU. This program serves as an advanced education for graduates of the postgraduate program to deepen their scientific research skills independently to make significant contributions in the field of information technology.

With great pleasure, I am pleased to welcome you to the Doctoral Program in Computer Science at the Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology, Universitas Sumatera Utara.

In our pursuit of academic excellence and alignment with global education standards, the Doctoral Program in Computer Science at Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) actively participates in the university’s Internationalization Program. We are committed to fostering a research-driven environment that not only enhances theoretical understanding but also equips our graduates with the practical skills necessary to contribute effectively to the field. Our curriculum is continuously refined based on feedback from various stakeholders, including industry experts, alumni, and academic partners. This ensures that our program remains relevant and responsive to the dynamic landscape of technology and research. The Doctoral Program in Computer Science is dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of education that meet international benchmarks. We prioritize the development of our students’ research capabilities by encouraging collaboration with industry and academic institutions. Our partnerships provide valuable opportunities for real-world application of research and foster innovation. Additionally, we focus on enhancing the quality of our faculty by incorporating industry practitioners into our teaching and mentorship processes. Moreover, we promote a culture of integrity, transparency, and accountability in all administrative processes. Our program encourages active participation from students and stakeholders, facilitating a collaborative learning environment. We welcome input, suggestions, and concerns through various channels, including our online platform at wbs.usu.ac.id, reflecting our commitment to continuous improvement and effective communication. May God bless our efforts in advancing quality education and contributing to society. Ameen.

Prof. Drs. Poltak Sihombing, M.Kom., Ph.D
Head of Doctoral Program in Computer Science
