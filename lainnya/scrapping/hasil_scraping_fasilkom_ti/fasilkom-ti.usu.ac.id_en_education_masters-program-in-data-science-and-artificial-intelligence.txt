As an advanced study program after the Undergraduate Programs in Computer Science & Information Technology, the Master's Program in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence of Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) focuses on developing its students' capabilities in two specific areas, namely data science and artificial intelligence. Data science is a discipline that combines knowledge in a field with skills in programming, mathematics, and statistics, aiming to extract information or knowledge from data. Meanwhile, artificial intelligence focuses on solving problems related to human cognitive functions, such as machine learning, problem solving, and pattern recognition, which are then applied in technology.

With great pleasure, I am pleased to welcome you to the Master's Program in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at the Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology, Universitas Sumatera Utara.

In the realm of the Master’s Degree Program in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at the Universitas Sumatera Utara, a distinguished academic institution deeply dedicated to fostering inventive thinking and technology-driven solutions for a more intelligent and interconnected future, you are warmly welcomed. During this era of continual digital advancement, the significance of data science and artificial intelligence has been steadily increasing. Our program has been meticulously crafted to furnish students with the essential knowledge and expertise required to spearhead innovation and adaptation in alignment with the contemporary challenges. The meticulously structured curriculum encompasses comprehensive theoretical modules along with hands-on practical applications, empowering students to effectively address the authentic obstacles encountered in today’s global landscape. Within this educational framework, students are encouraged to delve into the intricacies of data, algorithmic advancements, machine learning, and the practical deployment of artificial intelligence across diverse business and technological scenarios. Our faculty consists of a cadre of distinguished educators who are authorities in their respective domains, actively engaged not only in academic instruction but also in cutting-edge research endeavours and interdisciplinary collaborations. Recognizing that innovation thrives in a collaborative environment, we foster interdisciplinary cooperation and have forged partnerships with a multitude of institutions and industries to ensure that our educational offerings remain pertinent and actionable in real-world settings. Our students are given the opportunity to undertake challenging projects, partake in research initiatives alongside faculty members and partake in global engagement through student exchange programs and academic conferences. We take immense pride in our multifaceted community, which brings forth fresh viewpoints and concepts to our campus, thereby enhancing the educational journey for all involved. Within the Master’s Degree Program in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, we are cultivating not only proficient data analysts and artificial intelligence specialists but also ethical and perceptive future leaders. We eagerly anticipate your involvement in this expedition to delve into uncharted realms of knowledge, collaboratively forging a path towards an inventive and sustainable future. With gratitude and felicitations on becoming a cherished member of the Universitas Sumatera Utara’s extended family.

Dr. Erna Budhiarti Nababan, M.IT
Head of Master's Program in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence
