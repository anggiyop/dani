Profile

Pauzi Ibrahim Nainggolan, born on September 14, 1988, in Kajang, Malaysia, completed his undergraduate education in 2012 at Bogor Agricultural University, followed by a Master of Science education at Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia. He is currently pursuing his Ph.D program at Universitas Sumatera Utara. Mr. Pauzi was called to join the 8th Taplai Lemhannas Ri in 2014.

In 2012, he became a Research Assistant at the Institute of Visual Informatics. Starting his career as a permanent lecturer at the University of North Sumatra from 2020, Mr. Pauzi was involved as an Expert for the Communication and Informatics Office of Medan City in 2021. Mr. Pauzi was once asked for an opinion as an IT expert in a land dispute case at the Sidikalang District Court.
