Learning Outcomes

The Masters Program in Informatics Engineering is a nationally and internationally recognized study program that excels in education, research, and social contribution in the field of informatics engineering, particularly in the fields of cyber security, machine learning, and the Internet of Things. This is accomplished through the advancement of science and technology in the production of innovative work to solve scientific, technological, and practical problems in society through measurable and scientifically tested research management and development, with the goal of gaining national and international recognition.

Learning Outcomes
1	Integrate original new ideas (improvements/innovations) for the advancement of informatics engineering/computer science through ethical, logical, critical, systematic, creative, and innovative thoughts, concepts, and scientific studies that can be published in reputable national or international journals.
2	Develop informatics engineering / computer science knowledge using a cross-disciplinary or multidisciplinary strategy to solving relevant and complicated social or industrial problems.
3	In research planning or project implementation, evaluate critically and professionally.
