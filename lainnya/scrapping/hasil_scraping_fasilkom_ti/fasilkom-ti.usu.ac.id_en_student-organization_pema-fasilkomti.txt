Pemerintahan Mahasiswa Fasilkom-TI USU (PEMA FASILKOM-TI)

About

An executive organization called Student Government of the Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology (PEMA Fasilkom-TI) USU was formed on December 21, 2012 and is based in the Fasilkom-TI USU Building, University Street No. 24 A USU Campus, Medan. PEMA Fasilkom-TI USU acts as a student representative at the faculty level, whose main function is to voice student aspirations to the faculty and university. PEMA is also responsible for organizing activities that support students' academic and non-academic development, such as seminars and workshops, and acts as a liaison between students and the faculty. In addition, PEMA is active in empowering students in various fields and advocating for academic and non-academic issues faced by students.
