The Dean`s Welcome Speech

At the Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology of Universitas Sumatera Utara, we have been providing high-quality, world-class education and research since 2012, as well as serving the requirements of our Nation for highly educated human resources, which is expected to own the ability to anticipate and adapt to the needs of Indonesia Emas 2045.

On behalf of the faculty and staff members, I am delighted to welcome you to the Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology (Fasilkom-TI) of Universitas Sumatera Utara.

Dr. Maya Silvi Lydia B.Sc., M.Sc.
