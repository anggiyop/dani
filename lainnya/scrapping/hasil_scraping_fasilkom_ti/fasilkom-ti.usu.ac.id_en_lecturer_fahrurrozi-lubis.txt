Profile

Fahrurrozi Lubis earned his Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Information Technology from Universiti Utara Malaysia, graduating in 2009 and 2012, respectively. He is keenly interested in network development and the Internet of Things (IoT), with research focused on applying technology to enhance connectivity.
