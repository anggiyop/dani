Outstanding Achievement of Fasilkom-TI USU at GEMASTIK 2024
Published At

26 September 2024

Published By

Ahmad Fadhil S.Kom

Thumbnail Outstanding Achievement of Fasilkom-TI USU at GEMASTIK 2024
Fasilkom-TI USU teams advanced to the GEMASTIK 2024 finals, showcasing innovative ideas and competitive skills in ICT, guided by experienced lecturers.

The Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology (Fasilkom-TI) at Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) has once again made a remarkable achievement at the national level. Several student teams have successfully advanced to the final round of the National Student Exhibition in Information and Communication Technology (GEMASTIK) 2024, which will be held at Universitas Negeri Semarang.

GEMASTIK is an annual competition organized by the Indonesian Talent Development Center, National Achievement Center, Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology. The main goal of this competition is to enhance students' competencies in Information and Communication Technology (ICT), enabling them to become agents of change in the development and utilization of ICT, both during their studies and after graduation.

The success of these teams demonstrates that Fasilkom-TI USU students possess competitive abilities and are ready to compete at the national level in the field of technology. With guidance from highly competent lecturers, they are able to develop innovative ideas that are relevant to the progress of ICT in Indonesia.

Congratulations to all Fasilkom-TI USU teams who have advanced to the finals. May this success inspire them to continue excelling and making meaningful contributions to the development of technology in Indonesia.
