Profile

Dr. Mohammad Andri Budiman was born in Medan on October 8, 1975. He holds a bachelor's degree in Engineering from ITB, a Master of Computer Science degree from University of New South Wales, Sydney, Australia and a Master of Engineering Management from University of Technology, Sydney, Australia. In 2021 he earned his doctoral degree from the Doctoral Program in Computer Science at Universitas Sumatera Utara.

From 2009–2017, Dr. Mohammad Andri Budiman was secretary of Master’s Program in Informatics Fasilkom-TI USU. During that time, he also appointed as Chair of the USU ICT Task Force (2012-2013), member of the USU senate (2013-2014), member of Team Formulator of USU's Strategic Plan and RPJM (2014), and Chair of Fasilkom-TI Advisory Council for the 2013-2017 period. From 2017 to 2021, he appointed as head of Fasilkom-TI Algorithm and Programming Laboratory, and currently Dr. Mohammad Andri Budiman is Vice Dean I for Academic, Student Affairs and Alumni, Fasilkom-TI.

Dr. Mohammad Andri Budiman is certified as a Java Programmer from Sun Microsystems, United States of America. He loved by his students, therefore for 2 consecutive years (2013-2014) he won the position of Favorite Lecturer in S-1 Computer Science Study Program. At the 2015 USU Anniversary, he was the second best presenter in the Lecturer Category. Not only that, his ability as a lecturer was also recognized by getting the Best Lecturer at the 2019 Fasilkom-TI Awards. Mr. Mohammad Andri Budiman also has a high IQ, so he can join the Member of IHIQS (The International High IQ Society) in 2016 and Member of Mensa International High IQ Society in 2017.
