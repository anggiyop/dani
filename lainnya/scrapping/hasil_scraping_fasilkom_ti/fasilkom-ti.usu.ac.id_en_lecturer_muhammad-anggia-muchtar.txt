Profile

Muhammad Anggia Muchtar completed his Bachelor's degree in Architecture at Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) in 2003. He then continued his education at Universiti Putra Malaysia, earning a Master's degree in Information Technology in 2006. Recently, he obtained his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Universitas Sumatera Utara, with research focused on Natural Language Processing and Multimedia.
