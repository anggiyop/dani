Thesis Examination Procedure

If the student has been declared eligible by the supervisor to take the final examination and has fulfilled the requirements for the final examination, then the student is entitled to take the thesis examination.
Students who have fulfilled the requirements for the thesis examination must fulfill the requirements in the administrative staff at the Vice Dean I to make an invitation for the thesis examination.
Students submit thesis examination invitations to supervisors, examiners and comparison lecturers as well as the administration and administration of the Study Program.
Students are required to be present 15 minutes before the exam takes place
After the exam is over, the supervisor fills out the Thesis Assessment Form and Determination of Final Thesis Examination Passing Status.
Thesis Assessment Form and Determination of Final Thesis Examination Passing Status that has been filled in by the supervisor, comparator and examiner is submitted to the administrative staff at the Vice Dean I.
