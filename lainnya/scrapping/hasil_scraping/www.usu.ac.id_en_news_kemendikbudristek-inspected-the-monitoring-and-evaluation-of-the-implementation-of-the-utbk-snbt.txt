Kemendikbudristek Inspected the Monitoring and Evaluation of the implementation of the UTBK-SNBT
 

Published by

Bambang Riyanto

Published at

Wednesday, 05 June 2024

Approximately 37,000 UTBK-SNBT participants took the exam at USU in 2024, necessitating careful and detailed monitoring of the test administration.

USU PR—USU Rector, Prof. Dr. Muryanto Amin S.Sos., M.Si., along with the Director of Learning and Student Affairs of the Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology (Kemendikbudristek RI), Prof. Dr. Ir. Sri Suning Kusumawardani, S.T., M.T., personally inspected the Monitoring and Evaluation of the implementation of the UTBK-SNBT Phase II in 2024. The inspection began at the Information System Center, continued to the Faculty of Engineering, and concluded at the Faculty of Medicine on Thursday (May 16).

Approximately 37,000 UTBK-SNBT participants took the exam at USU in 2024, necessitating careful and detailed monitoring of the test administration.

Significant preparations have been made to ensure the smooth running of the SNBT exam. USU Rector, Prof. Dr. Muryanto Amin S.Sos., M.Si., mentioned that measures have been taken to anticipate possible cheating incidents.

Drawing on previous experiences of suspected cheating, USU Rector stated that the issues were addressed properly through thorough interrogation by the committee to understand the chronology of the problem. However, only preliminary evidence was found before the exam; thus, no formal report was filed with the authorities.

Interviewee: Prof. Dr. Muryanto Amin, S.Sos, M.Si - Rektor USU
