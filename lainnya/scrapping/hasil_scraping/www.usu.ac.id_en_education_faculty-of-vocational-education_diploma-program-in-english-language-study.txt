Diploma Program
English Language Study
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Diploma Program in English at Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) is a vocational program exploring English science.

We offer four excellent programs: English for Specific Purposes (Journalism & Public Speaking), English-Indonesian & Indonesian-English Translation, Oral Translation, and Tour Guide Techniques. Our students are mentored by knowledgeable and dedicated lecturers. The English Diploma Program firmly commits to preparing its graduates to become intermediate experts ready to work professionally as interpreters, translators, tourism guides, and English teachers.
