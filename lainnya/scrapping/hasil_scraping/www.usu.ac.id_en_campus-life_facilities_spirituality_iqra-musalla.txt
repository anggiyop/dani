Iqra Musalla

The Iqra Musalla is located between the premises of the USU Library and the USU Faculty of Economics and Business (FEB USU). To access this mosque, the USU academic community and the surrounding community can use the entrance path beside the USU Faculty of Social Science and Political Science Postgraduate Building or from Jl. Abdul Hakim beside the USU Faculty of Law. This musalla also has a spacious size and is surrounded by a beautiful environment. Worshipers who come to this musalla can comfortably worship with the support of adequate facilities.
