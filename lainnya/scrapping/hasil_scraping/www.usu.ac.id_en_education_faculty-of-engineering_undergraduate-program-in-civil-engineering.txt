Undergraduate Program
Civil Engineering
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Undergraduate Program in Civil Engineering has been established since January 18, 1958 and is one of the oldest study programs on campus. The program explores principles related to the planning, design, construction, and maintenance of physical infrastructure, such as roads, bridges, buildings, dams, sanitation systems, and other structures that form the foundation of modern society. Students of this program will gain an in-depth understanding of mechanics, construction materials, structural engineering, environmental engineering, and project management.

This program has a vision to be the best in the field of civil engineering and globally competitive based on local excellence. We invite you to join the Universitas Sumatera Utara Undergraduate Program in Civil Engineering and embark on an in-depth journey in understanding modern infrastructure and sustainable architectural innovation. Graduates of this program will hold a Bachelor of Engineering (S.T.) degree, ready to play a role in advancing the field of civil engineering in the future.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
