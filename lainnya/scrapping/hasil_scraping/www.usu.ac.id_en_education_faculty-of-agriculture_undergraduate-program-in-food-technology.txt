Undergraduate Program In Food Technology
Undergraduate Program
Food Technology
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Undergraduate Program in Food Technology is a study program that focuses on processing, preserving, and providing food by applying scientific and technological principles. The goal is to create food products that are safe, high quality, and of high nutritional value. Food Technology covers various aspects, from product formulation, processing techniques, to innovation in creating new foods. With excellent accreditation (A), this study program emphasizes the importance of the food industry in meeting consumers' needs for delicious, safe, and nutritious food.

Supported by qualified and nationally certified teaching staff, the program has a strong commitment to produce skilled and responsible food technology graduates for the community and country. Graduates of the Undergraduate Program in Food Technology Program have broad career prospects, including in the field of research & development and as food quality experts. Upon completion of this study program, students will be awarded a Bachelor of Food Technology (S.T.P.) degree in recognition of their expertise and qualifications in the field of food technology.

Vision of Undergraduate Program in Food Technology

Becoming a leading Food Technology Study Program in the development of science, technology, and human resources to support national food sovereignty, which includes the fields of food crops and plantations in a global order in 2039.

Mission of Undergraduate Program in Food Technology

Education

Organizing education and teaching that can produce Bachelor of Food Technology with STAR characters and get international recognition.

Research

Conducting and developing innovative Food Technology research in the field of TALENTA at national and international scope.

Community Service

Providing expert services in food technology to support national food security in the food crop and plantation agriculture sectors, especially utilizing local food ingredients.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
