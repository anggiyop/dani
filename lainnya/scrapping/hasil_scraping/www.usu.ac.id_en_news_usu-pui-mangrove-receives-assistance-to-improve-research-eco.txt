USU PUI Mangrove Receives Assistance to Improve Research Ecosystem
 

Published by

Bambang Riyanto

Published at

Wednesday, 21 September 2022

HUMAS - After being established for more than 3 years, PUI Mangrove USU has succeeded in becoming the first Science and Technology Excellence Center (PUI) at the Universitas Sumatera Utara which was recognized by the Directorate of Research, Technology and Community Service, Ministry of Education, Culture, Research and Technology. This is stated in the Decree of the Director General of Higher Education, Research and Technology number 0753/E5/AL.04/2022 at 08/12/2022.

USU's PUI Mangrove succeeded in getting assistance to improve the PUI-PT research and innovation ecosystem for the 2022 budget year, and its activities will also be funded throughout 2022. The announcement of the acceptance of the Center for Excellence in Higher Education Science and Technology (PUI-PT), which was submitted as a result of the proposal selection, was warmly welcomed by the Chairperson of USU's PUI Mangrove, Prof. Mohammad Basyuni, S.Hut, M.Si, PhD.

Director General of Higher Education Research and Technology through Plt. Director of Research, Technology, and Community Service, Teuku Faisal Fathani, hopes that USU's PUI Mangrove will continue to perform as well as possible to be able to submit a proposal again in the coming year. (©ULC)
