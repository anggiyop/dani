About Technical Implementation Unit of Lecture Center & Basic and General Sciences Laboratory

Established in 1980, the Technical Implementation Unit of Lecture Center & Basic and General Sciences Laboratory (UPT PP LIDA) is a trusted and forward-looking academic support institute responsible for organizing and conducting the teaching and learning process for all USU students in the fields of general courses (MKU) and basic courses (MKD) as well as developing the materials and syllabus for both fields. UPT PP LIDA USU is committed to consistently improving the quality of programs and performance professionally to become a platform for knowledge development, providing better services to students, researchers, and society.
