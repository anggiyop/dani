Bakti BCA Scholarship
Bakti BCA Scholarship
General Criteria for Bakti BCA Scholarship 
Active students of state universities 
Bachelor Program Semester 3 
Not currently receiving other scholarships 
Maximum age 21 years and unmarried 
Have social awareness & commitment to learning Active in committee/organization (except for non-academic achievement categories)

 

Scholarship Categories & Specific Criteria

Financial Aid 

GPA min. 3.0, recommendation letter of financial condition from faculty 

Academic Achievement  

GPA min. 3.25, certificate of academic achievement 

Non-Academic Achievement 

Certificate of non-academic achievement such as sports, arts, culture, etc.

 

Benefits of BCA Bakti Scholarship

Receive Education Fund (monthly allowance + tuition support) and development program (future competencies) for 1 year
