DECREE OF THE RECTOR OF UNIVERSITAS SUMATERA UTARA

NUMBER 192/UN5.1.R/SK/KEU/2024

CONCERNING

 

CHANGE OF THE DECREE OF THE RECTOR OF UNIVERSITAS SUMATERA UTARA NUMBER 747/UN5.1.R/SK/KEU/2022

 

CONCERNING

 

DETERMINATION OF THE AMOUNT OF EDUCATION FEE PER SEMESTER FOR STUDENTS ACADEMIC YEAR ADMISSION 2015/2016 TO ACADEMIC YEAR 2021/2022 FOR BACHELOR'S PROGRAM (S1) INDEPENDENT PATH, INTERNATIONAL INDEPENDENT PATH AND EXTENSION IN UNIVERSITAS SUMATERA UTARA

 

RECTOR OF UNIVERSITAS SUMATERA UTARA

 

Considering:

a. that based on the provisions of Article 76 paragraph (3) of Law Number 12 of 2012 concerning Higher Education, universities or higher education organizers receive payments that students bear to finance their studies according to the ability of the student, the student's parents, or the party who finances it;

b. that in order to implement the policy of the Ministry of Education and Culture of the Republic of Indonesia concerning Single Tuition Fees at State Universities, it is necessary to adjust the amount of education fees per Semester for students at Universitas Sumatera Utara; and

c. that based on the considerations as referred to in letters a and b, it is necessary to stipulate the Decree of the Rector of Universitas Sumatera Utara concerning Amendments to the Decree of the Rector of Universitas Sumatera Utara Number 747/UN5.1.R/SK/KEU/2022 Concerning Determination of the Amount of Tuition Fees Per Semester for Students Accepted in the 2015/2016 Academic Year Up to the 2021/2022 Academic Year for Undergraduate (S1) Study Programs through Independent, Independent International and Extension Paths at Universitas Sumatera Utara

 

Remembering:

Law Number 20 of 2003 concerning the National Education System (State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia 2003 Number 78, Supplement to the State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia Number 4301); 

Law Number 12 of 2012 concerning Higher Education (State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia 2012 Number 158, Supplement to the State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia Number 5336); 

Government Regulation Number 4 of 2014 concerning the Implementation of Higher Education and Management of Higher Education Institutions (State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia 2014 Number 16, Supplement to the State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia Number 5500); Government Regulation Number 16 of 2014 concerning the Statutes of Universitas Sumatera Utara (State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia 2014 Number 42, Supplement to the State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia Number 5510); 

Government Regulation Number 26 of 2015 concerning the Form and Funding Mechanism of State Universities as Legal Entities as amended by Government Regulation Number 8 of 2020 concerning Amendments to Government Regulation Number 26 of 2015 concerning the Form and Funding Mechanism of State Universities as Legal Entities (State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia 2020 Number 28, Supplement to the State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia Number 6460); 

Regulation of the Minister of Education and Culture of the Republic of Indonesia Number 25 of 2020 concerning the Standards for Higher Education Operational Costs at State Universities within the Ministry of Education and Culture (State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia 2020 Number 642); 

Decree of the Minister of Education and Culture of the Republic of Indonesia Number 81/E/KPT/2020 concerning Single Tuition Fees at State Universities in the Form of Universities and Institutes; 

Regulation of the Board of Trustees Number 1 of 2022 concerning the Organization and Governance of Universitas Sumatera Utara as amended by Regulation of the Board of Trustees Number 2 of 2023 concerning Amendments to Regulation of the Board of Trustees Number 1 of 2022 concerning the Organization and Governance of Universitas Sumatera Utara; 

Regulation of the Rector of Universitas Sumatera Utara Number 12 of 2022 concerning Financial Management of Universitas Sumatera Utara; and 

Decree of the Rector of Universitas Sumatera Utara Number 747/UN5.1.R/SK/KEU/2022 concerning Determination of the Amount of Tuition Fees Per Semester for Students Accepted in the 2015/2016 Academic Year Up to the 2021/2022 Academic Year for Study Programs of the Bachelor's Degree Program (S1) Independent, Independent International and Extension Paths at Universitas Sumatera Utara.

 

DECIDES:

To stipulate:

 

DECISION OF THE RECTOR OF UNIVERSITAS SUMATERA UTARA ON THE AMENDMENT TO THE DECISION OF THE RECTOR OF UNIVERSITAS SUMATERA UTARA NUMBER 747/UN5.1.R/SK/KEU/2022 ON THE DETERMINATION OF THE AMOUNT OF EDUCATION FEE PER SEMESTER FOR STUDENTS ADMISSION IN THE ACADEMIC YEAR OF 2015/2016 UNTIL THE ACADEMIC YEAR OF 2021/2022 FOR THE BACHELOR'S PROGRAM (S1) INDEPENDENT PATH, INTERNATIONAL INDEPENDENT PATH AND EXTENSION IN UNIVERSITAS SUMATERA UTARA

 

FIRST: To amend the attachment to the Decree of the Rector of Universitas Sumatera Utara Number 747/UN5.1.R/SK/KEU/2022 Concerning Determination of Tuition Fees Per Semester for Students Admitted to Academic Year 2015/2016 Up to Academic Year 2021/2022 for Undergraduate Program (S1) Independent, International Independent and Extension Paths at Universitas Sumatera Utara;

 

SECOND: Changes to the attachments to the Decree of the Rector of Universitas Sumatera Utara as stated in the FIRST DICTUM are made in Attachment I, Educational Development Contribution (SPP) for the Doctoral Education and Entrepreneurship Study Programs, and in Attachment II, Educational Development Contribution (SPP) for the Doctoral Education, Accounting, Development Economics, Management and Dentistry Study Programs as stated in the attachments which are an integral part of this Chancellor's Decree;

 

THIRD: This Decree shall come into effect from the Even Semester of the 2023/2024 Academic Year.

 

Established in Medan

On January 16, 2024

RECTOR,

 

 

MURYANTO AMIN
