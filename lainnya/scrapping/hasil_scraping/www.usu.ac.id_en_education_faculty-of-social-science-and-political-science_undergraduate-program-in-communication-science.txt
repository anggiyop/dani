Undergraduate Program In Communication Science
Undergraduate Program
Communication Science
Universitas Sumatera Utara

Established in 1983, the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Undergraduate Program in Communication Science is a center of excellence for higher education in communication science that focuses on public speaking, literacy, media production, advertising, and other communication sciences.

With the assistance of the best individuals who are strongly determined as educators, communication science learning is designed to help students convey messages effectively by creating a pleasant learning atmosphere. Graduates of the Undergraduate Program in Communication Science have a wide range of job prospects, including media frontliners, event organizers, public speakers, and news presenters in journalism.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
