Master's Program In Communication Science
Master Program
Communication Science
Universitas Sumatera Utara

As an advanced level study from the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Undergraduate Program in Communication Science, the Master's Program in Communication Science consistently implements and develops quality communication science education.

With a focus on research and study of science on mass communication, interpersonal, group, intercultural communication, and media literacy, students can conduct research related to social issues. Qualified individuals in our study program can produce journals and scientific works that are accredited by DIKTI and indexed internationally with the help of highly dedicated and nationally certified teaching staff. The USU Master's Program in Communication Science is firmly committed to making its graduates excellent and globally competitive teachers and researchers in the field of communication as well as competent communication science experts as media practitioners and in political communication.

Vision of Master's Program in Communication Science

To become a center for communication science education that produces masters with competence and global competitiveness in the field of communication science in 2021

Mission of Master's Program in Communication Science

Education Development

Implementing and developing a quality communication science master program education.

Research

Conduct research and studies in communication science (mass communication & new media, interpersonal communication, group communication, intercultural, media literacy).

Public Service

Performing community service through training in communication science, public communication campaigns, and developing media literacy programs.

Scientific Information

Disseminate scientific information in communication through journals, scientific meetings, seminars, etc.

Organizational Capacity

Developing Study Program Organizational and Network Capacity.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
