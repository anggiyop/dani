Dr. Nurzainah Receives Hitachi Global Foundation Asia Innovation Award
 

Published by

Bambang Riyanto

Published at

Wednesday, 14 February 2024

The Hitachi Global Foundation Asia Innovation Award is an award program launched in 2020 to promote science, technology, and innovation, which contribute to solving social problems and realizing a sustainable society in the ASEAN region.

USU PR: Dr. Ir Nurzainah Ginting, M.Sc, a lecturer at the Faculty of Agriculture, Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU), made a brilliant achievement and won the Hitachi Global Foundation Asia Innovation Award 2023. Her research is entitled “Efforts to Find Alternative Antibacterial Solutions as Bio-Disinfectants for Livestock and the Environment”, which takes the big theme “Development of Livestock and Environmentally Friendly Sustainable Alternative Disinfectant (SDA).”

The Hitachi Global Foundation Asia Innovation Award is an award program launched in 2020 to promote science, technology, and innovation, which contribute to solving social problems and creating a sustainable society in the ASEAN region.

The program recognizes individuals and groups who serve the public interest through their outstanding achievements in research and development (R&D) in the field of science and technology to achieve the SDGs.

(R&D) in science and technology to achieve the SDGs. In 2023, the Hitachi Global Foundation Asia Innovation Award gathers research achievements from 27 universities and research institutes in 6 ASEAN countries (Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos, Myanmar, Philippines, and Vietnam). (RJ/Nur Sa'adah)
