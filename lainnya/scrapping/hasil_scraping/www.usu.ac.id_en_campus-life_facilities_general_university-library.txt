University Library

The Universitas Sumatera Utara Library is a paradise for book lovers and knowledge seekers. This library offers friendly and efficient services such as Membership Services, Circulation Services, Short Loan Collection Services (KPS), Reference and Information Literacy Services, Photocopy Services, Accreditation Support, and Inter-Library Cooperation Services. Not only that, online services such as SKBP Management, E-Journal and E-Book Article Ordering, Book Ordering (KPS), Turnitin Test, and Final Work Submission are also available. With complete facilities, such as a comfortable reading room, conference room, information literacy room, and practical facilities such as photocopying, wifi, canteen, musalla, and parking area, this library is a place that inspires and provides valuable experiences in seeking and exploring knowledge.
