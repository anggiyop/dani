Jalan Semarang Culinary Center

Jalan Semarang in Medan City is a thrilling and unique area with two distinct functions. During the day, the place transforms into a vehicle parts sales center, but when night falls, the atmosphere completely changes into a culinary paradise. The Jalan Semarang area in the Medan Kota sub-district reflects Medan's rich multiethnic culture. The vital role of ethnic Chinese in their cultural contact with the Malay and Batak ethnic groups has created a diverse and pleasant atmosphere here. Since the 1960s, this area has been a favorite night out for Medan citizens, and to this day, it continues to captivate visitors with various delicious culinary offerings. At night, the 200-meter-long street is lined with food stalls serving mouth-watering non-halal Chinese food. If you visit Jalan Semarang at night, you'll be spoiled for choice with mouth-watering Chinese food, ranging from light snacks to heavy dishes, available until midnight. Savor the delights of fluffy Hainanese duck rice or the pleasures of Tiong Sim dumpling noodles. However, what makes the culinary experience at Jalan Semarang even more interesting is the extreme menus that are rarely found elsewhere. You can try the unique sensation of tasting snake meat, monitor lizards, labi-labi, and even bats. Don't worry about your budget, as Jalan Semarang has a wide range of prices. From Rp20,000 to Rp50,000 per portion, you can find a dish that suits your taste and pocket. Not only for locals, Jalan Semarang is also an attraction for tourists who want to experience Medan's culinary richness and cultural uniqueness. So, if you're looking for a place that combines an exciting culinary adventure, Jalan Semarang is a destination you shouldn't miss in Medan City.
