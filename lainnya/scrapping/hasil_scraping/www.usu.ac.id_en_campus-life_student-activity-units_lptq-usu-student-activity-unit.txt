About LPTQ USU Student Activity Unit

LPTQ USU is one of the student activity units at USU that focuses on spirituality for Muslim students and serves as a platform for the development of Quran recitation skills for its members. Established on December 21, 2017, this student activity unit has been instrumental in encouraging its members to achieve excellence in Quran recitation competitions at regional and national levels. It is a suitable platform for USU students who wish to discover and enhance their interests and talents in the field of Quranic knowledge and skills. LPTQ USU is guided by the principle of creating a generation of Quranic achievers. For USU students who aim to excel in Quran recitation competitions, enhance their soft skills, improve their capacity for Quranic knowledge, and expand their network of fellow believers, this student activity unit is a thoughtful choice.
