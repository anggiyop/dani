USU Love Park

The USU Love Park is one of the tree-filled parks at USU. This park is located on Jl. Dr. A. Sofian at the 1st entrance area of USU. This park is near the USU Soccer Field, in the corner of the USU 1st Entrance complex. Students and the local community usually use this park to relax while enjoying the shady and fresh environment.
