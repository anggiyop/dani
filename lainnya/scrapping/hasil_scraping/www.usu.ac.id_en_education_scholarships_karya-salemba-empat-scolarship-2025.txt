Karya Salemba Empat (KSE) Scholarship Registration Timeline 2025

Online Scholarship Roadshow and Socialization: March 15-16, 2025
Online Account Registration (Application): March 16-25, 2025
Online Registration and Student Data Completion: March 16-30, 2025
Online Document Completeness Selection by KSE: May 1-14, 2025
Interview Selection by KSE Community: May 15 - June 31, 2025
Submission of Candidates Who Passed the Interview Stage: June 14-24, 2025
Preparation of Candidate Profile Documents for Interview: June 25 - July 1, 2025
Online Interview and Selection: July 19, 20, 26, and 27, 2025
Scholarship Acceptance Announcement: August 1, 2025
PTN Acceptance Verification: August 4-16, 2025

 

Karya Salemba Empat (KSE) Scholarship Terms and Conditions 2025/2026

Active undergraduate student at the University of North Sumatra
Not receiving a scholarship from another party
Unmarried applicants
Outstanding academic performance and active in social activities
Completing all data and uploading documents on the official Karya Salemba Empat scholarship website

 

Karya Salemba Empat (KSE) Scholarship Benefits and Amount 2025/2026

Living allowance of IDR 750,000 per month, extendable up to the 8th semester
Membership in KSE USU and KSE Nusantara communities
Access to Leadership Programs, Workshops, and Coaching
Opportunity to participate in Technology For Indonesia (TFI), research training, design, and technological innovation for Indonesia
Community development programs in several assisted villages under the KSE USU Community
Networking opportunities with students from across Indonesia

 

Karya Salemba Empat (KSE) Scholarship Document Requirements 2025/2026

Close-up and full-body photos wearing an alma mater jacket with a white background
Photocopy of ID card (KTP), student ID card (KTM), and family card
Parent/guardian salary slip or income statement
Academic transcript
Latest electricity bill
Latest water bill (if applicable)
Recommendation letter/certificate stating no other scholarships are received
Essay
Vlog or video (1-2 minutes) introducing yourself, family background, and reasons for applying for the scholarship
