About Archives Office

Universitas Sumatera Utara Archive Center was established on September 21, 2011, based on Rector Decree Number 2472/UN5.1.R/SK/TPM/2011 on the Establishment of the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Archive Center. The establishment of the USU Archives Center by Law Number 43 of 2009 concerning Archives in Article 27 aims to smooth archives management and orderly administration within USU and follow up on the Decree of the USU Board of Trustees Number 1/SK/MWA/I/2005 dated January 8, 2005, concerning USU Bylaws. The USU Archives Headquarters building is located at Perpustakaan Street Number 3, USU Medan Campus. Opening hours are 08.00-16.00 WIB. Website address archive.usu.ac.id, and Instagram @arsipusu.official, Youtube: USU Archives, email pusatarsip@usu.ac.id
