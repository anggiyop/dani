Undergraduate Program In Public Administration Science
Undergraduate Program
Public Administration Science
Universitas Sumatera Utara

With an A accreditation rating, the Undergraduate Program in Public Administration Science becomes the academic unit that executes the management of public organizations, such as human resources, performance management, local government systems, leadership, financial administration, public policy, etc.

With an exceptional lineup of nationally certified teaching staff, students are provided with knowledge of the state administration system, local government, and bureaucracy as well as skills to comprehensively manage various types of organizations and the ability to make strategic decisions. Graduates have broad career prospects, including politicians, government consultants, activists, journalists, researchers, and educators.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
