Medan Zoo

Medan Zoo is a zoo located in Medan City and has been established since 1952. As one of the oldest zoos in Indonesia, Medan Zoo has a large area that includes a variety of natural habitats. It houses various species of animals, including mammals, birds, reptiles, and amphibians. Visitors can explore the zoo while learning about biodiversity and conservation efforts. Medan Zoo also provides other facilities like a children's playground, restaurants, and exciting animal shows. Until now, the zoo is still managed by the Medan City government and is ready to serve visitors. The entrance fee for Medan Zoo may vary depending on the category of visitors, such as children, adults, locals, or foreign tourists.

Other Information

Medan Zoo, established in 1952, is one of the oldest zoos in Indonesia that offers various other facilities as well, such as children's play areas, restaurants and animal shows.
