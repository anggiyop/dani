The Faculty of Psychology (FPsi) at the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) has been an excellent educational platform since 2007, providing the best quality psychology learning. FPsi USU offers three main education programs: an Undergraduate Program in Psychology, a Master's Program in Professional Psychology, and a Master's Program in Psychological Science. We provide laboratories with the best equipment to support practicum activities and a comprehensive curriculum on various aspects of human behavior taught by qualified and experienced faculty. FPsi USU is committed to becoming a center of educational excellence, integrating local wisdom and agro-industrial aspects in psychology education to produce psychologists competent in understanding behavior, mental health, and human relations.

Mission of Faculty of Psychology

Education

To provide psychology education which serves as a foundation for character development and professionalism of human resources based on scientific studies, morals, and conscience.

Research

To create psychological scientists and psychologists who are capable of being agents of change in the empowerment of local expertise and agro-industrial development based on religious morals, culture, psychological code of ethics, and having competitiveness at the national and international levels.

Service

To carry out education, research, and community service in the field of psychology in order to improve academic quality, which is beneficial for improving society.
