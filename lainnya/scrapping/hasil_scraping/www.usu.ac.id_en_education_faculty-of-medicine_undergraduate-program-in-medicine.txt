Undergraduate Program
Medicine
Universitas Sumatera Utara

As the oldest study program at Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU), the Undergraduate Program in Medicine has long been at the forefront of providing quality medical education. With a comprehensive curriculum and lecturers who are experts in their fields, this study program aims to train competent and charismatic doctors who are ready to face challenges in an increasingly complex medical world. Students will gain in-depth clinical experience supported by adequate facilities.

Built by a team of leading academics and medical practitioners, the USU Undergraduate Program in Medicine produces graduates ready to contribute to ensuring quality healthcare services and play an active role in the development of the national health system. Graduates of this study program will earn the Bachelor of Medicine (S.Ked.) degree.

Vision of Undergraduate Program in Medicine

To establish the Medical Doctor Profession Education Program of the Universitas Sumatera Utara Faculty of Medicine into a quality medical doctor profession education program by 2024, towards the Center of Excellence in Tropical Medicine and Oncology Prevention in Southeast Asia in supporting the Vision of the Universitas Sumatera Utara Faculty of Medicine to become an educational institution with TALENTA Competitive edge.

Mission of Undergraduate Program in Medicine

Quality of Education, Research, and Community Service

To enhance the quality of education, research, and community service, consistently, effectively and efficiently, and in accordance with advancements in medical science and technology, through good faculty governance and a good quality assurance system.

Agent of Change

To produce graduates who are agents of change as a driving force of modernization in society, equipped with scientific competence, relevance, and strong competitiveness as well as professional and ethical intellectual behavior within the framework of Interprofessional Education / Collaboration (IPE/IPC).

Public Leadership

To establish public leadership through the benefits of research, and community service that has an impact on epidemiology especially in tropical diseases and oncology prevention, with product and scientific development orientation.

Independent and Professional

To establish an independent and professional Faculty in the management and development of the institution and build collaborations with both national and international institutions, especially in the fields of tropical medicine and oncology prevention.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
