Minokit
Modul Pembelajaran Robotik Dasar
Product Description

Minokit is a robotics learning kit designed to introduce fundamental concepts to users in assembling analog and digital robotics modules. These modules include sensors, potentiometers, LEDs, LCDs, buttons, and keypads. The Minokit consists of 11 modules that can be used simultaneously with up to 8 modules. The unique feature of Minokit lies in its cable-free design, making it easier for elementary and junior high school students to learn electronics and basic concepts of robotics that involve the use of sensors.
