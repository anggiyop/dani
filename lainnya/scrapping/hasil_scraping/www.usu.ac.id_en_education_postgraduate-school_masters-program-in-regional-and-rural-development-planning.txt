Master's Program In Regional And Rural Development Planning
Master Program
Regional and Rural Development Planning
Universitas Sumatera Utara

Established in 1992, the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Master's Program in Regional and Rural Development Planning focuses on multidisciplinary education based on an integrated system approach.

Supported by outstanding lecturers with national teaching certificates, our students are guided to master, analyze, and solve regional development planning problems, especially in village areas. Graduates of the USU Master's Program in Regional and Rural Development Planning are expected to become educators and experts who can become the core of regional development problem solvers spread across various agencies in Indonesia, both government and private.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take.
