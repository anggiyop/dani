Artsari Soap
 

Published by

Renny Julia Harahap

Published at

Tuesday, 13 July 2021

Artsari is a solid coconut oil based bath soap/shampoo. It uses natural basis ingredient with no SLS (sodium lauryl sulfate) that often used as detergent.

Artsari soap/shampoo consists of 4 soap variants, which are lemongrass, eucalyptus, summer squash and betel

Two shampoo variants, which are spinach and aloe vera    

The products are ready for sale and has been produced with the provision of CPKB (Good Ways of Making Cosmetics) standardized of BBPOM (National Agency of Drug and Food Control)

Inventor : Ir. Lilis Sukeksi, M.Sc, Ph,D

Faculty : Chemical Engineering of USU

Product Category : Cosmetics
