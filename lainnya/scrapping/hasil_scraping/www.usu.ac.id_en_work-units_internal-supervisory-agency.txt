About Internal Supervisory Agency

The Internal Supervisory Agency of Universitas Sumatera Utara (BPI USU) is a supporting unit that operates under the coordination of the Rector and reports to the Rector for the internal control of both academic and non-academic activities at USU. BPI USU operates independently from all work units in Universitas Sumatera Utara. With a focus on the implementation of preventive, oversight, and examination functions, BPI USU aims to enhance accountability, efficiency, effectiveness, and compliance with applicable regulations.
