Kampoeng Selfie (Kongsi)

Now, a new trend in tourist destinations is sweeping several regions in Indonesia: creative neighborhood tourism. Medan City is one of the hosts for Kampung Selfie Tourism, the first neighborhood destination in North Sumatra that captivates the heart. This neighborhood represents the diversity of ethnicities and religions, beautifully restored by the sub-district government and local residents. However, what makes it truly special is the stunning artistic creativity with striking paint colors that decorate the entire neighborhood. Kampung Selfie Medan officially opened on November 25, 2017. After that, this destination has become an attraction for tourists who want to experience the excitement of taking selfies against the backdrop of various creative photo spots. Currently, because this tourist destination is still developing, visitors can enjoy it without being charged an entrance fee. Located on Jalan DR. Cipto, Anggrung, Medan Polonia, Medan City, North Sumatra, Kampung Selfie Medan is a place not to be missed for anyone who wants to explore and enjoy the uniqueness of art and culture in an enchanting creative atmosphere. Come visit to enjoy the excitement and beauty this Kampung Selfie offers, an interesting and innovative tourist destination.

Other Information

Kampoeng Selfie, located on Jalan DR Cipto, Medan Polonia, has been a creative village tour in Medan that showcases artistic creativity and provides contemporary photo spots for tourists since 2017.
