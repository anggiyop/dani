Avros Park

Avros Park, an extraordinary tourist destination and educational park, is located in the middle of Medan, precisely on Jalan Avros, Kampung Baru, Medan Maimun District. Its charm lies not only in its strategic location, but also in the creativity of utilizing the Deli River as one of the enjoyable educational rides. Various educational rides at Avros Park offer an unforgettable experience. Visitors can participate in handicraft-making activities, spread fish seeds, and plant trees to support environmental sustainability. However, the highlight of the exclusive experience at Avros Park is the boat ride along the Deli River. This ride has successfully changed the negative perception of the Deli River, as it presents an in-depth understanding of the river's history and the importance of keeping the environment clean, especially around the river. Besides presenting inspiring educational rides, Avros Park also provides an appetizing culinary area. It has beautiful photo spots, which are perfect for capturing and ideal for sharing beautiful moments on social media. Visitors can enjoy delicious dishes at affordable prices, making a visit to Avros Park an experience that satisfies all the senses. Avros Park welcomes visitors daily, from 09:00 to 23:00 WIB on weekdays and 08:00 to 23:00 WIB on weekends. Visitors can enjoy the beauty of this park free of charge, but to enjoy the educational rides provided, tickets are available with prices starting from IDR 20,000. So, come join the fun and educational adventure at Avros Park, where happiness and knowledge await you!

Other Information
