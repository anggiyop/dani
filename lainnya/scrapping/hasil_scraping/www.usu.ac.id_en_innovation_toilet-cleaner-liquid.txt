USU Toilet+
Toilet Cleaner Liquid
Product Description

USU Toilet+ is an eco-friendly toilet cleaner with a coffee aroma. Using this cleaner will make the toilet clean and free from unpleasant odors. In addition, this cleaner's formula is designed so that the remaining cleaner that enters the water will not interfere with the activity of decomposing bacteria.
