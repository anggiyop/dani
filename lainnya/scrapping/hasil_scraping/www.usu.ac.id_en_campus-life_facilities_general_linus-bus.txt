Lintas USU (Linus) Bus

The Lintas USU (LinUS) Bus is an innovative mobility solution from USU that provides free services to students. With 10 buses, it travels around the USU Campus area every lecture day. Students can take this bus by waiting at the LinUS Bus stops, which can be found around USU's 1st and 4th entrances. A favorite among students, the LinUS Bus offers comfort with ergonomic seats and air conditioning. Apart from being transportation, this bus is also a place to meet and interact with students. It is an attractive and efficient transportation option on the USU Campus.
