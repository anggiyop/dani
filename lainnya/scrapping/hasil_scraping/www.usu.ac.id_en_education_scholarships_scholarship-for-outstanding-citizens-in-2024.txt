Scholarship for Outstanding Citizens in 2024

What is the Excellent Scholarship?

The Excellent Scholarship is a government-funded educational grant provided by the Indonesian government to the nation’s top talents pursuing undergraduate, master's, and doctoral degrees at designated universities.

What is the Prestigious Community Scholarship?

The Prestigious Community Scholarship is a part of the Excellent Scholarship program awarded to individuals with outstanding intellectual, emotional, and spiritual abilities to pursue higher education at the undergraduate, master's, and doctoral levels.

Timeline for the Prestigious Community Scholarship

Registration: July 1 – July 14, 2024
Stage I Selection: July 15 – August 4, 2024
Stage I Selection Results Announcement: August 9, 2024
Stage II Selection: August 12 – August 31, 2024
Stage II Selection Results Announcement: September 10, 2024
Briefing and Technical Explanation for Contract Signing: September 2024
