Master's Program In English Language
Master Program
English Language
Universitas Sumatera Utara

As an advanced study program after the Undergraduate Program in English Literature, the Master's Program in English Language of Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) takes a more in-depth look at the English Language, including an in-depth analysis of the language's origins and etymology as well as its global impact. Supported by English literature experts with national teaching certifications and an integrated curriculum, we are determined to provide intense and effective guidance and learning experiences to our students. Our goal is to enhance students' abilities and prepare them to become competent and excellent English literature experts in their fields so that they can compete both in the national and international markets. Graduates of the Master's Program in English Language are expected to have expertise in the philosophy of science, research methodology, cross-cultural communication, sociolinguistics, and a deep understanding of crisis discourse.

Graduates of the Master's Program in English Language have the potential to pursue careers in various fields, including as translators, educators, embassy staff, or language editors.

Vision of Master's Program in English Language

To become a leading center of excellence in education, development, and study of socio-culture and the English language theoretically and practically by the year 2026.

Mission of Master's Program in English Language

Scientific Quality

To enhance the quality of knowledge (education, development, and research) in the English language.

Research

To disseminate research findings in the English language not only at regional and national levels but also internationally through journal publications and scientific events (seminars, conferences, etc.).

Academic Cooperations

To develop academic and professional cooperation (exchange of teaching staff and/or students) with similar study programs at national and international levels.

International Level

To not only achieve the highest national accreditation level but also to be recognized as a study program at the international level.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
