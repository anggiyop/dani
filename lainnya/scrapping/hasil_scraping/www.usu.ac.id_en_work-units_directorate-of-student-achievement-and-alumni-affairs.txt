About Directorate of Student Achievement and Alumni Affairs

The Directorate of Student Achievement and Alumni Affairs at Universitas Sumatera Utara manages extracurricular student activities, such as training, coaching, achievements, welfare, and alumni affairs at the university level. These activities include the Student Creativity Program (PKM), student activity proposals, scholarships, and tracer studies to track alumni history. The Directorate of Student Achievement and Alumni Affairs is led by Vice Chancellor IV. The main tasks of the Directorate of Student Achievement and Alumni Affairs include preparing work plans for the Bureau of Student Affairs and Alumni Affairs, as well as developing, improving, and implementing administrative activities related to student interests, talents, and welfare.
