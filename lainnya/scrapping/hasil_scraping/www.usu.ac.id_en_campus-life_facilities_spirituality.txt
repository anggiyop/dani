Spirituality Facilities

Spiritual facilities at Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) are designed to support the spiritual and religious needs of students, providing spaces and services that allow them to practice their religion comfortably. One example is several mosques and prayer rooms located in various faculties to facilitate students in performing prayers and other religious activities without having to leave the campus area. With these facilities, USU is committed to supporting students' religious activities and creating an inclusive campus environment that supports various spiritual needs.
