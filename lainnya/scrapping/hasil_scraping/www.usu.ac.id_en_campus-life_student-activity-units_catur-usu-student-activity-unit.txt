About Catur USU Student Activity Unit

The Chess Student Activity Unit (UKM Catur) at USU is a platform for students interested in chess talent. This organization was established to develop strategic thinking skills, enhance analytical abilities, and foster a spirit of sportsmanship among students. Regular activities conducted by members include scheduled training sessions, internal tournaments among students, and participation in external competitions at regional and national levels.
