Undergraduate Program
Political Science
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Undergraduate Program in Political Science at the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) was established in 2001 and has obtained A accreditation. The program is responsible for the study of socio-political phenomena at home and abroad.

With three concentrations of study, namely Indonesian Politics, Comparative Politics, and Islamic Politics, we have expert lecturers who have extensive experience in the field of politics. Supported by adequate facilities and infrastructure, we are committed to producing students who become competent political science candidates. They are ready for careers in various government agencies, non-governmental organizations, and private companies.

Mission of Undergraduate Program in Political Science

Institutional Capacity

Increase institutional capacity in implementing teaching and learning, research, and public service.

Agent of Change

Producing graduates who become agents of change as a force of modernization in the life of the wider community, who have scientific competence, relevance, and strong competitiveness, and who behave with ethical scholarship.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
