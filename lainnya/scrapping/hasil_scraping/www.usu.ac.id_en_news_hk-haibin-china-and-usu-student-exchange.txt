HK Haibin China and USU Student Exchange
 

Published by

Bambang Riyanto

Published at

Tuesday, 30 August 2022

WR III said that the collaboration between the two parties was aimed at student exchanges between HK Haibin and USU in the field of masters. There will be 15 students from HK Haibin who will continue their master's education at USU, such as at the Faculty of Cultural Sciences (FIB), Faculty of Social and Political Sciences, Faculty of Economics and Business (FEB), and others.

PR USU - Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) cooperates with HK Haibin Education Group Limited China. This is stated in the memorandum of understanding signed by the Vice Rector III for Research, Community Service, and Cooperation, Prof. Dr. Poppy Anjelisa Zaitun Hasibuan, S.Si., M.Sc., Apt with Hu Haibin B. Eng., MEc., MBA., Ph.D. The activity took place in the Academic Senate Room of the USU Central Administration on Monday (8/22/2022).

WR III said that the collaboration between the two parties was aimed at student exchanges between HK Haibin and USU in the field of masters. There will be 15 students from HK Haibin who will continue their master's education at USU, such as at the Faculty of Cultural Sciences (FIB), Faculty of Social and Political Sciences, Faculty of Economics and Business (FEB), and others.

"A total of 15 HK Haibin students study at USU by taking a Master program," he said.

WR III also hopes that this collaboration will not only be in the field of education but in the fields of research, research, and others so that this collaboration is a form of improving the quality of human resources, as well as opening up wider space for the implementation of various activities that support skill development and also hopes to get a letter of recommendation from the minister of education in China so that USU name is better known in the international arena.

Hu Haibin, PhD said that if there are students at USU who want to continue their education in China, HK Haibin is ready to help USU to become a bridge between USU and universities in China because there are three universities that want to cooperate with USU as well.

Hu Haibin, PhD said that his party chose USU as a cooperation partner because USU is one of the best universities in Sumatra. And also USU is ranked in the QS World Rankings and various master's majors are available at USU so that HK Haibin students can choose as they wish.

“We hope that this collaboration will run smoothly as expected and that many students will study at USU. USU is made as a partner because USU is the best university in Sumatra," he said. (©ULC)
