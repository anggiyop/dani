Doctoral Program In Communication Science
Doctoral Program
Communication Science
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Doctoral Program in Communication Science is the highest educational study in communication science at USU. This Doctoral Program provides the best quality communication science education emphasizing research aspects.

Our study program focuses on exploring communication studies to prepare students to face changes and developments in scientific concepts and communication theories. Supported by the best teaching staff who are competent in the field of communication, our study program is ready to produce doctoral candidates in communication science who are globally competitive and contribute significantly to the development of communication science research.

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take.
