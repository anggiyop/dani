BSI Scholarship Inspirasi 2023

BSI Scholarship is a scholarship program initiated by BSI Maslahat and Bank Syariah Indonesia. The target of this program is specifically for undergraduate students (S1) in the 3rd & 5th semesters or 2nd & 3rd-year students. This program will last for 2 years or 4 semesters.
BSI Scholarship Inspirasi is a scholarship aimed at equalizing education for underprivileged students. It is hoped that the BSI Scholarship Inspirasi program can form future leaders of the ummah who are characterized, trustworthy, and role models who can contribute to building the ummah, especially in the Islamic financial economy industry.

What are the benefits of the BSI Scholarship Inspirasi Program?
A tuition fee support of IDR3,000,000 for 4 semesters.
Self-leadership guidance and character development.
Islamic financial economics training.
Social Project Program.
Opportunity for an internship in the Islamic economic industry.
Who can apply for the BSI Scholarship Inspirasi program?
Those in the category of underprivileged students, as evidenced by SKTM (Confirmation of Poor Family).
Active second-year and third-year students or in their third and fifth semesters from designated campuses.
Minimum GPA of 3.00
Not currently receiving a similar type of scholarship.
What documents and files are required to complete for the BSI Scholarship Inspirasi?
Formal photo (wearing a black suit/blazer with a green background, turquoise green color code: 009B97)
Scanned Identity Card (KTP)
Scanned Student Identity Card (KTM)
Scanned Family Card (KK)
Scanned transcript of the latest GPA
Scanned latest SKTM from the local authorities
Scanned certificates of achievements
Scanned proof of the last three months' electricity bill payments
Scanned proof of last semester's tuition fee payment
Photos of the house (front view and living room)
