Third Place for Best Stand Winner, USU's Innovation Products Are in Demand
 

Published by

Bambang Riyanto

Published at

Wednesday, 10 August 2022

"After participating in the exhibition, it turns out that many people are interested in the products we serve, which is marked by 4 superior products that are selling well, including yoghurt, wikstea, calbena, and rough drinks," he said.

PR USU - Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) participated in the 8th North Sumatra Innovation and Investment Week. The activity took place at the Open Stage (Pagoda), Parapat, Simalungun Regency. For 3 days, July 27 to 29, 2022.

In this activity, USU was ranked 3rd as the best stand by exhibiting a number of USU lecturers' innovation products and innovation products that had been released by PT. PAS some time ago.

Head of USU's Innovation Research Development Agency, Buchari, S.T. M.Kes said that the innovations exhibited on this occasion were approximately 27 product variants. Among them are divided into food clusters, advanced materials, cosmetics and also information technology.

"At this exhibition there are approximately 27 product variants on display, where these products are innovations by lecturers who collaborate with students," he said.

He hopes that with this exhibition USU can introduce existing innovation products.

"After participating in the exhibition, it turns out that many people are interested in the products we serve, which is marked by 4 superior products that are well-sold, including yoghurt, wikstea, calbena, and rough drinks," he said.

Product Commercialization Manager Nana Dyki Dirbawanto S.E. M.A.B, said, in the future USU will digitalize its business and there will also be innovative products from students, lecturers or alumni that we will onboard on digital platforms and also in the near future we will create a marketplace owned by USU in collaboration with the existing digital platform.
