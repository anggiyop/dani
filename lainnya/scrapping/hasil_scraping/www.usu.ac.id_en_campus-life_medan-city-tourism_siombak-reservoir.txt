Siombak Reservoir

Siombak Marelan Reservoir, famous for being formed from excavating a landfill in 1980, holds a million mysteries and unique charms. Some sources even mention the possibility of the reservoir being part of the ancient Chinese City site. Visiting this lake with a diverse historical background and interesting stories is an unforgettable experience. Around Siombak Marelan Reservoir, some facilities facilitate visitors, such as public toilets, parking areas, and stalls serving delicious snacks. However, the schedule for visiting this lake needs to be considered, as it does not operate 24 hours. The daily opening schedule starts from 08.00 WIB to 17.00 WIB. Siombak Marelan Reservoir is an exciting choice for family tourist destinations. Visiting this reservoir will give visitors the alluring feeling of a lake vacation without traveling far. The atmosphere is unique and different from most lakes, making it a favorite destination for those seeking adventure and tranquility at the same time. So, don't miss the opportunity to explore the beauty and adventure of Siombak Marelan Reservoir with your beloved family.

Other Information

Lake Siombak Marelan is famous for its origin and unique attraction. Various facilities are provided to facilitate visitors when visiting this tourist spot, such as public toilets and food stalls.
