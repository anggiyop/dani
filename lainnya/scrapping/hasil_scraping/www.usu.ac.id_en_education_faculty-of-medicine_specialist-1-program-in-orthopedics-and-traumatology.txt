Specialist-1 Program In Orthopedics And Traumatology
Spesialist-1 Program
Orthopedic Surgery and Traumatology
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Specialist Program in Orthopaedics and Traumatology of Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) is a study program that studies specialized medical skills focusing on teaching orthopedics and traumatology, designed to produce graduates who are competent in diagnosing and treating various injuries and diseases of the musculoskeletal system. With an A accreditation, the cooperation between this program and orthopedic and traumatology education centers in Indonesia and abroad makes graduates of this specialist program possess a high work ethic and professionalism in diagnosing, managing, and treating injuries caused by sports or physical activities.

Graduates of the USU Specialist Program in Orthopaedic and Traumatology are able to compete nationally and internationally. Students who have completed this study program will hold the Orthopaedics and Traumatology Specialist (Sp.O.T.) degree.
