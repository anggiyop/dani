About Gamadiksi USU Student Activity Unit

Gamadiksi USU is a student activity unit specifically designed for Bidikmisi Scholarship and Kartu Indonesia Pintar Kuliah recipients. Gamadiksi USU was officially established as a recognized student organization for Bidikmisi recipients on December 17, 2011. This student activity unit, built on a foundation of kinship, encourages its members to become high-achieving, creative, independent, moral, responsible, and socially-minded Bidikmisi students. In various activities, Gamadiksi USU often organizes seminars focused on developing soft and hard skills, as well as involving its members as speakers. Gamadiksi USU Student Activity Unit always serves as a platform for Bidikmisi students to enhance their skills and become agents of social control and change.
