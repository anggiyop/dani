Diploma Program In Tax Administration
Diploma Program
Tax Administration
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Diploma Program in Tax Administration at the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) was established in 1995. It became a program under the Faculty of Vocational Education of USU, which is responsible for providing education on applied taxation science.

This study program is filled with lecturers who have broad insight and high integrity in the teaching process. It is supported by the best facilities and infrastructure to create a comfortable, effective, and efficient learning environment. The Diploma Program in Tax Administration offers three career concentration paths: assistant tax managers, internal tax auditors, and tax accounting staff. Students are expected to have competencies in tax planning, tax liability execution, tax control, and a deep understanding of relevant tax laws.

Vision of Diploma Program in Tax Administration

Becoming an International Standard Study Program with Local Excellence in Tax Administration that can Compete at the Global Level in 2026.

Mission of Diploma Program in Tax Administration

Education

Organize education in the field of Tax Administration based on Smart Digital Governance.

Globally Competitive

Empower the potential and build students' character to excel through a conducive academic climate to improve the quality of graduates of the Diploma III Tax Administration Study Program who are globally competitive.

Research

Conduct and develop research in the field of Tax Administration collaboratively and published in national and international journals.

Service

Carry out and develop community service in the field of inclusive Tax Administration.

Collaboration

Build local, national, and international cooperation networks to increase competitiveness and collaboration as well as the contribution of tax administration science at the regional, national, and international levels.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
