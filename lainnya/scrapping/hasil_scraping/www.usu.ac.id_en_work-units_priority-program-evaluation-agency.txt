About Priority Program Evaluation Agency

The Priority Program Evaluation Agency is one of the working units assigned by Vice-Rector IV for Human Resources to provide full supervision and evaluation of the work programs of each unit at Universitas Sumatera Utara, as stated in the USU Strategic Plan 2020-2024. The agency is tasked with activity management, performance assessment, and evaluation as feedback for decision-makers in further planning, implementation, monitoring, and control. The agency has well-planned achievement targets to support the vision and mission of Universitas Sumatera Utara to become a world-class university.
