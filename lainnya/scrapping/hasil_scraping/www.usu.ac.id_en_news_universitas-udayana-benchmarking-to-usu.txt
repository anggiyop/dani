USU - Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) received a visit from Udayana University (UNUD) in the Benchmarking Activity for the Financial Management of State Universities with Legal Entities (PTNBH). The activity took place in the Video Conference Room of the USU Central Administration Bureau Building on Monday (11/12/2023).

This activity is a benchmarking study on the digitalization system for university financial management. Emerson Pascawira Sinulingga, ST., M.Sc., Ph.D., the Director of the Directorate of Digitalization and System Integration of USU, as the speaker. This is a collaboration between the USU Finance Bureau and the Vice Dean II for Finance and Human Resources of UNUD.

USU Vice Rector II Muhammad Arifin Nasution S.Sos., M.SP., in his speech, revealed that since 2021, the financial management process at USU has been digital-based. USU adjusted this change in line with efforts to achieve World Class University.

"Today, we will discuss how to manage finances, especially the management of SPI and UKT at USU. We only created this model at the end of 2021, using digitalization in management. We are in a position requested by the ministry to achieve World Class University. In this process, USU has also changed its financial management," said Vice Rector II of USU.

Director of the Directorate of Digitalization and System Integration of USU, Emerson Pascawira Sinulingga, ST., M.Sc., Ph.D., stated that USU and UNUD have similarities in university management. Therefore, this visit aims to be a place for learning and sharing ideas to develop and accelerate the process of PTNBH policy management at UNUD.

"One of the team leaders stated that the Director General of Dikti recommended USU as a very suitable place to learn how to manage PTNBH. So that's why they visited us as a place to learn and exchange ideas in developing and accelerating the acceleration process of PTNBH policy management at Udayana," said Emerson Pascawira Sinulingga, ST., M.Sc., Ph.D.
