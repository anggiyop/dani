Diploma Program In Chemistry
Diploma Program
Chemistry
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Diploma Program in Chemistry at Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) was established in 1995 and is responsible for vocational education in industrial and analytical chemistry development.

Our study program is managed by professors, doctors, and masters as outstanding educators, supported by the best facilities to create an effective and efficient academic atmosphere. The main objective of the Diploma Program in Chemistry is to train graduates skilled in synthesizing renewable chemical compounds, chemical analysis techniques, interpretation of analytical data, and understanding the environmental impacts of industrial activities and their solutions. Graduates of this program have broad career prospects, including becoming quality control and quality assurance personnel, laboratory analysts, researchers, and industrial practitioners.

Vision of Diploma Program in Chemistry

To become a regionally superior study program and produce professionals in the field of laboratory analysis who can analyze chemistry and quality control in various products consumed by the public and industrial processes according to national and regional standards.

Mission of Diploma Program in Chemistry

Competent Graduates

Develop a teaching and learning process based on a curriculum and syllabus oriented towards the labor market's needs to produce graduates who have competence in chemical analysis and quality control of various industrial products.

Collaboration

Engage with government institutions and the business world to facilitate field work and internships for students.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
