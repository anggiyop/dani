Percut Water Park

Percut Water Park is a stunning tourist destination in Deli Serdang, North Sumatra. The park is located on approximately 15 hectares of land and offers a concept that combines water tourism and education, creating an ideal place for various exciting activities. In addition to providing fun water entertainment, Percut Water Park also offers a variety of educational activities. Amidst the stunning natural beauty, visitors can learn about biodiversity, keeping water clean, and the importance of environmental conservation. To get the latest updates, see interesting photos, information about facilities, special event schedules, and other exciting content related to Taman Air Percut, please visit its Instagram profile at @tamanairpercut. There you will find more complete and up-to-date information about this tourist destination.
