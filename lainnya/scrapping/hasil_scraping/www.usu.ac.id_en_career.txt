In Universitas Sumatera Utara

Get an amazing opportunity to grow and contribute to the world of higher education at Universitas Sumatera Utara.

Join a dynamic and innovative academic community, where your practical experience and in-depth knowledge will be valued in an inspiring learning environment. We invite individuals who are passionate about building a career in higher education to explore our collaborative opportunities, which include relevant curriculum development, impactful research, and tangible contributions to society. Join the change that shapes future leaders and embark on a meaningful career journey with us at Universitas Sumatera Utara.

University Lectures

Be part of a team of excellence at Universitas Sumatera Utara. We invite individuals with educational qualifications and expertise in their fields to join us and contribute to moulding future generations through quality teaching and impactful research. The latest announcement about USU Permanent Lecturer Recruitment can be seen at the following link:

USU Permanent Lecturer Recruitment
General Requirement
Indonesian citizen;
Pious to God Almighty
Not yet 35 (thirty-five) years old for graduates of master program (S2) and not yet 45 (forty-five) years old for graduates of doctoral program (S3) as of 1 October 2023;
physically, mentally, and mentally healthy, and drug-free as evidenced by a doctor certificate from a hospital (the certificate is submitted if it has been declared accepted);
never been sentenced to imprisonment or confinement for committing a criminal offence based on a court decision that has permanent legal force;
not under contract as a permanent employee/lecturer at another institution;
has never been dishonourably dismissed as a Civil Servant or as a private employee;
has a high commitment to the development of the Universitas Sumatera Utara;
have academic qualifications of master programme (S2) or doctoral programme (S3) in the field of science in accordance with the formation, except those that require academic qualifications in certain fields;
are graduates of undergraduate (S1) and master (S2) programmes accredited by the National Accreditation Board for Higher Education (BAN-PT)/Independent Accreditation Agency (LAM) at least equivalent to B (for domestic graduates);
graduates from universities accredited by the National Accreditation Board for Higher Education (BAN-PT) at least equivalent to B (for domestic graduates);
one applicant can only apply for one formation; and
general requirements change if stipulated in special requirements special requirements
Spesific Requirement
not yet 45 (forty-five) years old for graduates of specialist doctor/dentist programs as of 1 October 2023;
for graduates of doctoral programmes (S3) accredited by BAN-PT or LAM (for domestic graduates);
for graduates of doctor/ dentist specialist 1 (Sp.1) programme accredited by BAN-PT or LAM (for domestic graduates);
Cumulative Grade Point Average (GPA) of at least 3.00 (scale 4) for graduates of undergraduate (S1)/magister (S2)/doctor (S3)/professional/specialist 1 (Sp.1) programmes (for domestic graduates);
has a TOEFL score of at least 500 (five hundred) or equivalent;
have proficiency, expertise and supporting skills and competence in accordance with the field of duties (attach supporting evidence, if any); and
preferably have scientific articles published in accredited national journals/reputable international journals or have intellectual work that has received legal protection by the state (granted) totalling 1 (one) intellectual property;
