About Board of Professors

The Board of Professors is a board at Universitas Sumatera Utara that serves as an advisor, a monitor of the moral and ethical integrity of the academic community, as well as a developer of ideas and positions on national and international strategic issues. The primary purpose of the Board of Professors is to provide a review of successes and failures in achieving targets to determine the obstacles found in the implementation of educational activities.
