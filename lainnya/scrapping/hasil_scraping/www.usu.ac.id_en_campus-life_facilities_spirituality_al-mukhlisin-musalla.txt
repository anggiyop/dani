Al Mukhlisin Musalla

The Al Mukhlisin Musalla is located within the premises of the USU Faculty of Agriculture, precisely on Jl. Prof. Hanafiah. This musalla is of significant size, similar to a mosque, making it convenient for the academic community of the Faculty of Agriculture to perform congregational worship. This musalla is also already equipped with facilities such as prayer mats and air conditioning to provide comfort during worship.
