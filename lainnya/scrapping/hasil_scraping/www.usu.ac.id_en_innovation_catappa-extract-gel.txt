Catappa Extract Gel
Beauty Cream
Product Description

Catappa Extract Gel is a gel that contains 2% ethanol extract of catappa leaves as its natural active ingredient. This gel is proven to be highly effective in fighting acne-causing bacteria, such as Propionibacterium acnes and Staphylococcus epidermidis, thanks to its flavonoids and tannins. In addition, Catappa Extract Gel also has non-irritant effects on the skin, making it a safe and convenient choice for acne-prone skin care.
