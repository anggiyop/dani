About Kendo USU Student Activity Unit

The Kendo Student Activity Unit (UKM Kendo) at USU is a platform for students who have an interest and talent in the martial art of Kendo. This organization was established to develop skills in traditional Japanese sword techniques, enhance self-discipline, and foster a spirit of sportsmanship among students. Regular activities conducted by members include scheduled training, rank promotion exams (grading), and participation in competitions at both national and international levels.
