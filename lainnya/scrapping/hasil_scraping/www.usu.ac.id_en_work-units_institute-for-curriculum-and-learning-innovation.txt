About Institute for Curriculum and Learning Innovation

The Institute for Curriculum and Learning Innovation (LINKUP) of USU is an institute responsible for monitoring and evaluating all learning programs at Universitas Sumatera Utara. LINKUP USU is dedicated to expanding participation in learning by providing input to the university and faculties on learning issues as well as enhancing the teaching and learning process through the development of instructional science and technology and preparing teaching staff to deliver effective instruction. The management and all personnel of LINKUP USU are committed to improving the quality of services and educational development for the academic community of USU through relevant training in accordance with the advancements in science and technology.
