Tennis Court

In addition to basketball and futsal courts, the Universitas Sumatera Utara also provides tennis courts at the USU Mini Stadium area and the Civil Engineering Department. The USU tennis court is an adequate facility for students, staff, and lecturers who want to play tennis as a sports activity. With a beautiful and conducive environment, the USU tennis court can be the right place for tennis enthusiasts to practice and hone their skills. In addition, the facilities provided at the USU tennis courts are also complete and well-maintained, such as spectator seats, tennis nets, and adequate lighting. The USU tennis court is the right choice for students, staff, and lecturers who want to exercise and seek healthy entertainment on campus.
