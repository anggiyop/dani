Undergraduate Program In Biology
Undergraduate Program
Biology
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Undergraduate Program in Biology was established in 1988 with the responsibility to provide quality education in the fields of industrial biology, environment, and natural resource conservation. Supported by qualified and highly committed teaching staff, the Undergraduate Program in Biology aims to produce graduates who have global competitiveness.

They are expected to be able to conduct bioresources management and research, as well as develop products related to industrial biology, the environment, and its preservation. Graduates from this program have various career opportunities, ranging from becoming researchers, pharmacologists, biotechnologists, biologists, to ecologists.

Vision of Undergraduate Program in Biology

To become a superior and regionally competitive Study Program in the field of industrial biology, environment, and preservation of natural resources.

Mission of Undergraduate Program in Biology

Quality

Organizing quality education to produce graduates who are superior and regionally competitive in the fields of industrial biology, environment, and natural resource conservation.

Scientific Work

Carry out and develop research, community service and superior scientific work in the fields of industry, environment and natural resource conservation.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
