Tip Top Restaurant

Tip Top Restaurant, established in 1934, is one of the must-visit culinary destinations in Medan. The restaurant is located in the city center, precisely on Jalan Kesawan, making it easy to find by visitors. Tip Top Restaurant not only offers gastronomic delight but also incorporates a touch of tradition. Every dish served in this restaurant reflects the richness and authenticity of Medan's culinary heritage. In addition, visitors will feel the warm and friendly atmosphere at Tip Top Restaurant, which further adds to the exceptional value of this place. This restaurant is perfect for relaxing with family or friends and celebrating special moments. With the best service provided, Tip Top Restaurant is ready to fulfill the needs and desires of visitors. So, visit Tip Top Restaurant to explore the unique flavors of Medan and indulge yourself with the special dishes served there.
