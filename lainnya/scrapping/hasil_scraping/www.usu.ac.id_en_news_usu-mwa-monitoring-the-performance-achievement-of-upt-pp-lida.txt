USU MWA Monitoring the Performance Achievement of UPT PP LIDA
 

Published by

Bambang Riyanto

Published at

Thursday, 07 April 2022

"MWA visited to see how the activities and developments at UPT PP LIDA are, in terms of monitoring how the budgeting process, work program planning and achievements are being carried out," said the Head of UPT. PP. LIDA USU Prof. Dr. Timbangen Sembiring, M.Sc.

USU Public Relations - Technical Implementation Unit of Basic and General Science Lecture Center & Laboratory (UPT PP LIDA) Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) received a working visit from Commission III of the Board of Trustees (MWA) for Monitoring and Evaluation in order to discuss the performance achievements of UPT PP LIDA .

UPT PP LIDA explained the work program planning process, budgeting, and performance targets that have been achieved during the working visit of the MWA Commission III including Prof. dr. Guslihan Dasa Tjipta, SpA(K), on Wednesday (3/23/2022),.

"MWA visited to see how the activities and developments at UPT PP LIDA are, in terms of monitoring how the budgeting process, work program planning and achievements are being carried out," said the Head of UPT. PP. LIDA USU Prof. Dr. Timbangen Sembiring, M.Sc.

According to Prof. Dr. Timbangen, MWA provided very important inputs to improve performance in supporting the rector's work program and achieving the work program of UPT PP LIDA. One thing that requires special attention is the laboratory equipment and infrastructure. "We hope that the problem in terms of achieving the rector's work program, UPT PP LIDA's work program can be carried out properly. The implementation of work programs can be supported by good infrastructure," he said.

Prof. Dr. Timbangen added, UPT PP LIDA requires the support of facilities and infrastructure that are in accordance with laboratory standards. Through this visit, it is hoped that UPT PP LIDA can get more attention so that it can carry out its duties and functions better

 "MWA promises to help improve the performance of UPT PP LIDA," he concluded. (©ULC)
