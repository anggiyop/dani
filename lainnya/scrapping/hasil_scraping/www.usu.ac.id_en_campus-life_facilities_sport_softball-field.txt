Softball Field

The USU Softball Field is right beside USU Mini Stadium, with adequate facilities for softball sports activities. This field has a size that follows international standards, with a smooth and flat field surface that provides comfort for athletes in playing softball. In addition, the softball field at the USU Mini Stadium complex is also equipped with spectator benches, a large parking lot, and other supporting facilities that make it easier for athletes, students, and visitors to access and utilize this field. The softball field at the USU Mini Stadium complex is suitable for use both for sports activities and other activities.
