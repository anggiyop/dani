About Rollerskate USU Student Activity Unit

The Rollerskate Student Activity Unit (UKM Sepatu Roda) at USU is a platform for students who have an interest and talent in roller skating. This organization was established to develop skills in skating techniques, enhance physical endurance, and foster a spirit of sportsmanship and teamwork among students. Regular activities conducted by members include scheduled training, skill assessments, and participation in various competitions at both national and international levels. Students interested in joining can visit the official Instagram account of the USU Rollerskate UKM for more information.
