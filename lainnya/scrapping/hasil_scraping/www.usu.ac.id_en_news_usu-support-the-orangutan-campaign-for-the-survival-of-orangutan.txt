USU support the Orangutan Campaign for the survival of Orangutan
 

Published by

-

Published at

Wednesday, 22 February 2023

Yayasan Kehati with The Body Shop Indonesia and Orangutan Information Center (OIC) cooperate with Biro Penelitian, Pengabdian kepada Masyarakat dan Kerja Sama (BPPKMK) Universitas Sumatera Utara carry out a roadshow called “Peduli Orangutan Tapanuli” at Gedung Pancasila Universitas Sumatera Utara on Thursday 22th February 2023

Yayasan Kehati with The Body Shop Indonesia and Orangutan Information Center (OIC) cooperate with Biro Penelitian, Pengabdian kepada Masyarakat dan Kerja Sama (BPPKMK) Universitas Sumatera Utara carry out a roadshow called “Peduli Orangutan Tapanuli” at Gedung Pancasila Universitas Sumatera Utara on Thursday 22th February 2023, This Roadshow represented by Vice Rector 3 Universitas Sumatera Utara Prof. Dr. apt. Poppy A.Z. Hasibuan, M.Si and director of communication and partnership Yayasan Kehati, Rika Anggaraini.

This Roadshow’s objective is to educate the younger generation, and raised the awareness to rescue the Orangutan Tapanuli. This roadshow will be carried out on 4 Universities in Indonesia where USU will be the first stop for this workshop.

On this roadshow, there will be a talkshow to raise the concern for Orangutan’s habitat and biodiversity, there will also a puzzle game, photo contest, and Instagram with Orangutan Tapanuli’s mascot and other contest as well.
