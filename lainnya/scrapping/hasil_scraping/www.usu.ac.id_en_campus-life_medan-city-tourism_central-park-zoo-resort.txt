Central Park Zoo & Resort

Central Park Zoo and Resort in the Pancur Batu area, Deli Serdang, North Sumatra, is one of the fascinating choices of tourist destinations near Medan City. With the Water Park and Zoo facilities, this place offers water fun and animal activities. This 10-hectare tourist attraction claims that the entire area can be an educational place for visitors. Central Park Zoo and Resort is the perfect family destination to enjoy education and entertainment simultaneously. Please visit their Instagram profile @cpzooandresort for the latest and complete information on operating hours and admission prices.

Other Information

Central Park Zoo and Resort in the Pancur Batu area provides Water Park and Zoo facilities located on 10 hectares of land. Visit their Instagram profile for more information.
