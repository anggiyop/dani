Technological advancements have left us with a thorny and ever-discussed issue: environmental impact. The Industrial Revolution has transformed how humans view machines to help and support human life. But on the other hand, the development of these machines is not matched by solutions to preserve the environment. As we know, global warming and climate change have been the consequences of using machines that are not environmentally friendly.
