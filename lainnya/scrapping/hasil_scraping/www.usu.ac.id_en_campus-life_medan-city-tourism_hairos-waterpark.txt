Hairos Waterpark

Hairos Waterpark Medan, a popular waterpark destination in Medan, North Sumatra, offers exciting water rides for everyone, from swimming pools to water slides, along with facilities such as cafes, bathrooms, and relaxing areas. This place is the perfect choice to spend quality time with family and friends, enjoying the fun and giving a refreshing break to USU students who have gone through a week full of campus courses. For more information on the entrance ticket price and operating hours, please get in touch with Hairos Waterpark Medan through the contact details provided or visit the official website.

Other Information
