Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Master's Program Physics was established in 2002 as a provider of advanced superior education from the Undergraduate Program in Physics.

The main focus of the Master's Program in Physics is on education in the fields of material physics, instrumentation physics and electronics, and medical physics. With the guidance of the best lecturers who are highly dedicated and a conducive and comfortable academic environment, our study program aims to produce superior master candidates with national competitiveness who are able to develop research and scientific work, and implement knowledge in carrying out their duties in their respective fields of work.

Vision of Master's Program in Physics

Become a study program with excellence in the field of material physics, instrumentation & electronics in the national and global order.

Mission of Master's Program in Physics

Quality

Organizing a quality master-level education process to produce graduates who are superior and competitive at the national level in the fields of material physics (polymers, magnetic, electronics, ceramics and superconductive), instrumentation & electronics (sensors and sensor systems, microprocessors).

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
