Aromatheraphy Balm
Aromatheraphy Balm
Product Description

The balm offers a holistic relaxation experience with ginger, galangal, and lemongrass scents. Using carefully selected natural ingredients, this balm combines the virtues of aromatherapy with the traditional uses of balm, providing an optimal sensation of healing and freshness for the body and mind.
