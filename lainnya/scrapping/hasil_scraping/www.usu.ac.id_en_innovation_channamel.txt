Channamel
Dietary Supplement
Product Description

Channamel is a nutritional syrup that combines the goodness of honey and cork fish extract in one product. This syrup is best used as a dietary supplement, especially to fulfill protein needs during growth. In addition, Channamel has a refreshing fruit flavor highly preferred by children. This syrup preparation has been evaluated and tested to ensure good acceptance among the public, especially children.
