The Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology (Fasilkom-TI) of Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) was established in 2011 as a unit responsible for higher education in computer science and information technology at USU. Fasilkom-TI USU offers various programs, including the Undergraduate Program in Computer Science, the Undergraduate Program in Information Technology, the Master's Program in Informatics Engineering, the Master's Program in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, and the Doctoral Program in Computer Science. We consistently develop an educational curriculum that aligns with the rapid development of technology, with a commitment to produce graduates who can compete globally in their fields. Our teaching staff consists of experts with integrity and national teaching certification who are ready to assist students in their academic journey.
