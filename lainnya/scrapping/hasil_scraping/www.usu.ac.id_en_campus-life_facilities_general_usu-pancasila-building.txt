USU Pancasila Building

Often referred to as Pendopo USU, the USU Pancasila Building is a public facility Universitas Sumatera Utara provides to support student activities. The USU Pancasila Building is designed to have a beautiful atmosphere surrounded by shady trees. As the name implies, the building has five corners, reflecting the Pancasila. Open to the public, the USU Pancasila Building accommodates various activities, ranging from literary events, discussions, formal educational activities on campus, and sports events, to serving as a venue for mass COVID-19 vaccination conducted in 2021.
