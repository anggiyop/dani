About Information Systems Center

The Information Systems Center (PSI) of USU is a service unit responsible for the implementation of information and communication technology strategic plans designing, development, and policy implementation at Universitas Sumatera Utara. PSI USU provides various services, including Eduroam (education roaming), computer-based tests (CBT) for both internal and external parties, as well as information and communication technology training services. To ease the effectiveness of ICT activities, PSI USU also facilitates three computer laboratories that the USU academic community and external parties can use.
