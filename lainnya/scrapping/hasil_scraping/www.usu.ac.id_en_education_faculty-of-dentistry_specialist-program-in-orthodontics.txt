Specialist Program in Orthodontics
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Specialist Program in Orthodontics is a specialized study program focusing on dental science research on growth and development disorders of the teeth and face (dental and craniofacial) from childhood to adulthood. Established in 1965, the Specialist Program in Orthodontics consists of several courses, such as Diagnosis, Planning, and Patient Management, that require multidisciplinary treatment, such as cleft lip and palate and herbs in Orthodontics.

The vision of this study program is "Making the FKG USU Specialist Program in Orthodontics the best education program in Western Indonesia and regionally in 2025 in producing qualified and professional Orthodontists who have national and regional competitiveness by mastering the latest science, skills, and technology in the field of Orthodontics, having the ability to improve professionalism and being responsive to global changes". Students who have completed this study program will hold the title of Orthodontic Specialist (Sp.Ort.).

Vision of Specialist Program in Orthodontics

Establishing the FKG USU orthodontic specialist dental education program as the best education program in the regional and western region of Indonesia in 2025 in graduating qualified professional orthodontic specialist dentists with national and regional competitiveness by mastering the latest science, skills, and technology in the field of orthodontics, having the ability to improve professionalism and being responsive and responsive to global changes.

Mission of Specialist Program in Orthodontics

Education

Organize an educational process oriented to the development of science and technology and the needs of society in the field of orthodontics to produce orthodontic specialist dentists who are knowledgeable, skilled, democratic and full of responsibility, virtuous following the professional ethics.

Research and Devotion

Conduct research and community service oriented towards the development of science and technology to solve particular problems in the field of orthodontics scientifically as the primary foundation for growing and fostering abilities, mastering problem-solving methods through thinking skills, scientific reasoning, alternative thinking, and the ability to decide on the best course of action.

Service

Implement specialized services in orthodontics through learning experiences in clinics, attending national and international orthodontic courses, journals, and the internet, and utilizing advances in science and technology appropriately to improve specialized services in orthodontics.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
