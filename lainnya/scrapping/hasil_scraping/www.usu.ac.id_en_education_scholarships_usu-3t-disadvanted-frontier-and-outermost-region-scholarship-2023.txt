USU's 3T (Disadvantaged, Frontier, and Outermost) Region Scholarship 2023
I. GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

A. STUDENT STATUS

Prospective recipients are students of the Universitas Sumatera Utara.
Prospective recipients are students who are still active in the Diploma and Undergraduate programs.

B. DURATION

Scholarships are granted to active students based on the current fiscal year period and are awarded for 1 (one) year or 2 (two) semesters.

C. QUOTA AND GRANT AMOUNT

The quota is 12 students for all Faculties from all Regular pathways. 

The grant amount in 2023 is IDR3,000,000.00 per student per semester for one year.

 

II. SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS

To be a candidate and recipient of the Universitas Sumatera Utara 3T Regional Scholarship, students must fulfill the following requirements:

A. REQUIREMENTS

Undergraduate/Diploma IV/Diploma III is at least in the second semester and at most in the sixth semester for undergraduate and Diploma IV, at least in the second semester and at most in the fourth semester for Diploma III;

Active student and not on leave;

Minimum GPA of 2.75;

Coming from 3T areas (Disadvantaged, Frontier, and Farthest) according to the Presidential Decree Number 63 of 2020;

Coming from an economically disadvantaged family (preferably students who are recorded in the DTKS of the Ministry of Social Affairs);

Not currently receiving scholarships, working and or being in service bond status from other institutions / agencies;

Have non-academic achievements (optional)
