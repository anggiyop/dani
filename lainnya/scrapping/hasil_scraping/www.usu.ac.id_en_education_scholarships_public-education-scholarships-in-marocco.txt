Public Education Scholarships in Morocco

Based on a letter from the Bureau of Cooperation and Public Relations, Ministry of Higher Education, Research and Technology with number 21394/A6/KS.03.01/2023, we can convey the following matters.

Ministry of Foreign Affairs c.q. Directorate of Middle East has received a Diplomatic Note from the Embassy of Morocco in Jakarta no. 306/2023 dated April 12, 2023, stating that the Government of Morocco, through the Moroccan Agency for International Cooperation (Agence Marocaine de la Coopération Internationale/AMCI), has allocated 30 (thirty) general education scholarship quotas in Morocco offered to Indonesian students. 
In connection with this, the Bureau of Cooperation and Public Relations of the Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology intends to disseminate information on the scholarship offer and encourage participation from the Indonesian community. The Diplomatic Note of the Embassy of Morocco in Jakarta, no. 306/2023, and the details of the scholarship offer from AMCI are attached to this letter.

In connection with this information, we ask for your help in disseminating this information to Indonesia's academic community to take advantage of this opportunity. Applicants can send their applications through the Embassy of Morocco in Jakarta before July 30, 2023.
