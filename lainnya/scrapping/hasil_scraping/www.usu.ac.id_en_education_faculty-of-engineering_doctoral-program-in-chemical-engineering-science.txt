Doctoral Program In Chemical Engineering Science
Doctoral Program
Chemical Engineering Science
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Doctoral Program in Chemical Engineering Science at Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) is an advanced study program that examines plant design technology in depth, which can include various types of plants, such as chemical plants, bioprocesses, food factories, and so on. This program is an important step in understanding the complexity of modern industrial processes and how to apply chemical engineering knowledge to optimize production efficiency and sustainability. Established in 2002, this program is committed to providing excellent and research-focused education in an effort to create experts who are able to face technical challenges and innovations in the world of chemical engineering.

It has a vision to become a leading doctoral program that is able to produce graduates with superior research products in the field of environmentally sound process technology and can compete at the global level. Graduates of the USU Doctoral Program in Chemical Engineering Science are expected to have an in-depth understanding of environmentally friendly plant design technology, as well as the ability to apply this knowledge in addressing global challenges related to sustainability issues, waste management, and industrial process efficiency. Students who have completed this study program will hold a Doctoral degree (Dr.).

Vision of Doctoral Program in Chemical Engineering Science

To become a leading doctoral program that produces graduates with excellent research outcomes in environmentally conscious process technology and capable of competing at the global level.

Mission of Doctoral Program in Chemical Engineering Science

Expertise

To enhance theoretical, conceptual, and experimental expertise in environmentally conscious process technology in order to produce high-quality research published in international journals.

Collaboration

To establish collaborations with industry and government to support and accelerate students' research activities.

Community Service

To provide services to the community, particularly in the process industry sector.

Lifelong Learning

To foster a lifelong learning culture and keep pace with advancements in science and technology.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
