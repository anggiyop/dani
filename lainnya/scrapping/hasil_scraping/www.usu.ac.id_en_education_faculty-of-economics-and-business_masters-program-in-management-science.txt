Master Program
Management Science
Universitas Sumatera Utara

As one of the leading master's study programs in North Sumatra, the Master's Program in Management Science at Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) provides advanced studies for undergraduate management graduates.

By offering concentrations in 3human resource management, financial management, and marketing management, the program develops a curriculum and adopts a learning process to help students create job opportunities, entrepreneurship, and innovation to compete in the global market. Supported by competent teaching staff highly committed to educating students, the Master Program in Management Science has produced graduates with diverse profiles, including business consultants, entrepreneurs, researchers, corporate analysts, and educators.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
