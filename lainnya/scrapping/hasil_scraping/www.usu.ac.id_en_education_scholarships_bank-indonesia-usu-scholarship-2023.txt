Bank Indonesia USU Scholarship 2023
Bank Indonesia USU Scholarship 2023

SELECTION PHASE

Phase 1 Selection (administration and general requirements)
Stage 2 selection (interview by Bank Indonesia)
Announcement of Bank Indonesia scholarship awardees in 2023
Formation of GenBI 2023 Organization
Development of competence, knowledge, interests and talents, concern, responsibility, and social solidarity movements to carry out various social activities.

REQUIREMENTS

Regular undergraduate students have completed at least 3 (three) semesters or have taken 40 (forty) credit hours (preferably the batches of 2020 and 2021);
Each student receives a scholarship for a maximum of only 4 (four) semesters as long as they meet the predetermined criteria;
When determined as a scholarship recipient, the maximum age is 23 or not yet 24 years old;
Not currently receiving scholarships, working and/or being in official bond status from other institutions/agencies;
Have experience in carrying out social activities that have a beneficial impact on society;
Willing to play an active role, manage and develop Generasi Baru Indonesia (GenBI) and participate in all activities organized by Bank Indonesia.
Have a minimum Cumulative Grade Point Average (GPA) of 3.00 (scale 4);
Preferably from an economically disadvantaged background (pre-prosperous);
Make a personal resume and motivation letter in Bahasa Indonesia;
