Master of Notary FH USU Successfully Holds Alumni Gathering Through Various Activities
 

Published by

Renny Julia Harahap

Published at

Tuesday, 05 November 2024

“I hope that alumni can join hands to advance FH USU as a whole and make the best contribution to the nation and state,” concluded the Head of the Master of Kenotariatan Program at FH USU.

HUMAS USU - The Master Program in Notary of the Faculty of Law (FH), Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU), held its second Alumni Gathering on Saturday (2/11/2024) at the FH USU Notary Building.

 

The Rector of USU, Prof. Dr. Muryanto Amin S.Sos., M.Si. in his speech emphasized the important role of alumni in the development of a study program can pour a lot of impact and benefits. Alumni are considered capable of contributing to the place where they received their education in the past through various positive activities.

 

In the midst of the ranking process, alumni also play an active role in participating. The USU Rector advised that alumni of the Master Program in Notary FH USU can produce positive activities which are expected to benefit the university in the future.

 

“Keep the relationship and maintain communication between alumni and the university because these two things can give us a way out if there are many problems that occur,” said the USU Rector.

 

Currently, there are more than 2,200 alumni of the USU Faculty of Law Master Program in Notary in various provinces in Indonesia. The Alumni Gathering, according to the Head of the FH USU Master Program in Notary, Prof. Dr. Hasim Purba SH, M.Hum, is a moment to strengthen the relationship between the leadership of the Master Program in Notary, FH USU, and alumni.

 

In addition, activities such as Book Fair, community service, legal counseling in the field of land in several villages and villages in Deli Serdang Regency, social services, free health services to blood donors were also carried out.

 

“I hope that alumni can join hands to advance FH USU as a whole and make the best contribution to the nation and state,” concluded the Head of the Master of Kenotariatan FH USU.

 

Dr. Kiki Handoko Sembiring, S.H., M.Kn., as Head of IKA MKN FH USU, hopes that the implementation of this series of alumni gathering activities can strengthen ties and continue to raise the spirit of building the study program to be better in the future.
