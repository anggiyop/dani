Katamso Land

Katamso Land, a vibrant market in Medan, presents an unforgettable culinary and entertainment experience. Upon entering the area, visitors are greeted by around 50 food and beverage stalls that are organized and tempting. The delicacy of various dishes is ready to spoil your tongue in every corner. In addition to culinary delights, a variety of amusement rides also enrich the experience at Katamso Land. Walking across the food court, you will find a variety of exciting rides such as kora-kora and Ferris wheel. Your adrenaline is guaranteed to be spurred and provide unforgettable excitement. But that's not all. The true magic is in the Festival of Lights, the main attraction here. As dusk falls, the atmosphere changes with decorative lights illuminating Katamso Land. The festival creates a superb, colorful, and eye-catching scene. Capturing the beautiful moments here through photographs will be an unforgettable memory. Enjoying the beauty of this festival costs an affordable 20,000 rupiah per person. However, for children under 120 cm tall, this fun can be enjoyed for free. A trip that not only satisfies the stomach but also fills the heart with happiness and the unforgettable magic of lights at Katamso Land.

Other Information

Katamso Land is considered as Medan's contemporary market with around 50 food and beverage stalls, as well as various amusement rides.
