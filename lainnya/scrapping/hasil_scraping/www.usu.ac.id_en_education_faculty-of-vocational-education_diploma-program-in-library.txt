Diploma Program In Library
Diploma Program
Library
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Diploma Program in Library at the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) was established in 1985. It is a vocational study program that provides education in library science. Currently, this program has received Very Good (A) accreditation.

To prepare students to become professionals, we carry out practicum activities at the USU Library as part of our learning. The USU Diploma Program in Library is committed to producing graduates who are competent in documentation and information and who become expert consultants in library automation and library management for information centers or libraries in North Sumatra and other regions in Indonesia.

Vision of Diploma Program in Library

To become a leading study program in the Western Region of Indonesia that produces skilled vocational personnel in the library sector who are able to compete at the regional and national levels.

Mission of Diploma Program in Library

Education

Organize vocational education in the library field using learning methods that apply information and communication technology.

Research

Carry out research in libraries to improve academic quality and knowledge development.

Community Service

Carry out community service related to the library field that is beneficial for changing the lives of the wider community.

Competent Graduate

Produce graduates who have scientific competence in the library field with solid competitiveness and behave with ethical and intellectual behavior.

Academic Ambience

Improve the academic atmosphere conducive to the implementation of the tridarma of higher education.

Collaboration

Establish cooperation with other institutions in the library field to develop education, research, and community service.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
