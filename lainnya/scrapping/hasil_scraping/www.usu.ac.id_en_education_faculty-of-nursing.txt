The Faculty of Nursing (FKep) at the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) has been carrying out its mission since 1991 to produce the best graduates in the field of nursing. The Faculty offers three educational programs, including the Undergraduate Program in Nursing, Professional Nursing, and Master's Program in Nursing, all of which are well accredited. Focusing on the study of providing services to patients to achieve optimal health, the Faculty of Nursing is managed by experienced experts in the fields of health and nursing and supported by adequate facilities and an integrated curriculum. We are determined to guide our students to become competent nurses who can collaborate with other health workers to improve the health of the Indonesian people. Graduates of the Faculty of Nursing have various career opportunities, including as health service managers in various health institutions in Indonesia.
