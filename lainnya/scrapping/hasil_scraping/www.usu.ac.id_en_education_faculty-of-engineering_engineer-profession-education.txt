Engineer Profession Education
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Professional Engineer Education is an advanced level education program intended for undergraduate in engineering graduates to obtain their professional certificate. This study program aims to produce engineers who are competent, ethical, innovative, and able to collaborate, preparing them to take a leading role in various engineering sectors.

The vision of the Professional Engineer Education Program is to become an engineering study program that excels and competes globally in terms of scientific quality and professional engineering skills. Graduates from this program have access to a wide range of sectors, including technology, manufacturing, construction, energy, transportation, automation, and software development and information technology. They also have opportunities in innovative sectors such as technology, artificial intelligence, and more. Students who complete this professional program will be awarded the title of Engineer (Ir.) and are ready to contribute to the development of technology and industry with their skills and expertise.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
