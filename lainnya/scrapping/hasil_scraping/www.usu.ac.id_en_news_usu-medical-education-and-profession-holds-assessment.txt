PR USU - The Faculty of Medicine (FK) Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) held an Offline Field Assessment for the Education and Professional Doctoral Study Program. The event took place in the USU Medical Faculty Hall on Monday, (06/06/2022).

On that occasion, USU Rector Dr. Muryanto Amin, S.Sos., M.Sc. in his speech said that this activity was part of the accreditation activities for the Education and Professional Doctors Study Program and lasted for 2 days.

"Offline Field Assessment Activities, which started today and were carried out for two days, as part of the accreditation activities for the US Medical Faculty and Medical Education Study Programs and Profession," said Muryanto Amin.

The Rector also stated that a good accreditation assessment of USU's Education and Medical Profession Study Program is expected to increase further the enthusiasm of the lecturers and students in the study program to produce various achievements in both academic and non-academic fields. And can contribute by dedicating their capabilities in the human aspect.

"No less important, to contribute and dedicate the capabilities and expertise possessed to the humanitarian aspect, particularly helping improve public health as a whole," he said.

He said that this activity could provide good results for the accreditation of USU's Education and Medical Profession Study Program to increase self-confidence and quality of service to students and collaborating partners both in academic and non-academic spheres.

"We hope that this activity will give good results for the accreditation of USU's Medical and Professional Education Study Programs so that it can further increase self-confidence and the quality of academic and non-academic services to all students and collaborating partners," said the Rector.

The Rector hopes that after this assessment, the management of the Medical Education and Profession study program can follow the assessment standards. And now, it is making continuous improvements to provide educational services that are neatly arranged.

"The management of the study program is following assessment standards and continues to be carried out. We are currently making continuous improvements for education services that are neatly recorded," he said.

For information, the assessment carried out today is a continuation of the online visitation that has been carried out previously.
