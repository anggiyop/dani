The Faculty of Law (FH) of Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) is one of the best and most favored law faculties in Indonesia, ranking 11th as the Best Faculty of Law according to THE Asia University Rankings 2022. Established in 1996, this faculty is the second faculty at USU and is accredited Very Good (A), never failing to produce competent graduates scattered throughout Indonesia. Filled with highly experienced and dedicated lecturers, the USU Faculty of Law's mission is to produce highly qualified and ethically motivated legal professionals who are knowledgeable, innovative, and responsive to legal needs. The USU Faculty of Law also provides a wide range of study programs covering business law, criminal law, civil law, international law, and others, with high-quality education, personalized attention, and interactive learning experiences. We invite students and prospective students to join us on this journey of developing and enhancing intellectual aspects and legal awareness in Indonesian society.

Mission of Faculty of Law

Education

To organize competency-based legal education and research that emphasizes problem solving in accordance with the trends in national and international legal developments trends.

Research
