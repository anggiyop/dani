Djarum Plus Scholarship 2023
Djarum Plus Scholarship 2023

Applicant Requirements

Currently pursuing an undergraduate or diploma 4 degree in the fourth semester from all majors.
Minimum GPA of 3.00 in the third semester, and can maintain a minimum GPA of 3.00 until the end of the fourth semester.
Actively participate in organizational activities both on and off campus
Not currently receiving scholarships from other parties.

Student at Djarum Plus Scholarship Program University Partners

 

Required Documents

PDF printout of the online registration form.
One copy of a 4 x 6 cm colored photo wearing an alma mater jacket.
Photocopy of Student Identity Card.
Academic Transcript up to the third semester that has been legalized by the campus authority.
Copy of letter of statement or certificate of being active in organizations and/or certificates of achievement.
A certificate from the Rectorate that is not currently receiving scholarships from other parties (for example, for 2023 applicants, is from September 1, 2023 – August 31, 2024).
