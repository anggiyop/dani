SancitaCov
Sabun Cair Cuci Tangan
Product Description

SancitaCov is a liquid hand soap with two distinctive aroma variants: apple fruit and green tea leaves. With a composition consisting of Comperland CDE, SLS, Sanisol, Texapon, NaCl, coloring, fragrance, distilled water, and HPMC, this product effectively cleans hands of germs. Packaged in a 100 mL bottle, SancitaCov provides practical and efficient protection to maintain hand hygiene wherever we are.
