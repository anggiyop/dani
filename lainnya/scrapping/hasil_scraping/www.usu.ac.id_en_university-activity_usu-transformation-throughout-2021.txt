USU Transformation Throughout 2021
 

Published by

Bambang Riyanto

Published at

Thursday, 06 January 2022

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU), under the leadership of Rector Dr. Muryanto Amin and his staff, has transformed to achieve various achievements and work programs throughout 2021. Targets that are in line with the objectives of the USU Strategic Plan 2020-2024 in realizing the internationalization of USU, the USU Rector's Work Program 2021-2026, and penetrating the 500 QS WUR ranking have been carried out. One of them is the “Exist Program,” which is applied in general policy for five years, namely focusing on Excellent Human Integrity, Smart Friendly and Green Campus, Global Society Impact, Good University Governance, and Sustainable Funding.
