USU Research Institution (LP) signs agreement on 2021 Palm Grand Research Fund Support
 

Published by

Bambang Riyanto

Published at

Wednesday, 25 August 2021

USU Public Relations: The Head of the Research Institute of the Universitas Sumatera Utara, Prof. Dr. Robert Sibarani, MS, signed the 2021 Palm Oil Grant Research Fund Support Agreement online, Thursday (8/19/2021).

This year, the Universitas Sumatera Utara received a title of funding with a Research Team consisting of Prof. Dr. Eng Ir. Irvan, MSi, from USU as the Head of Researcher with research members Dr. Eng Taufiq Bin Nur, ST, M Eng (USU), Muhammad Sigit Cahyono, ST. , M Eng (University of Proklamasi 45), Muhammad Noviansyah Aridito, SPd, MSc (University of Proklamasi 45), withthe research title “Biogas Purification Process from Palm Oil Mill Effluent (POME) with NaOH-Sponge Iron Absorption System and Conversion to Hydrogen without Emissions "This research has been approved for funding for 2 years by BPDPKS.

Also attending the event were Prof. Dr. Eng Ir Irvan, MSi, as the Chief Researcher and Dr. Eng Taufiq Bin Nur, ST, M Eng, as Research Member and Syafriandy, ST, staff of the USU Research Institute.Funding for this research was sourced from the Palm Oil Plantation Fund Management Agency (BPDPKS).(*/©ULC)
