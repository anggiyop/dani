Search Scholarship

If you are looking for scholarships, the University offers various financial aid programs for students. These scholarships can be awarded based on academic achievement, financial need, or non-academic accomplishments, helping you complete your studies more easily.

Adaro Foundation Scholarship 2025

The Adaro Foundation 2025 Scholarship is open from May 1-21, 2025 for active undergraduate & postgraduate students with a minimum GPA of 3.00, especially those from underprivileged families.

05 May 2025

Pelindo Prestasi Scholarship 2025

PT Pelabuhan Indonesia (Persero), known as Pelindo, is one of Indonesia’s Top 10 State-Owned Enterprises (SOEs) engaged in port services. With great pride, Pelindo opens opportunities for outstanding students to receive the Pelindo Prestasi Scholarship as a contribution to the development of exceptional human resources in Indonesia.

27 March 2025

Karya Salemba Empat Scolarship 2025

The undergraduate scholarship program helps students with financial needs and encourages integrity, nationality, and love for the country. An allowance of Rp.750,000/month for 1 year, extendable, and the opportunity to participate in KSE coaching programs such as Face-to-Face, Leadership Program, Seminar, Workshop, and Coaching.

20 March 2025

Achievement Scholarship Year 2025

The Achievement Scholarship 2025 is a scholarship program for active students of Universitas Sumatera Utara from all admission pathways with a minimum GPA of 3.00. This scholarship does not require national or international achievements but having them can be an advantage. A quota of 250 students will receive Rp 2,100,000 per semester for one year.

10 March 2025

Pertamina Sobat Bumi Scholarship 2025

The Sobat Bumi Scholarship Program 2025 is a scholarship awarded to undergraduate students who excel academically, are actively involved in organizations or social-community activities, demonstrate environmental awareness, and are enrolled at Pertamina Foundation partner universities, including Universitas Sumatera Utara (under the Regular Sobat Bumi Scholarship category).

10 March 2025
