Wisata Merci

Wisata Merci is one of the exciting tourist attractions in Medan City, North Sumatra. This place provides various facilities that can be enjoyed by visitors. Visitors can see a Dutch-style cattle farm, explore replicas of European buildings, and enjoy multiple rides in the water boom as the main attraction. Merci Tourism is perfect for adventurers who seek challenges in water rides such as the tornado slide or black hole slide. In addition, visitors can also feel the thrill of adrenaline by sliding through a dark tunnel and spinning around at high speed, which will undoubtedly make the heart beat faster. For more information on admission prices and operating hours, you can visit their official Instagram account @wisatamerci.
