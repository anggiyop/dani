About Basket USU Student Activity Unit

Basket USU Student Activity Unit provides a platform for students who have an interest and talent in basketball. This student activity unit was established to develop skills, foster the spirit of students, and promote athletic integrity, particularly in basketball. The members of this student activity unit regularly engage in training sessions, internal campus competitions, and external competitions against other universities in Indonesia. The official presence of the Basket USU Student Activity Unit is a commitment from USU to achieve remarkable achievements for Universitas Sumatera Utara and support the physical well-being of USU students.
