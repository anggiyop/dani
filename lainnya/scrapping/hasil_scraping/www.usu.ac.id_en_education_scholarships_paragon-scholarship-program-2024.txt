Paragon Scholarship Program 2024
Paragon Scholarship Program 2024

Program Timeline

Online Registration: June 28 – July 29, 2024
Selection Process: July – September 2024
Scholarship Announcement: September 2024
Scholarship Program: September 2024 – April 2026

 

Recipient Qualifications

Active students of Universitas Sumatera Utara enrolled in Diploma III (3rd semester) or Diploma IV/S1 (5th semester) programs.
Minimum GPA of 3.30 and demonstrated leadership skills through active participation in campus activities.
Not currently receiving other scholarships and committed to not accepting any other scholarships during the program.
Committed to participating in development programs.

 

Required Documents

Curriculum Vitae (ATS format).
Academic transcript for semesters 1–4.
Scanned Identity Card (KTP).
Scanned Student ID Card (KTM).
Scanned Family Card (KK).
Scanned bank account details and a screenshot of mobile banking.
Recommendation letter confirming non-receipt of other scholarships.
Certificates of academic and non-academic achievements.

 

Scholarship Benefits

Funding support.
Development programs.
Community project opportunities.
Access to the Paragon EduLeads Ecosystem.
Opportunity to join ParagonCorp.
