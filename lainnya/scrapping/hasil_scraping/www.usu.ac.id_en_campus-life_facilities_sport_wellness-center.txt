Wellness Center

USU Wellness Center is ideal for students and the general public to practice taekwondo skills. Located in the Cikal USU area, the USU Wellness Center has complete facilities and a convenient area. The USU Wellness Center also organizes various local and national taekwondo events and competitions, making it a popular spot for campus taekwondo athletes and enthusiasts. With experienced instructors and a supportive environment, the USU Wellness Center is an excellent place for USU students to start or develop their taekwondo skills.
