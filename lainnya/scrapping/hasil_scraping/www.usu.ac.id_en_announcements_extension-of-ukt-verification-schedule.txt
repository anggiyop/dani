Number: 11874/UN5.1.R2/KEU/2024

Subject: Request for Extension of Verification Schedule

To:

1. Vice Rector for Academic, Student Affairs, and Alumni;

2. Director of the Directorate of Education Development;

3. Head of the Academic Bureau;

4. Head of the Information System Center; and

5. Head of the Public Relations and Protocol Office.

Universitas Sumatera Utara

Medan

Concerning the need to develop the website http://uktdatareg.usu.ad.id/ in accordance with the verification and validation guidelines for the imposition of Single Tuition Fees (UKT), we hereby request an extension of the submission of the results of the verification of complete UKT data and the issuance of virtual accounts for new students accepted through the SNBP route until April 29, 2024.

We also request that the permission granted be accompanied by an announcement of changes to the schedule to announce the results of the verification of complete UKT data and the issuance of virtual accounts to the relevant students.

Thus, we convey our gratitude for your attention.

Signed electronically by:

Vice Rector II

#TTE

Muhammad Arifin Nasution
