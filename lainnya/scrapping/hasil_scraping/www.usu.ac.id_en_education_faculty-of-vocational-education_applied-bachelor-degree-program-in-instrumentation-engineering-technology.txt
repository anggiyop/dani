Applied Bachelor's Degree Program In Instrumentation Engineering Technology
Applied Undergraduate Program
Instrumentation Engineering Technology
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Applied Bachelor's Degree Program in Instrumentation Engineering Technology at the Faculty of Vocational Education, Universitas Sumatera Utara, is designed to equip students with practical knowledge and skills in instrumentation and automation. The curriculum combines theory and practice, and students will learn measurement techniques, calibration, and control system programming.

The focus of the program is on developing the technical competencies and analytical skills needed to face the challenges of modern industry. Through a project-based learning approach and collaboration with industry, students will gain relevant hands-on experience.

Vision of Applied Bachelor's Degree Program in Instrumentation Engineering Technology

Producing applied graduates who are competent and professional, and becoming an excellent study program in the field of instrumentation engineering technology at the national and international levels.

Mission of Applied Bachelor's Degree Program in Instrumentation Engineering Technology

Education

Organizing quality vocational education in the field of instrumentation based on international standard engineering technology to produce competent and professional graduates.

Research

Carry out engineering technology-based research to encourage innovative instrumentation research, and contribute to the advancement of science and technology at the national and international levels.

Community Service

Carrying out community service by applying the results of research work into appropriate products and technology for the community.

Collaboration

Establishing cooperation with related parties in the fields of education, research, and community service in the field of instrumentation engineering technology at the national and international levels.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
