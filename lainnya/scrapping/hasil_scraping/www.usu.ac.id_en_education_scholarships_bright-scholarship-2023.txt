BRIGHT Scholarship 2023
BRIGHT Scholarship 2023

Who can apply?

First-semester undergraduate (S1/D4) students from State Universities (specifically Universitas Sumatera Utara, open to males)
Have a drive for achievement
Have organizational experience
Able to read the Quran
Financially restrained

What are the benefits?

Payment of Tuition Fees
Stipend
Dormitory facilities
National & international networking
Self-development program
Community empowerment experience
Student exchange and international competitions support

What are the requirements?

Completing the registration form
Creating a personal statement
Scanning or taking a photo of the tuition fee payment receipt
Scanning or taking a photo of the Family Card (KK) or a letter confirming family composition
Scanning or taking a photo of one of the following documents: a statement of parents' or guardians' income, Family Welfare Card (KKS), participant of the Program Keluarga Harapan (PKH), or a letter confirming financially restrained
Taking a photo of the front view of the house
Scanning or taking a photo of certificates or awards (if available)
