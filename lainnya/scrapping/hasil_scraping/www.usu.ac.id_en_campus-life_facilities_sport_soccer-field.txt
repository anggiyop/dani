Soccer Field

The USU Soccer Field located on the USU 1st entrance area, precisely on Dr. A Sofyan Street, is an alternative to the USU Mini Stadium on the USU 4th entrance area. This soccer field has a standard size and is complete with facilities such as goalposts, net support poles, and toilets. This soccer field is often used for various sports activities and internal and interlocal competitions by students and the general public. With good field conditions, soccer players can play comfortably and safely according to standards.
