Bakti BCA Scholarship Program 2025
Bakti BCA Scholarship Program 2025

Scholarship Timeline

Candidate Registration: September 9 - October 31, 2024
Registration is done via a data collection link. BCA will send a list of registered candidates for verification by the student affairs division.
Administrative Selection: November 1 - 5, 2024
Candidates who pass administrative selection will receive invitations for the next test via their registered email.
Assessment Test (Online) & Pre-Recorded Interview: November 12 - 14, 2024
Estimated Announcement: December 20, 2024
Development and Training Program: January - December 2025

 

Scholarship Benefits

Education Funding:
Monthly stipends and tuition fee assistance.
Bakti Champions Program:
A one-year personal development program.

 

General Eligibility Criteria

Indonesian citizen.
Active undergraduate (S1) students in their 3rd semester from Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) or designated public universities.
Not currently receiving other similar scholarships.
Maximum age of 22 years and unmarried.
Demonstrates social awareness and a strong commitment to learning.
Actively involved in committees or organizations (except for the "Non-Academic Achievement" scholarship category).
Not working full-time and not under a service bond with any other institution/foundation.
Committed to participating in all program activities for one year.
Meets the "Specific Criteria" of one of the BCA Bakti Scholarship categories.
Passes all stages of the BCA Bakti Scholarship selection process.

 

Scholarship Categories and Specific Criteria

Financial Aid Category

Offered to high-achieving students who face financial difficulties in completing their education or are at risk of dropping out.

Minimum GPA of 3.00 (Semesters 1-2).
Submit a recommendation letter certifying financial difficulty from the local RT/RW (neighborhood head).
Academic Achievement Category

Offered to students with academic excellence, such as competition/academic awards, organizational/committee experience, recognition/publications, or other academic achievements.

Minimum GPA of 3.25 (Semesters 1-2).
Submit supporting documents for academic achievements.
Non-Academic Achievement Category

Offered to students with talents and non-academic achievements, such as awards in sports, arts, culture, environment, or humanitarian activities with a positive impact on society.

No minimum GPA requirement.
Submit supporting certificates/documents for non-academic achievements and a talent video.
