Healthy Center

Welcome to the USU Healthy Center, a sports facility open to the public at the Faculty of Public Health, Universitas Sumatera Utara. We provide various sports facilities that will help you maintain your fitness and health in a fun and affordable way. For USU lecturers and staff, access to the Healthy Center is free. USU students can enjoy this facility for IDR5,000 per hour, while the fee is only IDR10,000 per hour for the general public. If you want to enjoy the benefits of exercising regularly, we also provide a monthly membership package for only IDR100,000. Join the USU Healthy Center and take care of your health in a fun and affordable way.
