BAZNAS Scholarship 2023
BAZNAS Scholarship 2023

Types of Scholarships Offered

Prioritized Major Category: Computer, professional (medicine, psychology, nursing, pharmacy), Engineering, Accounting, Statistics, Zakat and Waqf Management, Communication, Economics, and Education,  
Achievement Category: Having national and/or international achievements (preferably those that have been recorded at the National Achievement Center (Puspresnas Kemdikbud),  
Entrepreneur Category: Have a personal business that has been running for at least 6 months, and  
Activist Category: Active as an administrator of student organizations, at least at the department level or youth/community organizations.

Students interested in applying for BAZNAS scholarships can choose one of the 4 categories above. Related documents that must be completed are General Requirements Documents and Special Documents (according to the category selected)

 

Special Required Documents

Prioritized Major Category

Recommendation letter from the faculty signed by the Vice-Dean I

Achievement Category

Certificate and documentation of the awarding session 

Entrepreneur Category

Business license or business statement from the village.

Activist Category

Statement letter of the inauguration of organization administrator and/or statement letter of being active in the organization
