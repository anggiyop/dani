DECREE OF THE RECTOR OFUNIVERSITAS SUMATERA UTARA

NUMBER 312/UN5.1.R/SK/KEU/2024 NUMBER  /UN5.1.R/SK/KEU/2024

 

CONCERNING

 

CHANGE OF THE DECREE OF THE RECTOR OF UNIVERSITAS SUMATERA UTARA NUMBER 526/UN5.1.R/SK/KEU/2023

 

CONCERNING 

THE AMOUNT OF ADMINISTRATIVE FEE FOR POSTPONEMENT OF ACADEMIC ACTIVITIES (PKA) IN UNIVERSITAS SUMATERA UTARA

 

RECTOR OF UNIVERSITAS SUMATERA UTARA

 

Considering:

a. that based on the provisions of Article 76 paragraph (3) of Law Number 12 of 2012 concerning Higher Education, higher education institutions or higher education organizers receive payments that students bear to finance their studies according to the ability of the student, the student's parents, or the party financing it;

b. that in order to implement the Regulation of the Minister of Education and Culture of the Republic of Indonesia concerning the Standard Unit of Operational Costs of Higher Education at State Universities within the Ministry of Education and Culture;

c. that based on the considerations as referred to in letters a and b, it is necessary to stipulate the Decree of the Rector of Universitas Sumatera Utara concerning Amendments to the Decree of the Rector of the Universitas Sumatera Utara Number 526/UN5.1.R/SK/KEU/2023 concerning the Amount of Administrative Costs for Postponement of Academic Activities (PKA) within Universitas Sumatera Utara

 

Remembering:

Law Number 20 of 2003 concerning the National Education System (State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia of 2003 Number 78, Supplement to the State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia Number 4301);

Law Number 12 of 2012 concerning Higher Education (State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia of 2012 Number 158, Supplement to the State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia Number 5336); 

Government Regulation Number 4 of 2014 concerning the Implementation of Higher Education and Management of Higher Education Institutions (State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia of 2014 Number 16, Supplement to the State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia Number 5500); 

Government Regulation Number 16 of 2014 concerning the Statutes of Universitas Sumatera Utara (State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia of 2014 Number 42, Supplement to the State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia Number 5510); 

Government Regulation Number 26 of 2015 concerning the Form and Funding Mechanism of State Universities as Legal Entities as amended by Government Regulation Number 8 of 2020 concerning Amendments to Government Regulation Number 26 of 2015 concerning the Form and Funding Mechanism of State Universities as Legal Entities (State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia Number 28 of 2020, Supplement to the State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia Number 6460); 

Regulation of the Minister of Education and Culture of the Republic of Indonesia Number 25 of 2020 concerning Standard Operating Costs for Higher Education at State Universities within the Ministry of Education and Culture (State Gazette of the Republic of Indonesia Number 642 of 2020); 

Decree of the Minister of Education and Culture of the Republic of Indonesia Number 81/E/KPT/2020 concerning Single Tuition Fees at State Universities in the Form of Universities and Institutes; 

Regulation of the Rector of Universitas Sumatera Utara Number 12 of 2022 concerning Financial Management of Universitas Sumatera Utara; and 

Decree of the Rector of Universitas Sumatera Utara Number 526/UN5.1.R/SK/KEU/2023 Concerning the Amount of Administrative Fees for Postponement of Academic Activities (PKA) within Universitas Sumatera Utara

 

DECIDES:

 

To stipulate:

 

DECREE OF THE RECTOR OF UNIVERSITAS SUMATERA UTARA CONCERNING CHANGES TO THE DECREE OF THE RECTOR OF UNIVERSITAS SUMATERA UTARA NUMBER 526/UN5.1.R/SK/KEU/2023 CONCERNING THE AMOUNT OF ADMINISTRATION FEE FOR POSTPONEMENT OF ACADEMIC ACTIVITIES (PKA) IN UNIVERSITAS SUMATERA UTARA

 

FIRST: To amend the First Dictum of the Decree of the Rector of Universitas Sumatera Utara Number 526/UN5.1.R/SK/KEU/2023 concerning the Amount of Administrative Fee for Postponement of Academic Activities (PKA) in Universitas Sumatera Utara;

 

SECOND: Changes to the Decree of the Rector of Universitas Sumatera Utara, as stated in the FIRST DICTUM, are made to the amount of administrative costs for Postponement of Academic Activities (PKA) per semester by eliminating costs for the Diploma Three/Vocational Program and Undergraduate Program levels;

 

THIRD: This Decree shall come into effect from submitting Active Lectures Again (AKK) for the Even Semester of the 2023/2024 Academic Year.

 

Established in Medan

On January 24, 2024

RECTOR,
