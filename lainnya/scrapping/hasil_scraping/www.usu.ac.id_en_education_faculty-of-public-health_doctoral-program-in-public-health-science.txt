Doctoral Program
Public Health Science
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Doctoral Program in Public Health Science of Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) is the highest level of education in public health science at the Faculty of Public Health.

This program is a continuation of the Master's Program in Public Health Science, which aims to develop independent scientific research skills to make a meaningful contribution to the field of public health science. Our teaching staff consists of nationally experienced public health experts. We are committed to guiding students to become professional public health experts in developing health management concepts and research at both national and international levels. Our graduates are expected to be able to carry out their responsibilities as health experts, health researchers, or health promoters with high professionalism and dedication.

Vision of Doctoral Program in Public Health Science

To become an Internationally Standardized Doctoral Study Program in Developing Public Health Science in Tropical Regions.

Mission of Doctoral Program in Public Health Science

Technology-Based Education

To organize doctoral education that is of quality, responsive, innovative, independent, and based on modern learning technology.

Scientific Research

To organize scientific research to develop public health science in tropical regions and publish them in reputable international journals.

Community Service

To organize responsive and innovative community service to enhance the quality of public health in tropical regions.

Expanding Cooperation

To expand cooperation networks with national and international universities, governments, industries, professions, and organizations to support the quality of education, research, and community service.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
