About Lentera Nataraja Student Activity Unit

The Lentera Nataraja Student Activity Unit (UKM Lentera Nataraja) at USU is a platform for students who have an interest and talent in Indonesian traditional dance. This organization was established to preserve the rich cultural heritage of the archipelago while fostering creativity and enhancing students’ skills in various regional dances. Regular activities of this UKM include scheduled dance training, artistic performances at campus events, and participation in national and international dance competitions and festivals. The presence of UKM Lentera Nataraja USU reflects Universitas Sumatera Utara’s commitment to preserving Indonesian cultural arts and instilling pride in students for their nation’s heritage. Students interested in joining can visit the official Instagram account of UKM Lentera Nataraja USU for more information.
