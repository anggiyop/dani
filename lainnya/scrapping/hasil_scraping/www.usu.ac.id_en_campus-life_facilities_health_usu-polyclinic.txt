USU Polyclinic

Apart from being provided for USU students, lecturers, and staff, the USU Polyclinic also serves the general public. The doctors assigned to the USU Polyclinic are lecturers at the USU Faculty of Medicine, consisting of general practitioners and specialists. In addition, the medical staff assigned to the USU Polyclinic are dentists, nurses, and paramedics, who are USU staff. Health service facilities provided by the USU Polyclinic include general and dental medicine, nutrition, counseling, specialty services, laboratories, and pharmacies.
