USU Language Center

The Language Center is one of the USU facilities located behind the USU Faculty of Forestry. The Language Center functions as an institution that has facilities such as training, courses, translation services, and other language-related services. The institution, which was established in 1986, was originally part of the USU Faculty of Literature. The USU Language Center also provides language tests in English and Japanese.
