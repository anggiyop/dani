Build friendships with students from various regions
Opportunity to study at a different campus

Requirements Pertukaran Mahasiswa Merdeka

For Universities

The university that sends its students is under the coordination of the Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology.

For Students

Active students in D-3, D-4, or S-1 programs registered in the Higher Education Database (PDDikti).
At least in the 2nd semester at the time of registration.
Minimum GPA of 2.8 on a scale of 4 at the time of the PMM program registration. Students must attach the latest academic transcript during the registration process.
Must be an Indonesian citizen (WNI) and have a Population Identification Number (NIK). Students must attach scanned identification documents, such as their ID card (KTP) or Family Card (Kartu Keluarga), during registration.
Create a Statement of Absolute Responsibility (SPTJM) in accordance with the provided template on the registration page.
Have received at least the third dose of COVID-19 vaccination. Please attach a screenshot of the COVID-19 vaccination history during registration.
Physically healthy, proven by attaching a Health Certificate from an authorized healthcare facility.
Have an active bank account at BRI or BSI. Please attach a scanned copy of the student's savings book during registration.
Priority will be given to those who have BPJS Kesehatan or other health insurance that covers inpatient and outpatient care under the student's name.
