Dentistry Professional Study Program
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Dentistry Professional Study Program is an educational program that aims to prepare students to develop health and dental science and find solutions to the health problems of patients, communities, and health service systems. The program provides vital provisions in clinical skills, business management, and problem-solving so that graduates can positively contribute to the community's oral health and develop the dental profession.

The vision of this study program is "In 2025, the Dentistry Professional Study Program will become a leading study program in the fields of natural resources, technology, and art that has global competitiveness, professionalism, and entrepreneurship." The USU Dentistry Professional Study Program graduates are expected to have a high professional orientation, making them experts who can provide dental health services with international standards. Students who have completed this study program will hold the title Dentist (drg.).
