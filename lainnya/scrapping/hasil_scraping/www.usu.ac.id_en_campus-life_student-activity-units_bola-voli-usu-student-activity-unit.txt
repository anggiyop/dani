About Bola Voli USU Student Activity Unit

The Bola Voli USU Student Activity Unit has been established since at least 2018 and has captured the attention of USU students as one of the most captivating sports-oriented student activity units to participate in. The Bola Voli USU Student Activity Unit has achieved remarkable accomplishments in various competitions. Furthermore, the Bola Voli USU Student Activity Unit actively organizes internal and external matches, as well as participates in tournaments at both regional and national levels. The presence of the Bola Voli USU Student Activity Unit provides opportunities for students who have an interest and talent in volleyball to develop their skills, expand their social network, and experience the spirit of healthy competition. Joining the Bola Voli USU Student Activity Unit is the right first step for students who want to sharpen their volleyball skills and excel in this sport.
