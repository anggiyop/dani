About Society of Renewable Energy (SRE) USU Student Activity Unit

The Society of Renewable Energy (SRE) USU Student Activity Unit is a community dedicated to promoting and developing renewable energy on the campus and in the wider community. The passionate individuals behind the establishment of this organization believe that renewable energy is a sustainable and environmentally-friendly solution to meet current and future energy needs. Through educational activities, research, and concrete actions, this student activity unit aims to raise awareness about the importance of renewable energy and inspire positive changes in energy behavior in the surrounding environment. Society of Renewable Energy (SRE) USU Student Activity Unit regularly holds discussions, seminars, and workshops to address topics related to renewable energy and carries out real projects in implementing renewable energy technologies. This student activity unit is a branch organization of the broader SRE network, which has delegates across 34 prestigious campuses in Indonesia. For USU students interested in making a difference and gaining valuable experience in the field of renewable energy development, this student activity unit can be a great choice for you.
