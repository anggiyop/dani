USU FKM Celebrates the Peak of the 29th Anniversary
 

Published by

Bambang Riyanto

Published at

Thursday, 10 November 2022

The Dean of USU FKM hopes that the existence of USU's FKM will play an increasingly important role in providing benefits to public health in Indonesia.

USU PR - The Faculty of Public Health (FKM) of the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) celebrated the peak of its 29th Anniversary with a series of activities. Taking place at the USU Auditorium on Tuesday, (11/01/2022), the celebration of the 29th anniversary of the USU FKM with the theme “Be Strong and Useful for Public Health”.

The theme is a form of reflection for the entire USU FKM family at the age of 29 to become stronger as an institution that contributes to health development in Indonesia.

USU FKM Dean, Prof. Dr. Dra. Ida Yustina, M.Si said that USU's FKM has many programs that are in line with the vision towards a national level.

"USU FKM has many programs, of course," Prof. Ida concluded.

The Dean of USU FKM hopes that the existence of USU's FKM will play an increasingly important role in providing benefits to public health in Indonesia.

“Happy anniversary to our loving USU FKM. Hopefully it will exist more and can provide benefits to the development of public health in Indonesia," she said.

In line with this, the Chairperson of the Committee for the 29th Anniversary of USU FKM Ernawati Nasution, S.K.M., M.Kes. hopes that USU FKM can continue to give the best contribution to the community, especially in the promotion and prevention of disease, one of which is about stunting.

"Continue to advance USU FKM, hopefully it can continue to contribute the best, especially the promotion of stunting prevention," Ernawati said.

For information, the appearance of the USU FKM choir and multi-ethnic dance performances added to the lively celebration of the 29th Anniversary of USU FKM. The performance was then followed by a scientific oration delivered by Dr. dr. Cashtry Meher, M.Kes. M.Ked (KK), Sp.KK., FIHFAA. (©ULC)
