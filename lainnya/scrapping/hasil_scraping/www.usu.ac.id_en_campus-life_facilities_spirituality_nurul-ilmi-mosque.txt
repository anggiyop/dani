Nurul Ilmi Mosque

The Nurul Ilmi Mosque is located within the premises of the USU Faculty of Cultural Sciences (FIB USU). This mosque has sufficient space to accommodate congregational prayers and is equipped with air conditioning to provide comfort for the USU academic community and the surrounding community who wish to worship. The Nurul Ilmi Mosque also regularly conducts Friday prayers every week.
