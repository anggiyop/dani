Master Program
Development Studies
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Master's Program in Development Studies is a study program that provides education on knowledge and skills in analyzing the complexity of development, preparing development programs, and determining integrated and regional-based policies.

Accompanied by the best teaching staff with extensive experience, the Master's Program in Development Studies has a solid goal to produce graduates who have the ability and mastery of development theories that can be applied in examining development problems that affect humans, social, economic, and political sectors in local, national, regional, or international scope. Our graduates have broad career prospects as experts in government and private institutions.

Vision of Master's Program in Development Studies

Making the Master of Development Studies Program at the Faculty of Social and Political Sciences, University of Sumatera Utara, a leading education program in Development Studies characterized by continuous knowledge improvement and professionalism towards a university for industry.

Mission of Master's Program in Development Studies

Education and Teaching

Carrying out the education and teaching process to improve knowledge and practical skills in analyzing the complexity of development, developing development programs, establishing integrated and regional-based policies, and being able to compete at the local, regional, and national levels.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
