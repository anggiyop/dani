USU PR - Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) had the opportunity to receive a visit from the French Ambassador to Indonesia, Fabien Penone, and all staff. The visit, which discussed cooperation between the French Embassy / French Government and USU, took place in the Rector's Audience Room, USU Central Administration Bureau Building, Monday (25/09/2023).

In his remarks, USU Rector, Prof. Dr. Muryanto Amin S.sos., M.Si. stated that this meeting discussed cooperation in university institutional management, improving academic quality, student/lecturer exchanges, strategies for achieving reputation, as well as collaborative research, innovation and publication programs that include the French Government.

This cooperation is expected to make a significant contribution to improving university rankings, expanding research coverage, and promoting scientific discoveries that have a positive impact on the global community.

"This meeting can help Universitas Sumatera Utara to achieve progress as an internationally reputable university," said Prof. Muryanto.

Prof. Dr. Muryanto Amin S.sos., M.Si. hopes that this meeting will continue, thus providing understanding and agreement to formulate cooperation between the French Government, facilitated by the French Embassy to Indonesia and USU.

"We hope that this meeting will not only end today but can be followed up with further meetings," hoped Prof. Muryanto.

The French Ambassador to Indonesia, Fabien Panone, expressed his desire to establish close cooperation with educational institutions in Indonesia, including USU.

"We want to cooperate with all institutions in the archipelago. So, I came here to Medan to organize and establish relations with USU. Especially, this university has added some very interesting collaborations in the past," said Fabien.

Furthermore, Fabien Penone highlighted the importance of adjusting issues of concern to both parties and finding joint solutions to overcome the challenges faced.

"The important thing for us to do now is to assess what the USU's priority issues are, to see if they match what we want to do," Fabien said.

Fabien Penone hopes that the next meeting will result in a more concrete and in-depth framework for cooperation, and open the door for the development of joint initiatives that will create significant positive impacts for both parties.

"Of course, that's why we have to talk about this again by having a meeting that will discuss this cooperation in more depth," Fabien explained.
