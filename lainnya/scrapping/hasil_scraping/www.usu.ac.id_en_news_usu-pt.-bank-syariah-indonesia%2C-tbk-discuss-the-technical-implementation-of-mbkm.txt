USU-PT. Bank Syariah Indonesia, Tbk Discuss the Technical Implementation of MBKM
 

Published by

Bambang Riyanto

Published at

Wednesday, 25 August 2021

USU PUBLIC RELATION – MEDAN: The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) held an online technical meeting of the implementation of Independent Learning Independent Campus (MBKM) between USU and PT. Bank Syariah Indonesia, Tbk, Wednesday (8/18/2021). The technical meeting was opened by USU’s Vice Rector II, Dr. Poppy Anjelisa Zaitun Hasibuan, S.Si., M.Si, Apt.

The activity was filled with conveying the MBKM implementation by PT. Bank Syariah Indonesia, Tbk (BSI) and discussion between BSI and the Faculty of Economics and Business of USU (FEB) and Service and Development Unit of USU (UPP). Also attending in the meeting: USU Cooperation Expert Staff, Head of Research Bureau, Community Service and Cooperation of USU, the Head and Secretary of UPP, FEB Dean, FEB Vice Dean I and Head of Study Program of Economic Development. Also attending from PT. Bank Syariah Indonesia: the Branch Manager, Yoserinaldi and Puja Nur Arief as the Area Manager.

The Vice Rector III of USU hope that there will be a follow up from this meeting in the form of real cooperation between PT. Bank Syariah Indonesia, Tbk and USU and can be implemented in the near future. (RJ/©ULC)
