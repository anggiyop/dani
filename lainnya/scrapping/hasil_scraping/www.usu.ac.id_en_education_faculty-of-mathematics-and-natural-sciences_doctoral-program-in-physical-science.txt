Doctoral Program In Physical Science
Doctoral Program
Physical Science
Universitas Sumatera Utara

As a leading study program in the field of physical science, the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Doctoral Program in Physical Science emphasizes research in science. The program offers three main areas of study, namely theoretical physics, materials, and instrumentation.

Supported by high-quality faculty, the program is also equipped with the best facilities and amenities. We are committed to achieving our program goal of producing doctoral candidates in physics who not only have expertise in physics and its applications, but also have high morality. In addition, we aim to improve the quality of research, especially in the fields of material physics and energy physics.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
