USU Rector Inaugurates Vocational Vice Dean and Structural
 

Published by

Bambang Riyanto

Published at

Monday, 14 November 2022

"Stay consistent and steadfast in accordance with the oath and promise of office to carry out duties," USU Rector said.

USU Public Relations - The Rector of the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) officially inaugurated the Vice Deans of the Vocational Faculty, as well as Structural Officials such as the Deputy Head of the Information System Center, the Chair and Secretary of the Study Program, the Secretary of the Department and the Head of the Laboratory of the Universitas Sumatera Utara. This inauguration was held in the USU FEB Hall on Friday (11/04/2022).

With achievements and responsibilities carried out, consistency and sticking to the oath of office are the keys to the realization of a university that is worthy of being reckoned with at the global level.

Rector of the Universitas Sumatera Utara Dr. Muryanto Amin, S.Sos., M.Si explained that the position currently held requires commitment, sacrifice, hard work and great responsibility in its implementation in order to realize the development and progress of the university.

"Stay consistent and steadfast in accordance with the oath and promise of office to carry out duties," the USU Rector said.

Deputy Head of the Information System Center who has been appointed Ainul Hizriadi, S.Kom., M.Sc said that things need to be improved such as network infrastructure and information system services.

By filling the position as Deputy Head of the Information System Center, it is expected to be able to carry out work programs with staff in order to be able to achieve the targets that have been set.

"According to the rector's work program for this year and next year, it must be a problem of improving network infrastructure and for the sector so that the information system services at USU can run even better," he explained. (©ULC)
