USU Karya Salemba Empat (KSE) Scholarship 2023
USU Karya Salemba Empat (KSE) Scholarship 2023

Awardee Terms and Conditions

The respective student is not married
The respective student is not receiving scholarships from faculties, universities, institutions, or other third parties.
Scholarships are given as a living allowance of IDR750,000 (seven hundred and fifty thousand rupiah) per month.
This scholarship is given without official ties or anything against the recipient student or the campus.

 

Applicant Requirements

Upload the required documents at www.beasiswa.or.id.
Complete all data at www.beasiswa.or.id
Registrants must make a vlog with the terms and conditions:
Video about self-introduction, family/life background, and why I need and choose the KSE scholarship.
The video is 1-2 minutes long.
The video is uploaded on Google Drive on the respective email.
The video link is shared in the KSE online system (scholarship.or.id) on each account when filling in the KSE Online Registration data.
Videos may not be deleted.

 

Required Documents

Close-up photo using alma mater (white background)
Full body photo using alma mater
Indonesian ID Card, Student ID Card, and Family Card
Salary slip/statement of income of parents/guardians
The latest electricity bill
PDAM bill (if any)
Letter of recommendation/certificate of not receiving other scholarships from the campus/faculty
Academic Transcript
Essay
Vlog or video of 1-2 minutes duration about self-profile, family/life background, and why you need a scholarship
