Extra Pel +
Floor Cleaner Liquid
Product Description

Extra Pel+ is an eco-friendly floor cleaner with a fresh kaffir lime scent. By using this liquid, the floor will become clean and shiny. The plus point is that when used, this liquid does not irritate the hands and feet. In addition, the remaining liquid discharged into the sewer will not interfere with the activity of decomposing bacteria.

Dr. Ir. Nurzainah Ginting, M.Sc.
