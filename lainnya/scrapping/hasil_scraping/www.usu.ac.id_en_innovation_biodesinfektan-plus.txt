USU Biodesinfektan Plus
Hand Disinfectant Liquid
Product Description

USU Biodesinfektan Plus is a hand disinfectant liquid made from organic ingredients with a fragrant lemongrass aroma. Apart from being used to clean hands, this liquid can disinfect other surfaces, such as the face. Because it uses organic ingredients, this liquid is safe to use before praying.

Inventors:

Dr. Ir. Nurzainah Ginting, M.Sc.

Faculties:

Faculty of Agriculture

Product Category:

Health and Cosmetics

Innovation Status:
