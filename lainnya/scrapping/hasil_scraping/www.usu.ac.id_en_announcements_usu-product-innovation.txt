Description

The mastery and application of science and technology (IPTEK), such as creating innovative products, is integral to national development. In the coming era of trade and economic development. Indonesia can no longer rely solely on conventional industries.

 

The growth and development of innovative industries or technology-based start-up companies in Indonesia will benefit the welfare of society. This catalog is an overview of innovative products that contains product profiles, advantages, and inventors. This book is also intended as a promotional medium for innovative products owned by Universitas Sumatera Utara and supports the commercial efforts of the University of North Sumatra's innovative products.
