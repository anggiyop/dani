Titi Bobrok Acehnese Noodle

Titi Bobrok Acehnese Noodle is a delectable culinary destination in the heart of Medan, bringing Acehnese delights to every bite. It is famous for Acehnese Noodles, a spicy noodle dish that has become an icon of Aceh throughout Indonesia. Established by the late Mr. Fuadi Yusuf, Mie Aceh Titi Bobrok is located at Jalan Setia Budi No. 17 D, Sei Sikambing B, Medan Sunggal District, Medan City. This legendary Acehnese noodle restaurant has been established since 1996 and still remains one of the favorite culinary of Medan citizens to this day. The name Titi Bobrok itself describes the exciting story behind this stall. The name was inspired by the bridge's condition (called titi in Medan) near the shop, which was damaged and known as "titi bobrok". Unsurprisingly, Titi Bobrok Acehnese Noodle is always crowded with tourists. Aside from the unparalleled deliciousness on offer, the prices of the Acehnese noodles here are also very affordable, well within your budget. The price range for the available menus ranges from around IDR12,000 to IDR40,000. Don't miss the popular Mie Aceh Kepiting, which costs IDR27,000 per serving. Mie Aceh Titi Bobrok is a culinary paradise offering Acehnese dishes in Medan. Enjoy the famous Acehnese Noodles with its authentic spicy flavor, appetizing with every mouthful.

Other Information

Mie Aceh Titi Bobrok serves the most famous Aceh noodle dish in Medan. This place was built in 1996 with a menu that starts from 12,000 to 40,000 rupiah.
