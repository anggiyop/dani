About Bureau of Student and Alumni Affairs

The Bureau of Student and Alumni Affairs (BKK) of the University of North Sumatra is a bureau that specifically manages student and alumni affairs. To support student affairs development, USU's BKK develops programs concerning reasoning and sciences; talents, interests, and abilities; well-being; social concern; and supporting activities based on academic principles, morals, scientific ethics, and the interests of society. USU's BKK made various efforts to increase student achievement in sciences, arts, sports, and entrepreneurship.
