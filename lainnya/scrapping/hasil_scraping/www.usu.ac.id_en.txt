Education

Education is one of the most important keys for nations to survive in global competition and is the most strategic area of national welfare.

Intelligent and characterized human resources are a prerequisite for the development of a high civilization. development of a high civilization. The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) strives to promote human resources in Indonesia through quality education to improve the welfare of the nation and state. There are various vocational, undergraduate, professional, master, specialist, sub-specialist and doctoral education programs that you can choose according to your interests and talents to support your future career and professional expertise. professional expertise in the future.

Vocational Program

The USU Vocational Program offers various fields of study at the diploma level that focus on real experience and practical skills needed in the world of work.

Undergraduate Program

USU Bachelor's programs provide an in-depth education in a chosen field of study to develop knowledge, skills and analytical thinking for success in career and life.

Profession Program

USU Professional Programs offer specific fields of study to train practitioners with in-depth knowledge, practical skills, and professional ethics to produce graduates with specialized expertise in specific careers.

Master Program

The USU Master's Program offers areas of study with deepening knowledge and research to equip students with advanced skills to contribute to complex problem solving and innovation.

Doctoral Program

The Doctoral Program offers areas of study that take students to the highest level of expertise to prepare graduates as leaders and key contributors in scientific research and innovation.

Specialist Program

Specialist and Sub-Specialist programs offer areas of study that provide advanced clinical education and training in the medical field to make graduates skilled in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of complex health conditions.
