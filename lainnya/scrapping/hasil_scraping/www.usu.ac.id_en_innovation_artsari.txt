Artsari
Sabun Padat
Product Description

Artsari is a solid soap made from coconut oil. This soap uses natural ingredients and does not use SLS, often used as a detergent. Artsari soap consists of 4 variants: lemongrass, eucalyptus, pumpkin, and betel soap. Artsari is now on sale and has been produced with the provisions of CPKB and has a license from BPOM.
