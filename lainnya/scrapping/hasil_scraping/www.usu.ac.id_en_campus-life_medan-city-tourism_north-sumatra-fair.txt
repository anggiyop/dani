North Sumatra Fair

The North Sumatra Fair (PRSU) is an annual celebration that presents the cultural wonders and beauty of North Sumatra. The event celebrates the rich culture, arts, and proud local products of North Sumatra. Upon entering the PRSU area, visitors are greeted by stunning pavilions and exhibition booths, showcasing the charm and richness of the region's culture and products. Each pavilion and booth is full of uniqueness, offering a variety of handicrafts beautifully sculpted by local artisans. In addition, enjoy a variety of delicacies and culinary specialties that will delight the palate of visitors. Each dish reflects the unique and authentic flavors of the people of North Sumatra. Not only that, but the local products on display at PRSU also reflect the people's daily lives in North Sumatra. Everything is presented with unparalleled elegance, from colorful traditional textiles to fresh and abundant produce. PRSU runs for a month every year, creating an opportunity for everyone to experience the magic of culture and the richness of local products that captivate the heart. Don't miss this magical moment to join the PRSU celebration in Medan. Witness for yourself the enchanting cultural charm, unforgettable culinary delights, and unique local products that depict the splendor of North Sumatra.
