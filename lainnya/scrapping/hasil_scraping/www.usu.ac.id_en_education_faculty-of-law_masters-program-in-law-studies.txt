Master Program
Law Studies
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Master's Program in Law Studies of Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) is an advanced education for those who have earned a Bachelor of Law degree. First opened in 1987, this program is the oldest master's program at Universitas Sumatera Utara. With a focus on the application of legal teachings, doctrines, and theories, students are expected to be able to apply legal knowledge with logical, critical, systematic, and creative thinking so as to become qualified experts in the field of law.

This program's mission is to form students as the next generation of law in Indonesia. Graduates of the USU Master's Program in Law Studies have great opportunities for careers as competent notaries, lawyers, and prosecutors in their fields. The Master of Law (M.H.) degree obtained by students who complete this study program is clear proof of their ability to understand and apply law studies in the professional world.

Vision of Master's Program in Law Studies

To become a Learning Center for Law Studies at the Master’s Level, a Leading Center for Law Studies and Information Nationally with quality, moral, and professional graduates and has competitive advantages, both at national and international levels.

Mission of Master's Program in Law Studies

Legal Science Learning Center

Becoming a Learning Center for Legal Sciences at the Masters Level, a Leading Center for Legal Studies and Information Nationally with graduates who are qualified, moral, professional, and have a competitive advantage both at the national and international levels.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
