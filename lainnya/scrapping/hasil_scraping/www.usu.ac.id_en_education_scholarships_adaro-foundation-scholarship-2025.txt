Adaro Foundation Scholarship 2025 Registration Timeline

Registration: May 1, 2025
Document Submission Deadline: May 21, 2025
Selection and Verification: May 22 - 28, 2025
Verification Results Announcement: May 29 - 30, 2025

Eligibility Criteria for Adaro Foundation Scholarship 2025

Indonesian citizen (WNI)
Active student in S1 & D4 programs, minimum semester III
Minimum GPA of 3.00
Priority for students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds
Not currently receiving a scholarship from Universitas Sumatera Utara or other institutions (violations will result in sanctions)
Active in organizations within or outside the university
Academic or non-academic achievements

Required Documents for Adaro Foundation Scholarship 2025

Fill out the registration form (https://bit.ly/formbeasiswaadaro2025)
Photocopy of Student ID / Academic Portal Screenshot
Photocopy of Family Card (KK)
Photocopy of Latest Academic Transcript (KHS)
Photocopy of last 3 months electricity bill
Latest Statement of Economic Disadvantage (SKTM) from the village or sub-district head, or a statement of parentâ€™s income
Statement of Not Receiving Other Scholarships from Sila USU

Certificate of active membership in any campus or external organizations (if applicable)
Photocopy of certificates/awards from competitions (if applicable)

Recommended Study Programs for Adaro Foundation Scholarship 2025

Scholarship Amount for Adaro Foundation 2025

Monthly scholarship of IDR 850,000, distributed every 3 months via the university. Scholarship period from August 2025 to July 2026

Document Submission for Adaro Foundation Scholarship 2025

Complete the online registration form and submit all required documents in a Blue Folder to the Directorate of Student Achievement and Alumni Relations by May 21, 2025, at 3:00 PM WIB
