The Faculty of Agriculture (FP) at the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) has been a milestone in producing competent and dedicated graduates in various fields of agriculture throughout Indonesia. Guided by experienced and dedicated faculty members, FP USU's main mission is to create agricultural professionals who are not only highly qualified, but also driven by strong ethics, as well as being knowledgeable, creative, and responsive to the dynamic needs of agriculture, both at the national and international levels. FP USU offers a comprehensive education for those who wish to play an active role in a dynamic, growing and respected agricultural sector. We prepare students to engage in the development of agriculture, forestry, science, and related technologies, which form the backbone for the nation's progress.

Vision of Faculty of Agriculture

In 2039, become a leading agricultural higher education institution in the development of science, technology, and human resources, with plantations as its core competency to promote national food sovereignty in food crop agriculture, animal husbandry, and aquatic resources.

Mission of Faculty of Agriculture

Education

Organizing outstanding agricultural higher education that generates graduates with BINTANG characteristics and achieves worldwide recognition.

Research

Conduct and expand innovative applied agriculture research in the TALENTA domain on a national and worldwide scale.

Service

Providing specialist services in agriculture, particularly plantations, to assist national food security in food crop agriculture, animal husbandry, and aquatic resources.
