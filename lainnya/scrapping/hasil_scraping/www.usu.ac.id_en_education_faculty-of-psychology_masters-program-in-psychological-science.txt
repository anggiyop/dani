Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Master's Program in Psychological Science is a postgraduate psychology education that students can take from various undergraduate majors.

Established in December 2011, education in the Master Program in Psychological Science explores the science of psychology in depth and comprehensively to produce graduates who can solve problems through a scientific approach based on psychological principles, as well as conduct output-based research to develop science, especially in the fields of agro-industry and technology and the field of psychology in multi-ethnic communities, both national and international communities.

Vision of Master's Program in Psychological Science

Becoming a center for developing local wisdom-based education with academic excellence that can compete globally.

Mission of Master's Program in Psychological Science

Education

Organizing quality psychology education in multi-ethnic communities at the master's level.

Research

Carrying out output-based quality research to develop comprehensive and innovative knowledge in the field of psychology in multi-ethnic communities.

Community Service

Carrying out community service to disseminate problem-solving-oriented knowledge to improve psychological knowledge in multi-ethnic communities.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
