About Innovation Research Development Agency

The Innovation Research Development Agency (BPRI-USU) at USU is a newly formed unit in 2022 that primarily coordinates innovation management within USU. BPRI-USU is responsible for promoting the utilization of research results, technology transfer, and innovation development. BPRI-USU has targets and objectives to increase the growth of startup innovation products derived from the research of teaching staff (lecturers) at USU.
