LPS Utama Scholarship 2024
LPS Utama Scholarship 2024

Scholarship Timeline

Document Submission: September 17 - October 4, 2024
Document Selection: October 7 - October 11, 2024
Interview Selection: October 14 - October 25, 2024
Final Announcement: November 4, 2024

 

Scholarship Benefits

Scholarship funding of IDR 1,500,000 per month for 12 months.
Various soft skills training sessions.
Capacity Building & Champion Camp.
Community socialization programs.

Management

Scholarship Categories and Criteria

1. Outstanding Students
Age Requirement: Maximum 24 years old at registration.
Academic Requirements:
Minimum GPA: 3.5.
Enrolled in an undergraduate (S1) program.
Currently in the 3rd to 5th semester.
Minimum TOEFL score: 500 or equivalent.
Additional Requirements:
Recommendation letter from the university.
Statement letter confirming no other scholarships.
Motivation letter in English detailing the reasons for applying and future career plans.
2. Underprivileged Students
Age Requirement: Maximum 24 years old at registration.
Academic Requirements:
Minimum GPA: 3.25.
Enrolled in an undergraduate (S1) program.
Completed at least 40 credits (SKS).
Currently in the 3rd to 5th semester.
Additional Requirements:
Recommendation letter from the university.
Statement letter confirming no other scholarships.
Motivation letter in Indonesian detailing the reasons for applying and future career plans.
Certificate of financial hardship (SKTM) issued by the local village office or municipality.
3. 3T Area Students (Underdeveloped, Frontier, Outermost Areas)
Age Requirement: Maximum 24 years old at registration.
Academic Requirements:
Minimum GPA: 3.25.
Enrolled in an undergraduate (S1) program.
Completed at least 40 credits (SKS).
Currently in the 3rd to 5th semester.
Additional Requirements:
Recommendation letter from the university.
Statement letter confirming no other scholarships.
Motivation letter in Indonesian detailing the reasons for applying and future career plans.
Attach a copy of ID card (KTP) or Family Card (KK) proving residence or birthplace in a government-designated 3T area.

*) Refers to Presidential Regulation No. 63 of 2020.
