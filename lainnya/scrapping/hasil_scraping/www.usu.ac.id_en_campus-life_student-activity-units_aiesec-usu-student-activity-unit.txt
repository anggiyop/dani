About AIESEC USU Student Activity Unit

AIESEC USU is an active organization at Universitas Sumatera Utara. AIESEC is the world's largest student network focusing on leadership development, cultural exchange, and global career development. Members of AIESEC USU engage in various projects and programs involving student exchange, international internships, and social activities in the surrounding community. With a mission to build future leaders who have a deep understanding of global issues, AIESEC USU provides valuable experiences to its members regarding leadership skills, teamwork, and cross-cultural understanding. Joining AIESEC USU will offer a unique opportunity to grow and contribute on a global scale while forging close friendships with students from different countries.
