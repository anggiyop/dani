Basketball Court

The USU basketball courts are available in several locations on campus, including at Cikal USU, the Faculty of Medicine, and the Civil Engineering Department. These basketball courts have adequate facilities like scoreboards, basketball hoops, and large spectator areas. The basketball court at Cikal USU is located in the Cikal USU complex and is easily accessible to students and campus visitors. Meanwhile, the basketball court at the Faculty of Medicine is located in the student center. It is the most strategically located and thus suitable for sports and recreational activities for students and faculty. The Civil Engineering basketball court is located near the Civil Engineering futsal court. With complete facilities and an easily accessible location, the basketball court at USU is a favorite place for basketball lovers on campus.
