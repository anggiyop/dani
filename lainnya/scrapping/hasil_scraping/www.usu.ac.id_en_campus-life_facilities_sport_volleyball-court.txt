Volleyball Court

The USU volleyball court, located on Jl. Almamater, close to Cikal USU, is a prime destination for volleyball enthusiasts at the Universitas Sumatera Utara to practice and play. The court offers a comfortable and exciting playing experience with its standard size and smooth surface. Complete facilities, such as poles, nets, and volleyballs, allow players to use them optimally. Another advantage is its easy accessibility and strategic location within the campus, so students and the general public can easily reach it. With all these advantages, the USU volleyball court is an irreplaceable place to hone volleyball skills and enjoy the joy of the sport.
