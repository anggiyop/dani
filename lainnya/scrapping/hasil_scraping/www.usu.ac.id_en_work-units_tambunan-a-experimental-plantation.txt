About Tambunan A Experimental Plantation

Tambunan A Experimental Plantation is an academic support unit that functions to implement and manage the organization of education, research, services to the community, and practices in the fields of agricultural science and other sciences for both the university community and the external community in need. Covering an area of 555 hectares, Tambunan A Experimental Plantation is committed to producing educated and skilled alumni with integrity who become academic elements of science development in the field of applied plantations.
