Doctoral Program In Accounting Science
Doctoral Program
Accounting Science
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Doctoral Program in Accounting Science at Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) is an advanced educational program designed to prepare future academics through a thorough education in accounting research. This doctoral program aims to equip students with the necessary skills to conduct accounting research independently, make significant contributions to the development of accounting science, and solve problems related to the accounting field.

With the vision of "Becoming an excellent and competitive accounting program in ASEAN by 2030," it is expected that graduates of this program will become skilled academics and important contributors to the development of accounting and bring innovative ideas that can shape the future direction of this field. Students who complete this study program will be awarded a doctoral degree (Dr.).

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
