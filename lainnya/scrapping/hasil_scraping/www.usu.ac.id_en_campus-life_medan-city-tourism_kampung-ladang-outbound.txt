Kampung Ladang Outbound

Kampung Ladang is an exciting destination in the Pancur Batu district, close to Medan, making it easily accessible to visitors. This place offers a large land, becoming a favorite place for the surrounding community and certain groups for various exciting activities such as outbound and memorable pre-wedding sessions. Kampung Ladang is equipped with complete facilities, guaranteeing an unforgettable experience for every visitor. Flying Fox, beautiful garden decorations, an adventure arena with various obstacles and obstacles, and photo spots that offer stunning aesthetics are all here. Many young couples choose this location to hold their pre-wedding session. Not only does it offer an exciting experience, but the entrance fee to Kampung Ladang is also very affordable. Simply by paying starting from 5 thousand rupiahs, you can enjoy all these facilities to relax or hang out with campus friends. So, don't miss the opportunity to visit this exciting destination and experience unforgettable fun in Kampung Ladang.

Other Information

Kampung Ladang, located in the Pancur Batu area, offers various outbound activities and memorable pre-wedding photo sessions with entrance tickets starting from only 5,000 rupiah.
