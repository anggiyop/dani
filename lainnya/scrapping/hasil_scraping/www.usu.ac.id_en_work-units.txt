Agencies

The agencies of Universitas Sumatera Utara is the institution responsible for decision-making and overall university governance, with a focus on strategic policies, curriculum development, and coordination among units within the university.

Institutes

The institutes of Universitas Sumatera Utara are dedicated to research and development in various academic fields as well as providing a platform for the development of knowledge and collaboration among academics, students, and researchers at USU. These institutions play a crucial role in enhancing the quality of education and research as well as making positive contributions to society and regional development.

Institute for Curriculum and Learning Innovation
Institute for Scientific Writing innovation and Intellectual Property Rights
Institute for Community Service
Institute for Research

Units

The units of Universitas Sumatera Utara encompass various administrative and academic divisions that support the university's operations. Directorates

The directorates of Universitas Sumatera Utara oversee specific areas of management and implementation within USU, including academic affairs, research and innovation, and strategic planning. The directorates play a crucial role in ensuring effective coordination, efficient operations, and the realization of the university's vision and missions.

Directorate of Education Development
Directorate of Bureaucratic Reform and Transformation
Directorate of Digitalization and System Integration
Directorate of Internationalization and Global Partnerships

Bureaus

The bureaus of Universitas Sumatera Utara are administrative institutions responsible for the provision of administrative services and support in various fields, including finance, human resources, and facilities. The bureaus play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth operation of the university and ensuring efficient services to students, staff, and all members of the university.
