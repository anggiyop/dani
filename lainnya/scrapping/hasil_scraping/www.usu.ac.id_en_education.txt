Erasmus Program

The Erasmus Program is an EU initiative that involves the exchange of students and academic staff, as well as cooperation in education and research projects. The program focuses on enhancing cooperation between higher education institutions in Europe and partner countries, as well as promoting international mobility to improve the quality of higher education.
