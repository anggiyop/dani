Diploma Program In Secretarial Studies
Diploma Program
Secretarial Studies
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Diploma Program in Secretarial at the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) is one of the study programs in office management and secretarial that offers vocational education with a focus on secretarial practical knowledge.

This study program has four employment concentrations: junior secretaries, personal assistants, office administration staff, and banking staff. Through lecture activities emphasizing practicum, graduates of the Diploma Program in Secretarial are expected to have the necessary skills to work in an actual office environment as competent, ethical, and globally-minded secretarial intermediate experts.

Vision of Diploma Program in Secretarial Studies

To become a provider of Diploma education and teaching in the Office Management and Secretariat field that has superior competence in Office Management, morals, global outlook, and competitiveness.

Mission of Diploma Program in Secretarial Studies

Education

Organizing education that is oriented towards the formation of graduates who have competence, morality, global insight and technical skills in the secretarial field in accordance with the demands of the world of work.

Professional Graduates

Carrying out vocational education and teaching activities in the secretarial field so as to produce reliable graduates in the field of office administration.

Service

Improve services to students and other stakeholders.

Collaboration

Increasing networks and cooperation with private and government institutions as well as professional organizations and other institutions of national and international standards.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
