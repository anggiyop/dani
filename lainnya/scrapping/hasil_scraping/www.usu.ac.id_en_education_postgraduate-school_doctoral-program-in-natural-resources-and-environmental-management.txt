Doctoral Program In Natural Resources And Environmental Management
Doctoral Program
Natural Resources and Environmental Management
Universitas Sumatera Utara

As an advanced level of education from the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Master's Program in Natural Resources and Environmental Management, our study program focuses on providing education in the field of natural resources and environmental management, which is carried out in an integrated manner with an interdisciplinary, multidisciplinary, and transdisciplinary approach.

Supported by the best individuals with extensive experience and high integrity in teaching, our study program is firmly committed to making graduates academics, researchers, and productive professionals in natural resources and environmental management by prioritizing logical, critical, systematic, and creative thinking.

Vision of Doctoral Program in Natural Resources and Environmental Management

As a leading doctoral program in western Indonesia, managing natural resources and the environment to realize sustainable development.

Mission of Doctoral Program in Natural Resources and Environmental Management

Environment

Organizing a doctoral program (S3) in natural resource and environmental management in realizing environmentally sound sustainable development.

Quality Improvement

Organizing and spearheading research activities on natural resource management and the environment to be utilized in productive activities and improving the community's quality of life.

Public Service

Organizing and facilitating community service activities in managing natural resources and the environment in realizing environmentally sound sustainable development.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
