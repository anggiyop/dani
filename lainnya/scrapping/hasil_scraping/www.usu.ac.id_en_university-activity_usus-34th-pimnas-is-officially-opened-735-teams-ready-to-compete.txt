The 34th National Student Science Week (PIMNAS) at the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) was officially opened on Tuesday (26/10/2021) at the USU Rector Bureau Page, Padang Bulan Campus, Medan. Filled with a variety of 8 ethnic dances typical of North Sumatra Province by students on a magnificent stage, colorful spotlights, VVIPs, university leaders, and invited guests pressed the button to mark the opening of PIMNAS 34 USU.
