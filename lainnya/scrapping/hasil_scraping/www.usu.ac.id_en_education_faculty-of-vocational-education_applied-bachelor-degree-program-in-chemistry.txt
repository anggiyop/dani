Vision of Applied Bachelor's Degree Program in Chemistry

In 2028, the Applied Bachelor’s Degree Program in Chemistry will become a study program that produces superior and professional human resources in chemical analysis and quality control that can compete in the global industrial world.

Mission of Applied Bachelor's Degree Program in Chemistry

Education

Organizing vocational higher education in chemistry based on curriculum and syllabus as character development and professionalism of human resources that produce graduates with competencies that align with the needs of industry and the world of work.

Tri Darma

Implement, develop, and improve education, research, and community service programs in chemistry vocational to improve academic quality, develop superior chemistry vocational science, and benefit the industrial world.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
