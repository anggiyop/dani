LETTER OF DETERMINATION OF TENDER WINNER

OPERATIONAL COOPERATION FOR PARKING MANAGEMENT OF PROF. DR. CHAIRUDDIN P. LUBIS EDUCATIONAL HOSPITAL

UNIVERSITAS SUMATERA UTARA

Number: 9736/UN5.1.R5/PSS/2024

 

Based on the Proposal Letter of the Selection Team for the Operational Cooperation Provider for Parking Management of Prof. dr. Chairuddin P. Lubis Teaching Hospital, Universitas Sumatera Utara Number 05/KSO/PPRS/2024 on March 13, 2024 regarding the Proposal for Determination of the Winner of the Provider Operational Cooperation for Parking Management of Prof. dr. Chairuddin P. Lubis USU Teaching Hospital, the auction winner is hereby determined as follows:

 

Company Name: PT. Penta Artha Mandiri

Director Name: Kessi

Address: Gedung Merah Putih Telkomsel LT. Basement Jalan Putri Hijau No.1 Kesawan Village, West Medan District, North Sumatra Province

NPWP: 83.789.700.8-111.000

 

To implement the Operational Cooperation for Parking Management of Prof. dr. Chairuddin P. Lubis Teaching Hospital, Universitas Sumatera Utara according to the determined documents.

 

This letter of determination is made to be used as it should.

Medan, March 15, 2024

Signed electronically by:

Rector

Muryanto Amin
