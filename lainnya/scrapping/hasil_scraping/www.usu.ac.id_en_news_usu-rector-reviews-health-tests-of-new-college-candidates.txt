USU Rector Reviews Health Tests of New College Candidates
 

Published by

Bambang Riyanto

Published at

Monday, 23 May 2022

During the review process, the Rector of USU revealed that the medical examination this is a mandatory requirement that must be met by all new USU student candidates including the SBMPTN line and the Mandiri line.

USU PR - Rector of the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Dr. Muryanto Amin, S.Sos.,

M.Si., reviewed the course of health examination tests intended for candidates

new USU students on the National Selection Path to Enter State Universities (SNMPTN) 2022. The review was located at the Pancasila Building and the USU Polyclinic on Wednesday, (5/11/2022).

During the review process, the Rector of USU revealed that the medical examination this is a mandatory requirement that must be met by all new USU student candidates including the SBMPTN line and the Mandiri line.

The Rector of USU in his review assessed that the health examination test was running

smoothly based on the readiness of officers and students. He said that the health test has a neat flow and is running accordingly as planned.

"It runs smoothly, we saw earlier, the flow is also neat, the doctor continues to have it

specialists and general practitioners. I think that has gone as planned," said Muryanto Amin.

On that occasion, the Rector of USU instructed the prospective new USU students to

can be patient in following a series of rules if not already qualified. "After graduating from health, for those who have not qualified, be patient to follow all rules,” he said.

As for those who qualify, the rector hopes that new students can go through the study process well so that you can graduate on time and quickly get a job.

For additional information, this health screening test lasts for 3 days consecutively, starting from May 9 to 11. The test consists of an examinationtension, height, weight, eyes, and ears. (ULC)
