USU Auditorium

As one of the largest buildings at Universitas Sumatera Utara, the USU Auditorium is a facility provided by USU for hosting graduation ceremonies for its graduates. With a capacity of more than 1,500 people, the USU Auditorium is also used for open meetings of the academic community and ceremonial events, such as the commemoration of USU's Anniversary, academic orations, doctoral examinations, and other major events. The USU Auditorium also provides a running track in its courtyard to support sports activities for students and the general public.
