Specialist-1 Program In Ophthalmology
Spesialist-1 Program
Ophthalmology
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Specialist Program in Ophthalmology is one of the oldest specialist study programs at Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU). Based on a firm competency base, this program aims to produce ophthalmologists capable of providing quality eye healthcare. The educational stages in this study program include learning anatomy, pathology, diagnostics, and ophthalmology.

This study program offers promising career opportunities for graduates and contributes significantly to improving the eye health of Indonesian society. Students who complete this study program will be awarded the Ophthalmology Specialist (Sp.M.) degree.
