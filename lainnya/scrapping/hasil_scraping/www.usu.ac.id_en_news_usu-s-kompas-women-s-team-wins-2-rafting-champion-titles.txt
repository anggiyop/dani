USU's KOMPAS Women's Team Wins 2 Rafting Champion Titles
 

Published by

Bambang Riyanto

Published at

Thursday, 01 September 2022

The USU KOMPAS Women's Team won the title at the "XII Medan City Sports Week (PORCOT) 2022 Rafting Sports Branch" which was held on August, 10-11 2022. PORKOT was held again this year after the absence of its implementation last year due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

USU PR - The activity, which is located on the Tuntungan River, Medan Tuntungan District, brought joy to the white water rafting contingent from KOMPAS USU, which among others won 2nd place in the Slalom Race category and 2nd place in the Sprint Race category.

The USU KOMPAS Women's Team consisted of Trioni Narvatilova (KU 306 SA) from the Faculty of Public Health, Evakesumawardani (KU 311 BK) from the Faculty of Public Health, Adelia Devita Sari (KU 331 AA) from the Faculty of Nursing, Artika Lessanti (KU 333 AA) from the Faculty of Economics and Business and Vera Viva Windya (KU 344 AA) from the Faculty of Economics and Business. (Dedi/RJ – ©ULC)
