Van Deventer-Maas Indonesia Scolarship 2025/2026

Van Deventer-Maas Indonesia Scholarship 2025/2026 Registration Criteria

Eligible students: S1/D4 students in their 2nd – 6th semester and D3 students in their 2nd – 4th semester
Maximum age: 27 years old
Financial background: Must come from an economically disadvantaged family (proven by a Certificate of Financial Hardship (SKTM) and/or a recommendation letter from the university confirming the student's financial condition)
Minimum GPA: 3.00
Eligible Fields of Study: Agriculture, Fisheries, Animal Husbandry, Architecture, Arts and Culture, Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics, Physics, Communication, Economics, Education, Engineering, Language Studies, Management, Forestry, Geography, Health/Medicine, History, Informatics, Statistics, Law, Political Science, etc. (except Theology and Religious Studies)
Active involvement in organizations or community activities is preferred
English proficiency is an advantage
Must not be receiving another scholarship

 

Scholarship Benefits

Monthly allowance: IDR 800,000 (provided until the end of the study period: 6 semesters for D3 and 8 semesters for S1/D4)
Additional bonuses:
TOEFL bonus: IDR 400,000
On-time graduation bonus: IDR 700,000
Opportunity to participate in free Capacity Building training

 

Document Submission for the Van Deventer-Maas Indonesia Scholarship 2025/2026

Students who pass the First Stage Selection must submit original documents as per the Required Documents List in a Red Folder to the DITMAWALUMNI USU Office from April 12 – 19 at 15:00 WIB. (Please refer to the Scholarship Manual for detailed registration procedures).
