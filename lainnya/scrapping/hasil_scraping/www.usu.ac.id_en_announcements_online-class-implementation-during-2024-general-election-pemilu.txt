CIRCULAR LETTER

NUMBER 458/UN5.1.R1/SPB/2024

CONCERNING

THE IMPLEMENTATION OF ONLINE CLASSES DURING 2024 GENERAL ELECTION

To:

1. Dean of the Faculty

2. Director of the Postgraduate School

Universitas Sumatera Utara

Medan

With respect, based on the Regulation of the General Election Commission Number 3 of 2022 concerning the Stages and Schedule of the Implementation of the 2024 General Election, the General Election voting will be held on February 14, 2024.

Therefore, it is hereby conveyed to the Dean of the Faculty and the Director of the Postgraduate School to facilitate online classes for learning on February 12-16, 2024 so that students can exercise their right to vote in their hometowns.

Thus, this circular letter was created to attract attention and be implemented as appropriate.

Thank you for your attention and cooperation.

January 24, 2024

Electronically signed by:

Vice Rector I

Edy Ikhsan
