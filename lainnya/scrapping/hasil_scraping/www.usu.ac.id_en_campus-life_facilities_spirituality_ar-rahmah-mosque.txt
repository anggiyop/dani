Ar-Rahmah Mosque

The Ar-Rahmah Mosque is located on the 1st floor of the FK USU Building, right next to the FK USU Canteen. This mosque is accessible to both the USU academic community and the surrounding community during the building's opening hours. The mosque has two floors and is already equipped with air conditioning and other facilities to support worship. For Muslims, in addition to performing daily fard prayers, you can also perform Friday prayers here.
