Doctoral Program in Dental Science
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Doctoral Program in Dental Science is the highest level of study in dentistry in higher education. The program is designed to provide an in-depth understanding of the practice of dentistry as well as a robust scientific foundation in this field. The main objective of the Doctoral program in Dental Science is to produce graduate experts in dentistry who can develop the latest scientific and technological concepts through research and have leadership skills in developing research programs.

The vision of this study program is "To become a center for doctoral education in Dental Science that produces graduates with research excellence in the field of natural resources based on natural materials and globally competitive." Graduates of the USU Doctoral Program in Dental Science are expected to be able to fill the gap between academic research and practical applications in improving the quality of dental health services globally. Students who complete this study program will earn a Doctoral degree (Dr.).

Vision of Doctoral Program in Dental Science

To become a center of education for the Doctoral Program in Dental Science that produces graduates with excellence in research in the field of natural materials-based natural resources and global competitiveness

Mission of Doctoral Program in Dental Science

Education

To conduct doctoral education to produce graduates who are capable of solving various problems in the field of dental and oral health.

Research

a. To develop innovative and excellent research in natural resources based on local natural materials. b. To apply evidence-based research results to meet society's needs in dental and oral health.

Community Service

a. To conduct various community service activities related to improving dental and oral health according to the needs of society. b. To establish cooperation with institutions related to dentistry in fulfilling the latest science and technology at the national and international levels.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
