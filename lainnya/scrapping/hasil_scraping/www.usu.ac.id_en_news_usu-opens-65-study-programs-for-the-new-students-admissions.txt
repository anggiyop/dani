USU will launch 65 study programs in the New Students Admissions or Penerimaan Mahasiswa Baru (PMB) 2023, including 51 undergraduate programs (S1) and 14 applied undergraduate programs (D4). This year, USU offers at least 20% capacity on the SNBP route, followed by 30% on the SNBT route.

USU PR - Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) launches 65 study programs via the National Selection Based Based on Achievement (SNBP), National Selection Based on Test (SNBT), Selection of Mandir Students (SMM), and Selection of Diploma Programme Students (SPMPD) in 2023

Registration for the SNBP 2023 route began between 14 and 28 February 2023, the SNBP results will be released on March 28, 2023, and the SNBT will be open between March 23 and April 14, 2023.

USU will launch 65 study programs in PMB 2023, including 51 undergraduate programs (S1) and 14 applied undergraduate programs (D4). This year, USU offers at least 20% capacity on the SNBP route, followed by 30% on the SNBT route.

Prospective students can use The Ministry of Education and Culture's website at https://snpmb.bppp.kemdikbud.go.id/ for details on the capacity and demand for USU study programs for the SNBP and SNBT routes.

Students can visit the website and select Universitas Sumatera Utara from the University List option. The Ministry of Education and Culture's website will display USU's capacity data for 2018 through 2022.

USU offers the TALENTA USU Superior Achievement-Based Selection (SBPU) New Students Admission pathway for informational purposes. This prospective student screening method targets those with academic and non-academic abilities. This SBPU program is a new admissions pathway at USU approved by Rector's Decision Letter. SBPU has a capacity of around 0.5% on its own. The website https://www.usu.ac.id/id/penerimaan provides access to all information regarding USU's new student admissions, including the SMM and SPMPD paths.
