USU and Iqra University Establish Academic Partnership

Published by

Bambang Riyanto

Wednesday, 07 June 2023

Iqra University will be an academic partner of the Universitas Sumatera Utara and involved in various International Exchange Programs conducted by USU.

HUMAS USU - A delegation from the Universitas Sumatera Utara, led by Prof. Dr. Poppy Anjelisa Hasibuan, S.Si, M.Si, Apt, Vice Rector III, visited Iqra University in Karachi, Pakistan. Accompanying the delegation was Dr. Juni Kuncoro Hadiningrat, the Indonesian Consul General in Karachi. The USU team, which included Dr. Edy Ikhsan, SH, MA, Vice Rector I, Dr. Cut Fatimah Zuhra, S.Si, M.Si, Vice Dean I of FMIPA, and Dr. Rudy Sofyan, SS, M.Hum, Head of the International Affairs Office, received a warm welcome from Dr. Zaki Rashidi, the Director of Bahria Town Campus, along with the Corporate Liaison and International Linkages staff and other officials from Iqra University.

Following the meeting, the delegation was given a tour of the Iqra University campus. They had the opportunity to visit computer laboratories, the Business Incubation Center, the Fashion and Textile Design Lab, and the Digital Content Creation Lab. Iqra University will serve as an academic partner of the Universitas Sumatera Utara and will be involved in various international exchange programs conducted by USU.
