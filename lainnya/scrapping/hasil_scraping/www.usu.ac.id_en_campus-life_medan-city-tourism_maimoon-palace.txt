Maimoon Palace

Maimoon Palace, a historical tourist destination in Medan City, offers a rich experience with its distinctive architectural beauty, stunning yellow color, and priceless cultural heritage. Located on Jalan Brigadir Jenderal Katamso, Aur, Medan Maimun, the palace dazzles with its graceful Malay-Islamic architectural style, complete with exquisite embellishments that reflect the luxury of a bygone era. Visitors can explore the beauty of Maimoon Palace every day from 08.00 to 17.00 WIB with an entrance ticket costing IDR10,000 for adults and IDR5,000 for children. Take advantage of this opportunity to gain an in-depth experience of local culture and tradition.

Other Information

Istana Maium is a historical tourist destination in Medan City known for its architectural beauty and priceless exhibits of Malay-Islamic cultural heritage, which also reflects the cultural opulence of the past.
