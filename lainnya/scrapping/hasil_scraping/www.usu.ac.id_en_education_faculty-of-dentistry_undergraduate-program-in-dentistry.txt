Undergraduate Program in Dentistry
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Undergraduate Program in Dentistry at the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) is a study program focusing on human oral health care, including diagnosing, treating, and preventing dental and oral problems. During the program, students will learn various theories and practices on Basic Dentistry, Dental Conservation, Endodontics, Pediatric Dentistry, Oral Health, Oral Biology, Radiology, Periodontics, Biomedicine, Dental Caries, Dental Tissue Diseases, and other related topics.

This study program aims to become a leader in 2025 in the fields of natural resources, technology, and art with global competitiveness, professionalism, and entrepreneurial spirit. The USU Undergraduate Program in Dentistry graduates are expected to have broad global insights and the ability to compete in the international market. Students who complete this study program will be awarded an undergraduate degree in dentistry (S.K.G.).

Vision of Undergraduate Program in Dentistry

In 2025, the Undergraduate Program in Dentistry will become one that excels in natural resources, technology, and art with the ability to compete globally, be professional, and possess an entrepreneurial spirit.

Mission of Undergraduate Program in Dentistry

Education

To conduct the education process using up-to-date technology and information systems.

Research

To conduct research oriented on the development of science and technology excelling in natural resources, technology, and art for the development of academic, science, and technology beneficial for the public with the ability to compete globally.

Community Service

To conduct professional, integrated, and comprehensive dental and oral services.

BINTANG Characteristics

To form academic personnel with BINTANG characteristics (taqwa to the one and only God in the framework of diversity, innovative with integrity, tough and wise), professional, and possessing entrepreneurship spirit.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
