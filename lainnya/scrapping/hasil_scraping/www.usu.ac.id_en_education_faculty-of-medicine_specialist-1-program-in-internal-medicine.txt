Spesialist-1 Program
Internal Medicine
Universitas Sumatera Utara

With Excellent accreditation, the Specialist Program in Internal Medicine of Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) focuses on educating and treating various internal diseases with coverage of almost all parts of the human body, such as cardiology, endocrinology, and hepatology. Students will receive direct education from expert lecturers and a group of competent individuals with national teaching certificates.

The study program is deeply committed to making students competent, critical-thinking, and empathetic surgeons and organ specialists who can perform their profession well and fulfill their societal responsibilities. Students who have completed this study program will hold the Internal Medicine Specialist (Sp.P.D.) degree.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
