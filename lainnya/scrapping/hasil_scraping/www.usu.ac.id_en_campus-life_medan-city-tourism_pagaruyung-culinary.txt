Pagaruyung Culinary

In the past few decades, Pagaruyung Medan has become a major destination for Indian food lovers in the city. This place is not only favored by the residents of Kampung Keling but also many tourists from outside the city are interested in visiting it. Pagaruyung Culinary is located on Jalan KH. Zainul Arifin, Petisah Tengah Urban Village, Medan City. Despite being among tall buildings, Pagaruyung has a spacious dining area, creating an attractive and cozy atmosphere. Established in 2008, Pagaruyung was built to build the tourist brand of "The Little India," which includes the place itself, Shri Mariamman Temple, and Madras Village. Here, you can find a wide array of enticing food options. You can find it all, from delicious kebuli rice, specialty martabak, roti jala with chicken or mutton curry, and mouth-watering samin rice to crispy roti cane at Pagaruyung Culinary. Despite being in operation for a dozen years, Pagaruyung is well aware of the Indonesian palate when it comes to food. Hence, you can also find local food served here. Pagaruyung is dominated by Indian Tamil food vendors, although some indigenous vendors are also available. This Indian food spot opens at 4pm, and food stalls showcase their specialties late into the night. Enjoy an enjoyable culinary experience at Pagaruyung Medan, where delicious dishes and a lively atmosphere make this a can't-miss culinary destination for Indian food lovers and visitors looking for a place to eat around Sun Plaza.

Other Information

Pagaruyung Medan has long been the go-to destination for Indian food lovers in Medan. From nasi kebuli to roti cane, Pagaruyung is dominated by ethnic Tamil vendors and opens from 4pm.
