The Faculty of Vocational Education at the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) was established in 2022 to provide vocational higher education that emphasizes mastery of applied expertise. The Faculty of Vocational Education combines various branches of science, including engineering, economics and business, agriculture, cultural sciences, and social and political sciences. Supported by the best lecturers and teaching staff, USU Vocational provides various applied science education programs to help students' careers. Through this approach, students are equipped with an in-depth understanding of relevant problems and trained to find practical solutions, preparing them to enter the workforce.

Vision ofÂ Faculty of Vocational Education

Mission of Faculty of Vocational Education

Education

To organize vocational higher education, which serves as a platform for character development and human resource professionalism with an educational focus that emphasizes competencies aligned with the evolving needs of industry and the professional field.

Research

To create graduates with high expertise and international standards which can support the growth of the national industry in order to compete in the global industrial environment.

Service

To implement, develop, and improve education, research and community service programs in the vocational field in order to improve academic quality by developing advanced vocational science, which is beneficial to the industrial field.
