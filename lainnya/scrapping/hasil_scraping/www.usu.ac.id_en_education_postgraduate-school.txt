The Postgraduate School (SPs) of Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) is an institution that organizes several postgraduate study programs independently within the University of Sumatera Utara. SPs USU offers six graduate programs, including the Master Program in Management, Master Program in Property Valuation and Management, Master Program in Natural Resources and Environmental Management, Master Program in Regional and Rural Development Planning, Doctoral Program in Natural Resources and Environmental Management, and Doctoral Program in Regional Planning. SPs USU has achieved superior accreditation and is committed to providing the best education that focuses on finding alternatives to solving actual problems based on scientific studies. We are highly committed to producing professional and knowledgeable alums by providing internships or research opportunities at leading companies at home and abroad.

Mission of Postgraduate School

Education

To organize autonomy-based postgraduate education, which serves as a platform for character development and human resource professionalism based on empowerment which contains the spirit of democratization of education that acknowledges diversity with an educational orientation that emphasizes aspects of finding alternative solutions to actual problems based on scientific, moral, and conscience studies.

Research

To create postgraduate students who act as agents of change as a force of modernization in the life of the society at large, who have solid scientific competence, relevance and competitiveness, and who behave with ethical scholarship.

Service

To implement, develop, and improve education, research culture and community service programs in order to improve academic quality by developing advanced knowledge, which is beneficial for changing the lives of the community at large for the better.
