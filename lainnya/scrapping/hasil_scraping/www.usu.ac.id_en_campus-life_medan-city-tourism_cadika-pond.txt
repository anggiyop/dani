Cadika Pond

Cadika Pond is a hidden gem in the middle of Medan in Taman Cadika Medan, Jalan Karya Wisata, Medan Johor District, Medan City. As one of the low-budget tourist destinations, this pond is a favorite among the youngsters of Medan City. Upon entering the park, visitors will immediately be mesmerized by the pond's beauty, surrounded by various shady and flowering trees. Enjoying the charm of nature at Cadika Pond is an unforgettable experience. Visitors can sit back and relax in the shade of the trees that provide pleasant shade. The peaceful and beautiful atmosphere around the pond creates the perfect opportunity for visitors to unburden their minds from the routine and noise of the city. If you are planning to spend time with your family, friends, or loved ones, Cadika Pond is the perfect choice. Many visitors deliberately come to this park, especially in the morning or evening, to enjoy the tranquility and soothing peace. So, if you are looking for a place to relax and enjoy the beauty of nature in the middle of Medan, don't miss the opportunity to visit Cadika Pond. Together with the soothing atmosphere, this pond will bring precious moments in your leisure time.
