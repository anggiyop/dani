Benefits Kampus Mengajar

For Students

Direct involvement as teacher partners, school principals, and education personnel in the target schools in designing and implementing teaching strategies in schools.
Contribute directly as agents of change in Indonesian education.
Hone leadership skills, problem-solving abilities, communication skills, analytical thinking, creativity, and innovation directly from the field.
Expand networking and build friendships with fellow students in the placement schools.

Requirements Kampus Mengajar

For Students

Active student status, both in academic and vocational programs
Enrolled in an accredited study program at either a State University (PTN) or a Private University (PTS) under the supervision of the Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology
Must be at least in the fourth semester during the implementation of the program in the even semester of the academic year 2022/2023
Minimum cumulative GPA of 3.00
Upload the Letter of Integrity Pact
Obtain a Letter of Recommendation from the university's leadership, signed by the Vice Dean, at least
Upload the transcript of grades (cumulative GPA)
