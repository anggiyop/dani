Doctoral Program In Industrial Engineering Science
Doctoral Program
Industrial Engineering Science
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Doctoral Program in Industrial Engineering Science is an advanced study program from the Master's Program in Industrial Engineering that offers advanced deepening in the field of industrial engineering. Through a comprehensive curriculum, students will develop a deep understanding of theory and practice in operations management, systems analysis, production engineering, and industrial business strategy.

In addition, the program emphasizes innovative and applicable research in addressing modern industrial challenges. Students are directed to conduct research that is of high quality and has a real impact in improving industrial efficiency, productivity, and sustainability. With a focus on developing sustainable and industry-oriented solutions, graduates of this program are expected to become leaders in their fields and make meaningful contributions in facing complex industrial challenges in the future.

Vision of Doctoral Program in Industrial Engineering Science

To become a leading Doctoral Program in Industrial Engineering with high competence in integrated systems engineering, both nationally and globally.

Mission of Doctoral Program in Industrial Engineering Science

Education

To provide a Doctoral Education in Industrial Engineering that fosters character and professionalism in human resources, preparing them to excel nationally and globally.

Graduates

To produce professional graduates in the field of industrial engineering with an educational orientation that emphasizes the aspects of searching for new knowledge, innovation, and solving actual problems based on scientific competence and relevance, as well as behaving with ethical scholarship.

Research and Community Service

To implement, develop, and improve research, research culture, and community service programs in order to develop superior knowledge and contribute to better sustainable industrial development.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
