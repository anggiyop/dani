Pugun Tano Extract Capsule
Capsule
Product Description

This innovation is related to the capsule content formula of pugun tano leaf extract, which is free-flowing. Preparation of capsule contents includes preparation of pugun tano leaf extract, drying of pugun tano leaf extract, granulation, and formulation of capsule mass. The superiority of this capsule content is that it has free-flowing properties characterized by a Carr Index below 16%, a stationary angle between 25
-30
, and a flow time below 10 seconds so that the contents of this capsule can then be inserted easily into the capsule shell.
