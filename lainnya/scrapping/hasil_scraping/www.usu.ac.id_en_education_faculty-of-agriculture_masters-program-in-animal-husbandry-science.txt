Master's Program In Animal Husbandry Science
Master Program
Animal Husbandry Science
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Master's Program in Animal Husbandry Science is a study program that focuses on teaching the science of raising, managing, developing technology, and marketing livestock and livestock products. The program covers various aspects of science, including agricultural socioeconomics, nutrition, and livestock products. Since its establishment in 1981, the program has successfully produced quality graduates and skilled livestock experts in various parts of Indonesia.

Managed by qualified lecturers and experts in their fields, the USU Master's Program in Animal Husbandry is committed to presenting an integrated curriculum and the best teaching materials to guide students to become skilled and innovative individuals in the livestock sector. Graduates of this study program will earn a Master of Animal Science (M.Pt.) degree upon successful completion of this study program.

Vision of Master's Program in Animal Husbandry Science

Master of Animal Science Study Program that is superior and able to compete at the global level in the development of integrative animal husbandry based on plantation and agricultural resources in 2021

Mission of Master's Program in Animal Husbandry Science

Superior

Organizing education and teaching in the Master of Animal Science Study Program that is superior, competitive and able to develop integrative animal science based on plantation and agricultural resources.

Innovative

Develop innovative programs in the implementation of research and community service that can support the development of knowledge and technology in the field of integrative animal husbandry based on plantation and agricultural resources.

Research and Service

Aligning between education and research and public service to be applied to the community and published scientifically in the field of animal husbandry.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
