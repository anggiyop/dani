About Tenis Meja USU Student Activity Unit

The Table Tennis Student Activity Unit (UKM Tenis Meja) at USU serves as a platform for students interested in and talented in table tennis. This organization was established to enhance playing skills, foster a spirit of sportsmanship, and build athletic integrity among students, particularly in table tennis. Regular activities conducted by members include scheduled training sessions, internal matches among students, and participation in external competitions at regional and national levels. The existence of the USU Table Tennis UKM reflects Universitas Sumatera Utaraâ€™s commitment to supporting sports achievements and promoting the physical well-being of its students. Students interested in joining can visit the official Instagram account of the USU Table Tennis UKM for more information.
