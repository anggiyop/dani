BSI Scholarship Inspirasi 2024
BSI Scholarship Inspirasi 2024

Scholarship Timeline

Open Recruitment: September 2, 2024
Close Recruitment: October 13, 2024
Administrative Selection: October 14 - 23, 2024
Psychological Assessment: October 26 - November 5, 2024
Final Announcement: November 12, 2024

 

Scholarship Benefits

Tuition fee assistance of IDR 3,000,000 for 6 semesters.
Leadership and character development training.
Islamic finance workshops.
Social Project program.
Internship opportunities in the Islamic financial industry.

 

Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

Must be categorized as underprivileged, proven with an SKTM (Certificate of Financial Hardship).
Active undergraduate students in their 2nd year or 3rd semester from designated universities.
Minimum GPA of 3.00 on a 4.00 scale.
Not currently receiving a similar scholarship from another institution.

 

Required Documents

Formal photo (wearing a black blazer/jacket with a turquoise green background, color code: #009B97).
Scanned National ID Card (KTP).
Scanned Student ID Card (KTM).
Scanned Family Card (KK).
Scanned latest GPA transcript.
Scanned latest SKTM from the village office.
Scanned certificates of achievement (up to 3).
Scanned proof of electricity bill payments for the last 3 months.
Scanned proof of tuition fee payment for the last semester.
Photos of the house (front view and living room).
