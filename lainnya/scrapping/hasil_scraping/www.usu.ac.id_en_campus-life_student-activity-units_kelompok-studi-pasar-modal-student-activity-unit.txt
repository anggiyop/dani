About Kelompok Studi Pasar Modal Student Activity Unit

The Kelompok Studi Pasar Modal (KSPM) at USU is a platform for students interested in investment and the stock market. This organization aims to enhance students' investment knowledge and skills through various educational and practical activities. Members of KSPM USU regularly participate in training sessions, seminars, stock trading simulations, and capital market competitions at both national and international levels.
