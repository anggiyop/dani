Accredited Good

Vision of Applied Bachelor's Degree Program in Digital Office Administration

To become an organizer of an Applied Bachelor’s Degree Program in the field of Digital Office Administration is noble, independent, and highly competitive at the regional, national, and global levels.

Mission of Applied Bachelor's Degree Program in Digital Office Administration

Education

Organizing applied bachelor’s degree programs that produce professional, moral, and ethical graduates based on the BINTANG value system (Pious, Innovative, Strong, and Wise).

Collaboration

Provide excellent service to students and expand cooperation networks with other stakeholders.

Research

Carry out and apply research studies in Digital Office Administration to contribute to local, national, and international communities.

Community Service

Carry out community service in the field of Digital Office Administration by expanding partnerships with various related parties.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
