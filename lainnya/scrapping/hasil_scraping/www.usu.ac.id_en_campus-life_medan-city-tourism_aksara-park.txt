Aksara Park

Aksara Park, a theme park paradise amidst the glittering city of Medan, is here as an unmissable night entertainment destination. Located on Aksara Street, Pahlawan, Medan Perjuangan Sub-district, Medan City, North Sumatra, this place serves unlimited fun and enjoyment. Visitors will be greeted by a variety of exciting game rides as well as delicious food in the food court and street food. Not only that but Instagramable photo spots that are popular among teenagers are also scattered here, ensuring that every precious moment can be captured beautifully. Aksara Park is not just a place for a pleasant evening stroll but an amusement park that is full of fun. There are a variety of rides suitable for kids and teens alike, from the heart-pounding Ferris wheel to the giggle-inducing merry-go-round. For those who like a challenge, ombak banyu and kora-kora are ready to give you a heart-pounding thrill. There's no reason to feel bored or uninspired at night because Aksara Park is the answer. Experience unparalleled fun and excitement here, savor every exciting moment with friends and family, and take home beautiful memories of this mesmerizing amusement park.

Other Information

Aksara Park is a nightlife destination in the center of Medan that offers a variety of rides, food delights, and photo spots that are popular among young people.
