Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Master's Program in Pharmaceutical Science was established in 2000 and has achieved a Very Good (A) accreditation rating. As an advanced level education program from the Undergraduate Program in Pharmacy, the main focus of our program is on pharmaceutical science, clinical, community, and pharmaceutical technology.

The commitment of the Master's Program in Pharmaceutical Science is to produce graduates who are innovative, tested, qualified, and have high integrity with international standards. The students are assisted by teaching staff who are nationally certified, widely experienced, and experts in the field of pharmacy. With the availability of 14 laboratories and 2 supporting units, graduates of this program are expected to smoothly manage, lead, and develop research in various fields of clinical pharmacy, pharmaceutical science, and pharmaceutical technology.

Vision of Master's Program in Pharmaceutical Science

To become a Master's Program in Pharmaceutical Sciences that excels in the fields of Science, Clinical and Community Pharmacy, and Pharmaceutical Technology with local characteristics of international standards in 2024.

Mission of Master's Program in Pharmaceutical Science

Education

Organizing an innovative and tested research-based Master's Program in Pharmaceutical Sciences and quality education in the fields of Science, Clinical and Community Pharmacy, and Pharmaceutical Technology to educate and empower the intellectual abilities of graduates in the field of pharmacy and maintain national integrity and international standards.

Research

Conducting research characterized by excellence in the fields of Pharmaceutical Science, Clinical and Community, and Pharmaceutical Technology that supports the development of research and technology based on Tropical Science and Medicine, Disease Priority, and Natural Resources.

Community Service

Implementing community service based on research results in the form of dissemination and community empowerment to improve welfare.

Diversification

Increasing the capacity and carrying capacity and diversifying the USU Master Program in Pharmaceutical Sciences.

Partnership

Expanding partnerships with various domestic and foreign institutions that support the implementation of the three pillars of higher education.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
