Diploma Program
Statistics
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Diploma Program in Statistics at the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) has been running since 1993 and focuses on the application of statistics in various fields. This program focuses on data analysis, statistical computing, and surveying and sampling in accordance with industry needs.

With the support of the best lecturers who have high dedication in teaching, our study program is committed to producing graduates who are experts in computing, professional, and proficient in information technology. Graduates of this program have a wide range of job opportunities, including as marketing surveyors and statistical data analysts.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market.
