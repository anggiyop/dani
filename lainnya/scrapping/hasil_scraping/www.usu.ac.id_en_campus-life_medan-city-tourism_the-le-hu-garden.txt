The Le Hu Garden

The Le Hu Garden is a family tourism destination that carries the concept of a flower garden nearby Medan. Located in the suburbs of Medan, precisely between the borders of Marindal, Patumbak, and Deli Tua, this garden offers a natural atmosphere that seems to take us to the countryside. Aside from being an ample open space, The Le Hu Garden has various facilities that pamper visitors. Cases and shacks are available for rent, so visitors can enjoy delicious food and drinks while enjoying the garden's beauty. With so many options available, visitors can explore the garden area leisurely. The Le Hu Garden is a highly recommended place for those who want to experience the rural atmosphere around Medan City without traveling all the way to the village. For more information on operating hours and admission fees, please visit their Instagram account at @thelehugarden.
