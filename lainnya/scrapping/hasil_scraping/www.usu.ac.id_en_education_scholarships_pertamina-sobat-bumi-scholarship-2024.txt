Pertamina Sobat Bumi Scholarship 2024
Pertamina Sobat Bumi Scholarship 2024

Scholarship Category

Regular Scholarships for Undergraduate Students  
Affirmation Scholarships for Undergraduate Students located in the operating area of PT Pertamina (Persero)
Vocational Scholarships for Diploma Students (D3/D4)

 

Scholarship Benefits

Tuition fee aid 
Living allowance 
Capacity Building (Sobat Bumi Action, Catalyst for New and Renewable Energy, Activation of Sekolah Energi Berdikari, Nationalism of Love for the Country, Voluntary Days Perwira Pertamina) 
Connection with students throughout Indonesia, Pertamina Group, and Pertamina Foundation fostered.

 

Scholarship Requirements

Indonesian citizen and active student at least in semester 2 and maximum in semester 6 with the last GPA > 3.00 
Students of Sobat Bumi Scholarship partner universities 
Not currently receiving scholarships from any party (period January-December 2024) 
Never involved in drug abuse and criminal acts 
Having TOEFL and IELTS certificates is a plus
Making a paper with the theme “My contribution to the achievement of Net Zero Emission”, at least 3 (three) A4 pages with 1.5 spacing.
