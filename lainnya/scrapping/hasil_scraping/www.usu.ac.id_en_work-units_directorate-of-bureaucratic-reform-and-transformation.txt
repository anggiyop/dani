About Directorate of Bureaucratic Reform and Transformation

The Directorate of Bureaucratic Reform and Transformation of USU was established in 2022, precisely in May 2022. In its implementation, this directorate carries out the mission of implementing the USU Bureaucratic Reform Roadmap Book. The performance of the roadmap is carried out together with the formation of the USU Bureaucratic Reform Team, which carries out all the achievement activities of the roadmap. In addition, the Integrity Zone is implemented with the directorate as a companion for implementation in each faculty and school.
