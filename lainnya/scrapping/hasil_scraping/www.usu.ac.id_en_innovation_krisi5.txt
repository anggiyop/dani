KRISI5
Facial Cream
Product Description

A special night facial cream made from pomegranate leaves and betel leaves to reduce acne and premature aging. The results prove that after 4 weeks, using a combination cream from ethanol extracts of Punica granatum L and Piper betle L leaves shows its effectiveness as anti-aging with increased skin moisture content, smoother skin, smaller pores, reduced blemishes, and reduced wrinkle depth. The combination of ethanol extracts from pomegranate and betel leaves has acne activity against Propionibacterium acne.
