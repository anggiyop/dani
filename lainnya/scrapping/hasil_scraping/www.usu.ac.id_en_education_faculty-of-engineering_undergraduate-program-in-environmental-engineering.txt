Undergraduate Program In Environmental Engineering
Undergraduate Program
Environmental Engineering
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Undergraduate Program in Environmental Engineering is a study program that aims to train students in sustainable environmental management and maintenance. Focusing on the interaction between humans and the environment, students will learn about technologies and strategies to minimize the negative impacts of the industrial sector on ecosystems and improve environmental quality.

With a comprehensive curriculum and supported by faculty members who are experts in their fields, students will engage in field projects and practicums to apply the knowledge they learn to real-life situations. Graduates of this study program are expected to become agents of change who can contribute to maintaining environmental sustainability and building a society that is more aware of the importance of nature conservation.
