Martabak Piring Murni

Martabak Piring Murni, one of the favorite culinary delights in Medan, has been around since 1990. They offer two types of martabak, those with thick and thin dough. Visitors also have a choice of various topping flavors, such as chocolate, cheese, and peanut. The specialty of Martabak Piring Murni is its traditional baking process. The floury batter is poured onto a tin plate and then grilled with charcoal coals without a stove. This martabak is affordable, ranging from 4,000 to 8,000 rupiah. To enjoy this delicious food, visitors can visit Martabak Piring Murni on Jalan Tjong Yong Hian from 18:00 WIB to 23:30 WIB daily.
