The Faculty of Public Health (FKM) of Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) has been at the forefront of the health field since its establishment in 1993, offering 2 undergraduate programs, 1 master's program, and 1 doctoral program, all well-accredited. The USU Faculty of Public Health is committed to the study of disease prevention and management at the individual level as well as providing an effective and interactive education for students. Managed by experienced experts in public health and supported by adequate facilities and an integrated curriculum, we hope our students can contribute to realizing our vision and mission to produce a generation of global health leaders who have innovative ideas as well as are able to make significant scientific breakthroughs, while actively playing a role in improving the welfare and health of the people in Indonesia. Graduates of the Faculty of Public Health have a wide range of career opportunities, including in various industries and private organizations, such as in quality, health, safety, and environment (QHSE), and many more.

Mission of Faculty of Public Health

Partnerships

To expand cooperation networks with national and international universities, governments, industries, professions, and organizations to support the quality of education, research, and community service.

Education

To organize education that is of quality, responsive, innovative, independent, and based on modern learning technology.

Research

Service

To organize community service to enhance the quality of public health in tropical regions.
