Physics
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Diploma Program in Physics is a study program that provides vocational education in the scientific field of microcontroller/microprocessor-based physics.

The Diploma Program in Physics is staffed with exceptional individuals dedicated and certified to teach nationally. It is supported by the best facilities and infrastructure, including six laboratories that can help the effectiveness of education. Our study program is highly committed to producing intermediate experts with professional skills in microprocessor-based instrumentation use and maintenance, as well as basics with graduate profiles as instrumentation technicians, instrumentation analysts, instrumentation supervisor assistants, and instrumentation designers.

Need more information? visit the faculty website, or contact us via e-mail.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
