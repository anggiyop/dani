About Technical Implementation Unit of Integrated Research Laboratory

The Integrated Research Laboratory Technical Implementation Unit (UPT LPT) of USU is a laboratory unit directly responsible to the Rector and coordinated by the Vice Rector III for Research, Community Service, and Cooperation. UPT LPT USU provides services for research purposes within Universitas Sumatera Utara, including faculties, study programs, research institutions, Centers of Excellence for Science and Technology/Innovation (PUI), and other work units. UPT LPT USU plays a role in accelerating research implementation in an optimal and standardized manner as well as accommodating the interests of the USU Institute for Innovation and Technology Business Incubation, with the goal of producing innovative products that can be successfully monetized to support the growth of innovation within the environment of Universitas Sumatera Utara.
