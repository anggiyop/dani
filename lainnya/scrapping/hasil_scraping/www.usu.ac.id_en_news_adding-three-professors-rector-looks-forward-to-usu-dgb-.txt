USU PR - Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) again inaugurated three professors who took place at the USU Student Center on Tuesday (15/08/2023). The professors who were inaugurated directly by USU Rector Prof. Dr. Muryanto Amin S.Sos., M.Si., namely Prof. Dr. Delvian, S.P, M.P from the Faculty of Forestry, Prof. Dr. Ayodhia Pitaloka Pasaribu, M.Ked (Ped), SpA (K), Ph.D (CTM) from the Faculty of Medicine, and Prof. Dr. Miswar Budi Mulya, S.Si, M.Si from the Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences. In his remarks, the USU Rector congratulated the three professors who had been inaugurated and expected the innovative work of the USU Professors in supporting USU as a world-class university, especially in organizing to strengthen the educational ecosystem. "I congratulate the professors who have been inaugurated and their families.
