Pharmacy
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Undergraduate Program in Pharmacy (PSSF) was established in 1969. Currently, the program has been equipped with 9 comfortable laboratories that greatly support the learning process intensively and effectively.

Its commitment to becoming a center of higher education that excels academically and can compete at the global level is reflected in the provision of regular classes for domestic students and international classes for participants from abroad. Supported by outstanding individuals with national teaching certifications, our teaching staff are able to guide students to be ready to work in various fields of pharmacy, including in pharmacies, hospitals, pharmaceutical industry, food and cosmetics, drug distribution, and also in the government sector.

Vision of Undergraduate Program in Pharmacy

To become a leading international pharmacy undergraduate study program that emphasizes local excellence in 2024.

Mission of Undergraduate Program in Pharmacy

Education

Organizing quality undergraduate pharmacy education.

Research

Conducting research in the field of pharmaceutical science technology and clinical and community pharmacy that is innovative, competitive, and sustainable that benefits society and the development of science and technology.

Community Service

Implementing community service in the field of pharmacy that aims to improve the welfare and health of the community.

Partnership

Organizing and developing partnerships and networks of study programs in the fields of education, research, and community service.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
