Van Deventer-Maas Indonesia Scholarship 2024/2025
Van Deventer-Maas Indonesia Scholarship 2024/2025

Recipient Criteria

Bachelor's (S1) and Diploma 4 (D4) students in their 2nd–6th semester, and Diploma 3 (D3) students in their 2nd–4th semester
Active students of Universitas Sumatera Utara
Maximum age of 27 years old
From an economically disadvantaged family (proven by a Certificate of Economic Hardship - SKTM)
GPA ≥ 3.00
Actively involved in organizations or communities is preferred
Fluent in English is preferred
Not currently receiving any other scholarship

 

Assistance Components

Monthly allowance of IDR 750,000 (granted until the end of the study period, 6 semesters for D3 and 8 semesters for D4/S1)
TOEFL bonus of IDR 400,000 and On-time Graduation bonus of IDR 700,000
Opportunity to participate in free personal capacity-building training

Document Submission

For students who pass the First Stage, please submit the original documents according to the Required Documents list in a Red Folder. Documents should be delivered to the DITMAWALUMNI USU Office during working hours from March 12 to 15, 2024. Read more about the registration process in the Scholarship Manual Book.
