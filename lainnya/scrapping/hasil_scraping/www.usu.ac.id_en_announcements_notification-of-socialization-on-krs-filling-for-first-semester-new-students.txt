Number: 18661/UN5.1.R1/SPB/2024Attachment: 1 (one) setSubject: Notification of Socialization on KRS Filling for First-Semester New Students

Dear Dean of the Faculty of ………………,Universitas Sumatera Utarain Medan

Regarding the letter from the Head of the Information System Center Number: 17223/UN5.4.2.K/SIM/2024 dated May 28, 2024, on the subject mentioned above, we kindly request your assistance in informing new students admitted through the National Selection Based on Achievement (SNBP) 2024 to attend the Information System Center (PSI) on:

Date: Monday to Thursday, July 8 - 11, 2024Time: 08.00 AM - 04.00 PM WIBLocation: Computer Laboratory Room, PSI, Jl. Universitas No. 9, USU Campus

Additionally, we inform you that new students admitted through the National Selection Based on Test (SNBT) will receive KRS filling guidance from PSI after completing their medical check-up at the Polyclinic from July 22 to 26, 2024.

Thank you for your attention and cooperation.
