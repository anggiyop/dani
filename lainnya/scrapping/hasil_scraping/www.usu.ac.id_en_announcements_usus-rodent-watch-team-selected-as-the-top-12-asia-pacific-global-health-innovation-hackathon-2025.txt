USU's Rodent Watch team selected as the top 12 Asia-Pacific Global Health Innovation Hackathon 2025
 

Published by

Bambang Riyanto

Published at

Saturday, 18 January 2025

Documents
Description

The entire academic community of the Faculty of Medicine of USU would like to congratulate and wish success to the Rodent Watch Team from the Universitas Sumatera Utara, who were selected as the top 12 in the Asia-Pacific Global Health Innovation Hackathon 2025 organized by SingHealth.

dr. Sunna Vyatra Hutagalung, MS, Ph.d, from the Faculty of Medicine, Universitas Sumatera Utara led the team.

This research has a big vision:“The emergence of zoonotic diseases emphasizes the need for innovative approaches to enhance disease prevention. This work aims to integrate AI techniques with conventional disease control strategies to understand, predict, and mitigate zoonotic disease's impact.”
