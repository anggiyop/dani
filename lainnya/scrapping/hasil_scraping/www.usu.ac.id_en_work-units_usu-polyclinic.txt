About USU Polyclinic

The USU Polyclinic is a healthcare service implementation unit for the Universitas Sumatera Utara academic community, especially students, lecturers, and staff of the USU campus. The main task of the USU Polyclinic is to provide outpatient healthcare services supported by general practitioners, dentists, and specialists in ophthalmology, otorhinolaryngology, dermatology and venereology, and pediatrics. With a focus on comprehensive healthcare services, the USU Polyclinic is also equipped with basic clinical laboratory facilities and a pharmacy.
