About Marching Band USU Student Activity Unit

Marching Band USU is one of the student activity units at USU that focuses on the field of music. Established in 2012, this student activity unit has participated in several national competitions and has achieved prestigious accomplishments in Indonesia, such as the Bandung Marching Band Championship X 2019. The Marching Band USU Student Activity Unit is a suitable platform for students to experience real organizational work and the arts as well as sports.
