Ucok Durian

Ucok Durian, the most famous durian outlet in the Medan City, is a favorite culinary destination for domestic and foreign tourists with a jargon that echoes, "Don't Say You've Been To Medan If You Haven't Stopped By Ucok Durian." In fact, even President Joko Widodo has visited this bustling culinary attraction. With 40 years of experience, Ucok Durian serves the best quality fresh durian and a variety of delicious durian preparations, such as soft and sweet durian pancakes and appetizing durian ice cream. Come visit Ucok Durian and savor the unparalleled deliciousness of Medan durian.

Other Information
