Finance
Universitas Sumatera Utara

The Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Diploma Program in Finance is a financial education institution that excels in applied finance.

Our study program is supported by the best teaching staff who are competent and certified to teach nationally, resulting in a comfortable and practical learning atmosphere in learning applied finance. The Diploma Program in Finance is highly committed to producing professional, objective, independent, and qualified finance professionals. Graduates of our study program are expected to pursue careers as professionals in finance, banking, and taxation, as well as stockbrokers in private companies, government agencies, and self-owned businesses in Indonesia.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
