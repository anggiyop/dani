USU PR - Faculty of Public Health (FKM) and Faculty of Social and Political Sciences (FISIP) Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) in collaboration with the USU Bureaucratic Reform and Transformation Team in carrying out socialization activities for the Corruption-Free Regional Integrity Zone (ZI-WBK). This socialization activity was held at the FKM Auditorium and FISIP Auditorium on Monday (04/07/2022).

Director of the Directorate of Bureaucratic Reform and Transformation at USU, Dr. Nurman Achmad S.Sos., M.Soc.Sc said he was happy because FKM had started implementing ZI-WBK to make the FKM environment better and free from corruption. And the USU Bureaucratic Reform and Transformation team will assist FKM in implementing the ZI-WBK. This ZI-WBK will not harm the lecturers and staff of FKM but make FKM a faculty that has a neat and orderly environment.

"Today FKM has started to implement ZI-WBK within FKM so that it is free from corruption and becomes a neat and orderly environment," he said.

Dr. Nurman also said that the USU Bureau of Bureaucratic Reform and Transformation Team would also carry out the socialization of the ZI-WBK in all faculties and one of them in FISIP, and would provide assistance to FISIP in implementing the ZI-WBK.

Dean of FKM Prof. Dr. Dra. Ida Yustina, M.Sc. said FKM will strengthen the understanding of lecturers and staff to implement ZI-WBK within FKM. His party also committed to improving services and hoped they can continuously improve themselves so they were free from corruption-free areas and clean in serving.

"I hope that FKM can improve everything so that it becomes a corruption-free and clean area in serving," he said.

While the On-Duty Dean of USU's FISIP Dr. Hatta Ridho S.Sos., MSP said that FISIP had formed a team to enforce a zone of territorial integrity free from corruption. It was hoped that the academic community would be able to change by improving services and can immediately implement this activity so that the FISIP environment is free from corruption.

"All academics will improve services for service users at FISIP," he said
