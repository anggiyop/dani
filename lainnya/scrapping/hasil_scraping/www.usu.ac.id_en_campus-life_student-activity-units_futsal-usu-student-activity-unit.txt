About Futsal USU Student Activity Unit

Futsal USU Student Activity Unit was established a few years ago and has become one of the most renowned and favorite organizations at Universitas Sumatera Utara. This student activity unit was founded as a means of support from both students and the academic community at USU to uphold the important values of physical health and sportsmanship at Universitas Sumatera Utara. Futsal USU Student Activity Unit serves as a platform for USU students who have an interest and talent in futsal to develop their skills and contribute to USU. The Futsal USU Student Activity Unit team is already well-known in the national student league and has achieved significant success in various championships. To this day, this student activity unit actively searches for and selects young talents within the campus, making it highly recommended that new USU students register in this student activity unit.
