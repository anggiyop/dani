USU Establishes MoU with PT. Pos Indonesia
Â 

Published by

Bambang Riyanto

Published at

Tuesday, 03 August 2021

MEDAN - USU PR: Rector of the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Dr. Muryanto Amin, S.Sos., M.Si., signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) with PT Pos Indonesia (Persero), Thursday (1/7/2021).

The signing of the cooperation agreement covers the scope of the education sector, including the implementation of the Program Merdeka Belajar Kampus Merdeka, research, development and innovation, and community service. The activity took place in the rector's meeting room, USU Central Administration Bureau Building, 3rd floor, Medan.

Present from PT Pos Indonesia (Persero) Head of Regional 1 PT Pos Indonesia North Sumatra - Aceh Dino Ariyadi S.E., M.M., Regional 1 Financial Services Deputy Selo Edi, S.T., M.Kom., Deputy Head of SPI Reg 1 Medan Ahmad Saladin, S.E., Head of Medan Post Office Elan Pramudiansyah, S.E. and Regional Secretariat Manager I Budi Setiawan, SE. While accompanying the USU Rector, USU Vice Rector I Dr. Edy Ikhsan, S.H., M.Hum., USU Vice Rector III Dr Poppy Anjelisa Zaitun Hasibuan, Si., M.Si., Apt., Expert Staff Vice Rector 3 USU Siti Salmiah, drg, Sp KGA, USU IT Faculty Teaching Staff Indra Aulia, S.TI, M.Kom and Head of BP2KM Bureau and USU Cooperation Surya Darma, Sos.

According to Dr Muryanto, the main purpose of building this cooperation is to improve the quality of human resources owned and produced by both institutions, which will later be developed in the form of student and lecturer internship programs. In addition, it also opens wider space for the implementation of various activities that support the development of insight, science and innovation.

The Rector hopes that with the signing of this memorandum of understanding for the next five years, the relationship between the Universitas Sumatera Utara and PT Pos Indonesia can be even closer and able to make a positive contribution to the institutions involved, the nation and the state. Especially in the field of education, especially in carrying out various forms of Merdeka Belajar Kampus Merdeka programs. (RJ)
