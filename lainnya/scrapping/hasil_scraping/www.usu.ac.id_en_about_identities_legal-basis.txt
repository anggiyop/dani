Foundation
Universitas Sumatera Utara
The legal foundation of Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) is a series of legal regulations that govern the establishment, operation and governance of the university.
Law No. 20 of 2003 concerning the National Education System.
Law No. 25 of 2009 concerning Public Services.
Law No. 12 of 2012 concerning Higher Education.
Government Regulation No. 16 of 2014 concerning the Statute of Universitas Sumatera Utara
Government Regulation No. 26 of 2015 concerning the Form and Mechanism of Funding Legal-Entity Higher Education Institute.
Regulation of the Ministry of Education and Culture No. 73 of 2013 concerning the Application of the Indonesian National Qualifications Framework for Higher Education.
Regulation of the Ministry of Education and Culture No. 35 of 2014 concerning the Performance Accountability System within the Ministry of Education and Culture
Regulation of the Ministry of Research, Technology and Higher Education No. 13 of 2015 concerning the Strategic Plan of the Ministry of Research, Technology and Higher Education for 2015-2019.
Regulation of the Ministry of Research, Technology and Higher Education 4 of 2015 concerning National Standards for Higher Education.
Regulation of the Ministry of State Apparatus Utilization and Bureaucratic Reform No. 53 of 2014 concerning Technical Guidelines for Performance Agreements, Performance Reporting, and Review Procedures for Government Agency Performance Reports.
Regulation of USU Board of Trustees No. 4 of 2015 concerning Management of Funding for Universitas Sumatera Utara, which does not come from the government.
Regulation USU Board of Trustees No. 10 of 2015 concerning the Organizational Structure of Universitas Sumatera Utara.

Statutes

The Statute of the Universitas Sumatera Utara is a written regulation that regulates the organization, work procedures, and order in carrying out activities at the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU). This statute is an official document that outlines the organizational structure, duties, authority, and decision-making procedures within the university.

USU Statutes (Government Regulation of the Republic of Indonesia No. 16 of 2014 concerning Statutes of the Universitas Sumatera Utara)
