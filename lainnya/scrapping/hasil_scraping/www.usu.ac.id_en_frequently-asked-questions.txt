Kampus Merdeka

Can I apply for more than one Kampus Merdeka program in the same period?

Students may apply to more than 1 (one) program in the same period, but students can only choose or be active in 1 (one) program in the same period.

Is there a maximum limit for students to qualify and actively participate in activities in the Kampus Merdeka program?

The following are the maximum active provisions registered in the Kampus Merdeka program:

PMM: Maximum active and registered 1 time (1 batch)
MSIB: Maximum active and registered 2 times (2 batches)
Teaching Campus: Maximum active and registered 1 time (1 batch)
Graduation Books

How to get a graduation book at Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU)?

The Universitas Sumatera Utara graduation book is available in digital format. You can download it through the official USU website on the Graduation Book page. After the graduation ceremony, this book will be updated with the latest information on the list of graduates.

What information is contained in the graduation book?

The graduation book contains complete information about the list of graduates, including names, academic degrees obtained, as well as faculties and study programs. In addition, the book also contains information about academic awards and student achievements during their studies.

Is the graduation book printed in physical form?

Currently, the USU graduation book is only available in digital form. This book can be accessed and downloaded for free by graduates and families, as well as the general public through the official USU website.

When will the graduation book available for download after the graduation ceremony?

The graduation book is usually available for download a few days after the graduation ceremony. Announcements regarding the availability of the book will be informed through the official USU website and university social media.

Information Systems

What is the Universitas Sumatera Utara Information System?

The Information System at Universitas Sumatera Utara is a digital platform that integrates various academic, administrative, and operational services to support campus activities effectively. This system allows students, lecturers, and staff to access the required information, such as academic, research, financial, and university administration online.

What information system services are available at Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU)?

Universitas Sumatera Utara provides various information system services to support academic and administrative activities, including the Academic Information System (SIA), e-Learning, Digital Library System, and student portal that allows access to academic information, administration, and learning resources.

How can I access the USU Information System?

You can access the Universitas Sumatera Utara Information System through the page https://satu.usu.ac.id/login using the registered account.

What should I do if I forget my password to log in to the USU information system?

If you forget your password, please use the "Forgot Password" feature available on the Academic Information System portal. You will be asked to enter the registered email, and instructions to reset the password will be sent.

What should I do if I experience technical problems using the information system?

If you experience technical problems, users can contact the helpdesk service via email or phone number provided. This information is usually available on the official website or on the related information system page.

Are there tutorials or user guides for using the information system at USU?

Yes, USU provides tutorials and guides to help users operate various information systems. These tutorials can be in the form of videos or written guides accessed through the official USU website or directly in the information system platform of each service.

Who can access the Information System at USU?

The Information System at USU can be accessed by students, lecturers, staff, and alumni who have access rights. Each user has a personal account used to access information relevant to their role and function at the university.

Education Programs

What levels of education are offered by USU?

USU offers various levels of education programs ranging from Diploma, Bachelor (S1), Master (S2), to Doctorate (S3) programs. In addition, there are also Professional Education and Specialist programs in several fields of study.

Does USU provide international class education programs?

Yes, USU offers international class programs in several faculties, especially in the fields of Communication Science, Sociology, Social Anthropology, and Law. USU proudly hosts students from various parts of the world who choose to pursue education in the USU International Class Program.

Does USU provide scholarships for its students?

USU provides various types of scholarships, both from government and private institutions. These scholarships are offered based on academic achievements, economic backgrounds, and student social contributions. Further information on scholarships can be obtained on the USU scholarship page.

How to apply for USU education programs?

To apply for USU education programs, prospective students can follow the admission pathways through SNBP, SNBT, and Independent Exams. Complete information on registration procedures can be accessed through the official USU website in the "New Student Admission" section.

What study programs are offered at Universitas Sumatera Utara?

USU has various study programs spread across 16 faculties, ranging from health sciences, engineering, social humanities, to science and technology. A complete list of study programs can be found on the official USU education program page.

Student Activity Units

What is UKM?

Student Activity Units (UKM) are organizations established to channel students' interests, talents, and hobbies outside of academic activities. UKM at USU covers various different fields, such as sports, arts, culture, leadership, and social activities.

What are the benefits of joining UKM USU?

Joining UKM provides many benefits, including the opportunity to develop interests and talents, expand social networks, build leadership skills, and gain experience that can be useful in the workforce. In addition, UKM also contributes to creating a balance between students' academic and non-academic lives.

Are there any fees to join UKM USU?

Generally, there is no registration fee to join UKM at USU. However, some UKM may require membership fees to support operational activities or participation in certain events.

How to join UKM USU?

To join UKM at USU, students can directly contact the UKM management of interest or participate in UKM promotion activities usually held at the beginning of the academic year. Information on registration and UKM activities can also be accessed through the official USU website or related UKM social media.


 

Healthy Campus

What is Healthy Campus?

Healthy Campus is a USU initiative aimed at creating a campus environment that supports the physical and mental health of all academic communities, providing various resources and engaging activities that promote a healthy lifestyle.

Why is it important to have a Healthy Campus?

Healthy Campus is important to ensure that students and staff have access to resources that support health and well-being, so they can reach their full potential in education and research.

What activities are offered in a Healthy Campus?

The program offered includes sports activities, health seminars, regular health checks, and education on healthy lifestyles.

Who can participate in a Healthy Campus?

Healthy Campus is open to all students, lecturers, and educational staff at USU.

What can I do to support the Healthy Campus program?

You can support the Healthy Campus program by participating in activities organized by USU, spreading information about the importance of health in the campus environment, and adopting a healthy lifestyle in your daily life.

How is University Level Leave Granted?

Service Requests come to apply for University-level Leave by bringing the specified requirements.
Requirements: Leave Application Letter signed by the relevant leader.
The following is Information regarding University-Level Leave.

a. Annual leave: work for at least 1 year continuously and the length of leave is 12 days, cannot be divided into less than 3 days, annual leave can be suspended by the authorized official for a maximum of 1 year.

b. Long leave: Civil servants who have worked for at least 6 years are entitled to long leave for 3 months, and are entitled to receive full income, except allowances.

c. Sick leave: Civil servants suffering from illness can be given a maximum of 1 year, miscarriages are given 1.5 months of leave, sick leave gets full pay.

d. Maternity leave for the first, second and third births is entitled to maternity leave, 3 (three) months long, receiving full income.

e. Leave for important reasons: entitled to leave for a maximum of 1 (one) month, can be given for reasons of death in the family, taking care of inheritance rights, carrying out a first marriage.

f. Leave outside the state's responsibility: Civil servants who work for 5 years, are given the right to 3 months' leave, which can be extended for 1 year if there are important matters. Civil servants who take leave outside the state's responsibility are exempt from their position, except for their 4th maternity.

Completion period: 3 (three) Working Days.

How to Get Student Transfer from Another State University to USU?

Please, service applicants must submit a request for Student Transfer from another State University to USU by bringing the following requirements:

a. following the transfer of duties of parents/husband/wife who have the status of Civil Servants who carry out government duties or the student concerned has the status of a Civil Servant who carries out government duties transferred to North Sumatra.
b. following the transfer of duties of parents/husband/wife who have the status of Civil Servants who carry out government duties or the student concerned has the status of a Civil Servant who carries out government duties transferred to North Sumatra.
c. The location of the state university of origin and the place of duty of the parents/husband/wife must be the same.
d. The deadline for submitting an application with a Decree on the transfer of duties of the parents/husband/wife concerned is a maximum of 12 months after the date of the Decree on the transfer of duties.
e. The transfer student is not in a state of being subject to sanctions or dropping out of studies as stated in a letter from the Rector of the State University of origin.
f. Transfer students must have participated in academic activities at the original university, have studied for 2 (two) semesters and passed at least 25 credits, have studied for 3 semesters and passed at least 40 credits, have studied for 4 semesters and passed at least 55 credits, have studied for 5 semesters and passed at least 70 credits, have studied for 6 semesters and passed at least 85 credits, have studied for 7 semesters and passed at least 100 credits, have studied for 8 semesters and passed at least 115 credits.
g. The study load that must still be taken by transfer students is at least 30 credits.
h. The original Study Program has an accreditation rating of BAN-PT/ LAM PTKes at least the same as the accreditation rating of the study program at USU.

1. The Service Applicant comes to apply for Student Transfer from Another State University to USU by bringing the specified requirements.
2. ULT staff will verify the service files .
3. ULT staff will provide a Rector's Decree on Student Transfer from Another State University to USU.
4. The Service Applicant receives the output.
Completion Period: 20 (twenty) working days.

b. Students who do not fulfill and carry out academic obligations according to regulations must be fulfilled (two semesters).
c. Students who do not receive written permission from the Faculty Leader and University Leader, then the student is automatically declared to have dropped out of their studies or resigned.
d. Students who are found guilty by the district court and have been sentenced to a criminal sentence, then the student is automatically declared to have lost their rights as a university student and is declared a student to have dropped out of their studies
. e. Regular and independent regular program students (S-1) in the evaluation:
1) Final semester 2 evaluation of

2) Final evaluation of semester 4

Lecturer Profile

How do I search for a lecturer on USU’s Lecturer Directory?

You can search for lecturers easily using the search feature in the USU lecturer directory. Simply enter the name of the lecturer, faculty, or study program you want to search for. The system will display search results that match your needs.

What kind of information will I gain from the lecturer’s profile page?

Each lecturer profile in this directory includes comprehensive information, such as areas of expertise, scientific publications, research experience, and contacts available for academic collaboration or consultation.

Can I search for lecturers based on a specific study area (specialties/concentration/expertise)?

Yes, you can search for lecturers based on specific scientific fields or disciplines. USU's lecturer directory allows you to search for lecturers who are experts in various disciplines, from technology to health, social sciences, and humanities.

Who can use USU’s Lecturer Directory?

This directory is designed for students, researchers, lecturers, and external entities, such as industries or institutions, that wish to collaborate with USU. Users can easily find suitable lecturers for academic purposes or research collaboration.

How can I contact a lecturer I found in the USU’s Lecturer Directory?

Each lecturer's profile comes with contact information that allows you to contact the lecturer directly, either through email or other contacts available on their profile.

Does USU’s Lecturer Directory only include lecturers from certain faculty?

No, the USU lecturer directory includes lecturers from 17 faculties and more than 99 study programs with various disciplines. You can find lecturers from various academic backgrounds that fit your needs.

Does the information in USU’s Lecturer Directory is regularly updated?

Yes, the USU lecturer directory is regularly updated to ensure the information you access is always accurate and relevant, including recent publications or lecturer achievements.

Can I find the lecturer’s publication and research in the directory?

Absolutely. Each lecturer's profile lists their publications and research so you can learn about current research topics and scientific contributions.

Talenta Publisher

What is Talenta Publisher?

Talenta Publisher is a scientific publisher from the Universitas Sumatera Utara that aims to publish research and scientific papers, including journals, books, and papers, to support the development of science.

Who can publish in Talenta Publisher?

Talenta Publisher is open to scholars, researchers, and students from both within and outside the Universitas Sumatera Utara who have quality scientific papers and according to predetermined standards.

How do I submit a manuscript for publication?

Manuscript submission can be done by creating an account on the Talenta Publisher website or logging into an existing account, then following the manuscript submission guidelines available on the publication page according to the field relevant to your research or scientific paper.

Does Talenta Publisher support open access?

Yes, Talenta Publisher supports open access to facilitate the spread of knowledge, so that the public can access scientific journals for free.
