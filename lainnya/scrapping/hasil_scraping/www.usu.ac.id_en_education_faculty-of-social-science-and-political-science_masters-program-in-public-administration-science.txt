Master's Program In Public Administration Science
Master Program
Public Administration Science
Universitas Sumatera Utara

As an advanced level of education from the Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU) Undergraduate Program in Public Administration Science, the Master's Program in Public Administration Science is an educational unit that studies the process of implementing or implementing administrative regulations issued by the legislative, executive, and judicial bodies.

Our study program is staffed with the best individuals with integrity and high dedication to teaching. Assisted by optimal facilities and infrastructure, the Master's Program in Public Administration Science is highly committed to creating an effective and efficient learning atmosphere. Our graduates have broad career prospects in public administration, such as political members, activists of non-governmental organizations, researchers, and teaching staff.

Mission of Master's Program in Public Administration Science

Competitiveness and Integrity

Organize competitive and competitive higher education with integrity in Administration, Policy Science, and good governance.

Research

Carry out strategic research in Administration and Public Policy and collaborative local governance for the development of science and the nation's progress.

Community Service

Perform community service to realize a highly competitive society and local government in the fields of Administrative Science and Public Policy, and responsive governance.

Concentration

Concentration is a field of specialization that can be chosen by students to deepen specific knowledge and skills in the study program they take. By choosing a concentration that suits their interests and talents, students can develop more in-depth competencies that are relevant to the needs of the job market. Concentration also helps students to explore specific fields that are of interest and passion to them, thus expanding their horizons and enriching their learning experience.
