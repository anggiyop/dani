USU Extra Kilap+
Glass Cleaner Liquid
Product Description

Extra Kilap+ is an eco-friendly glass cleaner with a refreshing orange scent. Cleaning the glass with this glass cleaner will make it clean and shiny. The special thing about this glass cleaner is its ability to clean pathogenic bacteria on the glass surface.
